Three Categories of Vehicle Parameters

  • Contributiion to finishing the events (passing the technical inspection and reliability)
  • Vehicle performance (tire coefficient of friction (μ), mass, power, lift, and drag)
  • Those that allow your driver to extract all the potential performance of your vehicle (controls & ergonomics, balance, and control & stability).
