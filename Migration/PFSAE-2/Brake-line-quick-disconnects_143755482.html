Staubli SPH Clean-Break High-Pressure Fittings

Stäubli has been a leading quick release coupling specialist for over 40 years. These compact, light-weight dry disconnects are designed specifically for high-pressure motorsport applications. The "no-bleed" connection does not leak when disconnected and does not introduce air upon re-connection. The SPH clean break fittings have a maximum operating pressure as high as 200 bar (2900psi). 

Choose EPDM (ethylene propylene) seals for brake and clutch hydraulic lines.  
Use FPM (fluorocarbon) seals with petroleum fuels or mineral-based oils such as ATF.
