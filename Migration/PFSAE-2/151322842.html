DEADLINE FOR SENDING OUT: AUGUST 9

2021 season wrap

Dear (SPONSOR),

About a year ago we here at Purdue FSAE were left waiting to take our vehicle to competition, unsure of when that would actually be. Today we are proud to say that we were finally able to put the most competitive vehicle we have ever produced to the test and secure the best results in the history of our program with a 6th place finish at SAE Michigan, a 9th place finish at SAE Nevada and a 2nd place finish in the Overall Knowledge Event. 

With this news, we would also like to say that we could never have made it this far without your support. Thank you for your confidence and your backing this season, we hope that you are as happy with our partnership as we are. Because of your support we were able to create and operate an Engine Dyno, utilize hollow-core wings for an improved lightweight aerodynamics package, and develop a Lap-Sim to influence engineering decisions for our competition vehicle. These improvements in our team have helped us educate and train our team members with valuable skills they will be able to use in the industry and were made possible by your generosity.

Looking forward, we will be constructing a bleeding-edge vehicle for the 2021-2022 season, and hope that you will be able to support us and our passion again when the time comes. 

Once again, on behalf of the entirety of Purdue Formula SAE, thank you.
