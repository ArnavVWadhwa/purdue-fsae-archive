Purdue Formula SAE : Pretesting Checklists

Created by Unknown User (jpeerbol) on Feb 28, 2020

Electronics:

  • Batteries Charged
  • Big Power works
  • Check Sensors
    • TPS, check 0-100 range
    • Manifold Pressure, should be close to 101 kPA within 5 kPA
    • Manifold Temperature, should be room temperature 20-30C
    • Coolant temp
    • Oil temp
    • Oil pressure
    • Fuel pressure
  • Disconnect Fuel injectors
  • Disconnect Spark plugs
  • Crank Engine
    • Fuel Pressure
    • Oil Pressure climb
    • Cycle Lock
