Shift in Culture That I Think Should Take Place

We've done a great job getting new members involved thus far, and that is great, but at the end of the day, we still need to actually build a car. We should not delegate any more work unless that person has absolutely 100% proven that they themselves are very competent at manufacturing and you KNOW they will get it done. We learned this in design with dates slipping because we had incompetent people on the projects and they didn't know what was involved, they didn't know what was going on. Of course, when you are doing something, invite people to watch / help out with you, but at the end of the day we are the people who signed up to dedicate copious amounts of time to this club, and we are the people who signed up and said that we would do the truly difficult tasks on this team, so we have to and should be the ones dealing with it.
