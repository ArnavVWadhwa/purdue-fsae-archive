Notes about using the team email:

  1. If you're responding to an email, just hit reply all so that everyone on the email list can see that things have been responded to
  2. Watch out for phising scams, these typically take a different form than the typical 'cool cash' emails. They often include 'security alerts' and other things of that nature. When in doubt, don't click it. I have included some screenshots of examples of typical scam emails.
    1.  
  3. Because all of our social accounts should be linked to our team email (so the team always has access to them even if people graduate and have been long forgotten) we will occasionally receive notifications about logins from new devices and such. DO NOT FREAK OUT. It's probably fine, but if you are concerned that an account has been compromised get in contact with other people (specifically the person or persons that are in-charge of that specific account) and confirm that they are in fact the reason for the notification.
  4. You should set up a separate folder and outlook rules to keep your inbox cleaner, this also can prevent confusion and mistaking a team email as something directly addressed to you
    1. https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/office/video-how-to-set-up-rules-in-outlook-75ab719a-2ce8-49a7-a214-6d62b67cbd41
