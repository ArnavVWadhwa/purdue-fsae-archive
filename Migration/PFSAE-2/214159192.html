Random Important Stuff

figure out rule T.9.2.2 ALL low voltage batteries must have overcurrent protection that trips at or below the maximum specified discharge current of the cells. (I'm guessing this includes purchased batteries, like ours)

I think that antigravity must have this designed into the battery, but I could not quickly find documentation to prove this. It would be worth calling them and get this in detail that you could prove to a tech judge (consult your chief for advice on how to prove this), if you cannot prove this, you might need to add a fuse to battery, you could maybe make a piggy back harness between the battery and car power anderson connector in a pinch, you would still need to max current spec of the cells in the battery tho.
