Purdue Formula SAE : Grissom Reservation

Created by Rumsey, Helen Nicole on Jan 09, 2024

Email Roger rejohnson@bright.net. He'll let you know what dates are already committed and what dates are free. Don't email him before January 1st when asking about spring date. We're not allowed to use Grissom during the week. I wouldn't recommend asking for it cause then you might receive the spiel below.


"No – we allow the corporate folks the privacy of use for their testing.  They regard the place as a competitive advantage and even knowledge of their use is confidential.

No teams have been allowed weekday use for as long as I’ve been involved.


Let’s chat perhaps about ‘background’.  Not that many years ago, IUPUI and Purdue’s FSAE teams were not allowed to use the site because of a long series of abuses of the rules that govern use:

Key is keeping the gate staffed or locked.

Nobody on site before sunrise or after sunset.

Signed waivers EVERY person, EVERY visit (and I need to see copies of same)

Arrange your own porta-johns and take trash with you and more – please read the attached in detail.


This pavement adjoins a sensitive military area and the co-operation of the Air Police is also an important part of what we need to maintain general use

After the banishment, a Professor intervened and with promises to ‘never happen again’, use was once again *donated*.

This is a privilege not allowed anyone else.  I manage the paperwork and the calendar on a volunteer basis, and being TOTALLY blunt, find the need to re-educate new teams / officers every two years challenging.

Please accept what we are able to do as a a gift. And perhaps note in the sample contract above the $$ value of what you’ve been given.

And the terms in the contract and other information attached are applicable to your use.


I’m sorry it can’t be more – but proud to support your efforts to the extent that we can."


Basically, don't mess up our relationship with Grissom or Roger.
