Congratulations to the following people for completion of the onboarding project!

Send Dom your completed CAD over teams in the .zip file!!

Not all of these are perfect. The main issues among most passing submissions were: 

  1. Did not submit all the files, needed to resend
  2. Sheet Metal issues
  3. Filler Neck Location not well packaged
  4. mounting
  5. path tree
  6. naming convention


Non-listed possible reasons of not passing ( was very lenient, but missing these just shows you spent less than 3 hours on the assignment)

  1. no filler neck
  2. sending in incomplete files expecting to pass
  3. no attempted lines
  4. little to no effort


Improvements for next year (dom's perspective)

  1. More in depth cad training before onboarding
    1. Top down modeling
    2. assembly 
    3. importing parts from online (.step. iges, etc.)
  2. I need to do a better job at explaining project 
  3. Importance of the rules needs to be a priority
