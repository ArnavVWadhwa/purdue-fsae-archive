Changes/Justifications

Description of the Change

Picture

Justification

Reprofiled to satisfy new ground clearance target

Front wing tips raised to 2.2'' from 1.8''. To recover downforce lost due to this, tip chord is extended to utilize the entire rule box. Can't really reduce angle of attack of the upper flaps anymore due to pressure side separation, main element was extended. This should be the most effective in terms of regaining downforce anyways, so not mad about that.

Outwash angle is reduced to further increase surface area of the wing. Reduced outwash angle is one thing I wanted to do anyways to have outwash affect more of the flow, so this feature is desirable.

Concern is with increased ride height, tire squirt will be harder to control, and hurting undertray. We will see how strong this effect is.

I don't want to lower center ride height if I don't need to for undertray performance.
