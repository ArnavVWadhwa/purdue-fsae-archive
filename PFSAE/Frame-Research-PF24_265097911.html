Engine Mounts

IdeasCommentary

Interesting concept of aluminum engine mount brackets. 

Research done by Kashibai Navale college of Engineering, 
Pune, India.

Using aluminum might allow for weight reduction, with theadded cost of analysis for bolted connection to SHMB, structural integrity analysis, etc.

Steel tube mounts that allows for a lot of adjustability in terms of engine position and mounting.

Tube placement and weldability kind of breaks down at tight angles

Loading through engine not fully understood. Engine might be loaded more in this case than, say, a tab connected to a lateral tube.

Angle of mount also limited by bolt accessibility and overall serviceability in terms of mounting and dismounting engine.
