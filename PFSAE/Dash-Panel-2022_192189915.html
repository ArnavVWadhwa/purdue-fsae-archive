Purdue Formula SAE : Dash Panel 2022

Created by Unknown User (dnocon), last modified by Rumsey, Helen Nicole on Oct 18, 2021

Location 2022

All components need to be within a line from the top of the main roll hoop to the top of the front roll hoop. All components the driver interacts with needs to be below the front roll hoop (this includes everything but the color dash which was kept higher for driver visibility). 

X: -21.5

Y: 0

Z: 26.2 

Angle X: 80

Angle Y: 0

Angle Z: 90

All dimensions from the OG car coordinate system. 

Layout 2022 (should remain mostly constant year to year)

Refer to Chassis BOM for links to electronics & reference CAD for models of parts


Controls (Right Side of Color Dash)

  • Estop
    • Safety Sticker
  • Starter Switch
  • Launch Control Pot 
  • Traction Control Pot


Controls (Left Side of Color Dash)

Things on the left side should be things the driver does not need to change often due to being occupied with clutch shifter. This is most necessary in acceleration when you are rolling up and holding the clutch. The driver may also need to 1. start / stop the car and 2. adjust launch control easily

  • Deuesch Panelmount data pull thingy
  • Brake Bias


Controls (Steering)

  • Change Page
  • Mark Data


Approximate Driver View aka Driver Eyeballs

Main concern is visibility of the color dash.
