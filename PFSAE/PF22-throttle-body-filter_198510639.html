Purdue Formula SAE : PF22 throttle body filter

Created by Unknown User (jpeerbol), last modified on Nov 30, 2021

We would like a filter for the PF22 throttle body. 


Our current design restraints require the filter to be fairly low profile. 


The current idea is something like this for the air filter. This inspiration is for a individual throttle body (ITB) filter.

This would be half of a tea filter with a 3d printed adapter to mount it to the throttle body. You could possibly include a trumpet design to try and funnel air into the throttle body.


Here is the throttle body cad so you get get rolling with some designs.

PF22_POW_5200_EcoTronThrottleBody.prt


We have ordered some tea filters to try, we won't have them until the end of the week but you could get started roughing out designs based on measurements on the product page.
