Simulation: CZ-038

Changes/Justifications

Description of the Change

Picture

Justification

More strake changes

Took the 14-06 strakes and moved them outboard for undertray performance. Ditching the long strakes because the additional vorticity really hurt the undertray.

No change to cascades despite their shortcomings since I think the performance gain from further optimization is low and is not something we should worry about at this stage.




CFD Results Analysis

CFD Picture

Description

This is really junction induced separation, not slot gap alignment. Moving these outboard made this setup not able to survive through this region. But moving it outboard did produce the desired effect of moving wake outboard, it's just the separation messing things.
