Commentary: When Comparing our steering column to those of others teams, the difference that may be impacting how much the columns bends is the location of the support/alignment structures. It seems that the best method if to have a control point after the wheel and before the first U-Joint and one after the last U-joint. This should minimize the rotational moment that is translated into lateral moment. Another aspect we should aim for is to reduce the length of the long tube that connects to the steering rack. This is the column that historically moves the most and introduces the most play
