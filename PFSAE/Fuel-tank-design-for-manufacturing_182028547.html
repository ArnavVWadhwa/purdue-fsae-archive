Purdue Formula SAE : Fuel tank design for manufacturing

Created by Unknown User (reyno105), last modified on Jun 04, 2021

Following the discovery that PF20's original fuel tank was too small (documented here: FIXME), a new fuel tank was designed including design for manufacturing input from Wesley Reynolds (designed several years of fuel tanks for University of Oklahoma and has aluminum welding experience) and Mike Sherwood (ME dept. pro fabricator). Overlap was added so that welds could be inside 90s rather than external edge to edge. Originally 1/4" of overlap was added, but Mike Sherwood recommended to instead use overlap that is 1-1.5x the thickness of the sheet. This allows the overlap to be used as the fill for the most part. Also, Sherwood put the fuel cap fitting on the lathe to bore it out / chamfer it (not exactly sure what he did) so that it fits and centers on the neck.

One other change I made was adding feet to the fuel pump holder, which made it an easier weld than previous, head-ass designs that saved 3 grams.

The new fuel pump mount was designed separately here: Z:\2020\9_Technical\1_CAD\PF20_POW_4109_NEW_PUMP_MOUNT.prt
