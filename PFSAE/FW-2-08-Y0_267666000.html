Purdue Formula SAE : FW-2-08 Y0

Created by Matthew James Lamear on Aug 17, 2023

Simulation ML-050

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Removed endplate cutout
After reevaluating, I dont think the cutout is what made the vortex go into the first channel. This was likely the result of additional inwash generate by the new foot plate design. Thus, I have kept the foot plate design while removing the cutout. 
Reduced curvature near inner portion and increased curvature more outboard

Should result in more outwash more outboard, resulting in less wake the UT sees.
Added diveplane to end of inner section of endpalte.

This diveplane should improve the counter rotating vortex approach by increasing the endplate vortex strength while also generating an additional downashing-outwashing vortex.
