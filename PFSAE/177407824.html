Work started in Spring 2021.

Goals:

  • Able to switch between single and 4 cylinder harness' in about 15 minutes
  • Runs WRF450 Single cylinder engine
  • Build and Tune work


Sensors added to Engine:

Oil pressure

hall effect for cam shaft

different rectifier


Sensors on Engine:

  • gear position
  • crank position(not accurate enough at low speeds)
  • Engine/coolant temp
  • Starter
  • Stator


Has manual water pump
