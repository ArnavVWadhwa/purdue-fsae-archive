Description of the ChangePictureJustification

Outer airfoil reprofiled.

Outer airfoil AoA decrease 1.5 deg, slot gap increased .1 in Z dir.Â 

Increasing slot gap allows for more high energy flow to enter, resulting in potentially better separation characteristics.

Reducing AoA improves separation characteristics on the TE.

Airfoil reprofile shifts suction peak forward more, this results in a larger area of pressure recovery, which should recover more gradually with more SA. Ultimately, improving separation characteristics.
