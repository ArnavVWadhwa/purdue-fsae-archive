Purdue Formula SAE : Boots (Inboard and Outboard)

Created by Unknown User (dewey2), last modified by Panchal, Aarnav Nilesh on May 24, 2022

Boots are used to protect the CV rollers and CV housing from outside debris while also ensuring CV lubricant does not fly everywhere. In 2022 we started to use Gaff Tape to secure the outboard boots.
