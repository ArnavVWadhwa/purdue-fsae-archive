Jigsaw puzzle

Intent:

  • Investigate feasibility of achieving 49% front WD by changing engine location while keeping all other components the same (frame not included)

Relevant rules

  • Intake boundary rules
  • Tube bends rules


Stakeholders:

  • Powertrain:
    • Component placement 
    • Intake:
      • Component boundary defined by MRH placement
    • Oil pump
    • Exhaust manifold
  • Drivetrain:
    • Chain angle 
    • Diff carrier
    • Diff placement
  • Suspension
    • Clearance with rear dampers
  • Aerodynamics:
    • UT Clearance

Description of feature/changePictureJustification

Changed engine angle

Moved engine back 3" compared to PF23

Moved engine down 0.8" compared to PF23


  • Gains:
    • Moving engine back this much gets us to a front WD of 50%
    • Moving the engine down this much lowers the entire vehicle cg down by 0.16"
  • Tradeoff:
    • With engine x at 19.1 and MRH x at 7, exhaust-firewall clearance is still not improved
    • Moved MRH back to ensure intake remains within component boundary
    • Might consider changing lower rear diagonal tube to 
    • Had to change engine angle to 5 degrees so oil pump can fit
    • Also had to change MRH angle to 5 degrees
