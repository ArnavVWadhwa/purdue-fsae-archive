Justification

Stagnation point optimization

More front wing outwash

Increased the chord and reduced angle of attack on ME flap, lower flap, and outer flap. The increase in tip chord should increase outwash by quite a bit.

The increased chord should also bring more much needed downforce.

The physical z/x slope also reduced, which should be a lot nicer to the wingtip vortices.

CFD Results Analysis

CFD Picture

Description

More outwash

The outwash actually depowered diffuser vortex structures, leading to overall loss of downforce in undertray. Not a concern since diffuser needs to get a lot better first.

VC vortex is being kept alive for longer. This supports the idea that the lifespan of the VC vortex is closely coupled with how much the wing tip upwash.
