Purdue Formula SAE : Fuel Sizing Lessons Learned and PF20 Redo

Created by Unknown User (reyno105), last modified on Jun 04, 2021


"You don’t get an A because your fuel tank is 95% big enough."


It's critical to consider testing vs comp speeds, idle time, when fuel slosh occurs, extra volume from fill neck, etc. This spreadsheet was my attempt at that when it was found the original PF20 fuel tank was not sufficiently sized. See Yearly Fuel Updates - Purdue Formula SAE - Confluence for past sizing. This did not consider possible idling and, critically, that drivers were off pace. Completion of aero package for mass and drag as well as driving style have large effects.


Testing here (2021.05.19 - GP Track - Purdue Formula SAE - Confluence) showed that sizing was successful
