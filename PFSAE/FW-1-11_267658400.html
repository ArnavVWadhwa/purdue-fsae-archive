Purdue Formula SAE : FW-1-11

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Jul 26, 2023

Simulation ML-030

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Increased strake length and increased width between the floor gurney and the inner and outer strakes.

To aid in Y15 performance, the strake length has been increased to begin the outer vortex better. Increasing the width (slightly) will allow the vortices to develop better with more lateral expansion.
Cut some of the inner strake.Â 

To attempt to replicate the previous iteration's strake length while not flow separating on the third element.
