Minimum Viable Product Chassis

  • General:
    • PASS RULES! 
    • Pass SES (in general make all "extra" tubes minimum structural size and reduce ONLY if it will not break rules or structures requirements) 
    • Pass Tech 
    • Be held to acceptable tolerances for suspension mounting locations 
  • Mounting:
    • All required suspension mounting points (must be at a node if possible) (should be analyzed to prove safe) 
    • Additional mounting should be discussed with other sub team leads 
  • Cockpit:
    • Belts must meet specs defined in rules 
    • Steering assembly should have minimal play 
    • Steering assembly should have reasonable required input force 
    • Pedal box should be adjustable in range from 95% male to 5% female 
    • Headrest must meet all required rules and be in a comfortable location (ensure correct foam is used) 
    • Seat should support driver without contacting belts 
    • Firewall should seal with floorpan and body panel 
  • Packaging:
    • Ensure chassis is large enough for templates and Percy (with harness!) 
    • Allow room for easy engine removal and servicing 

Required reading for a Chassis Team Lead

  • All documentation for the previous year's design and manufacturing:  
    • Frame PF23 | Design
    • PF22 Frame Design
    • PF22 Frame Welding
    • Fixtures For Welding 101
    • make sure to pay attention to Considerations / Lessons learned / Ideas for next year for each design component (ie frame, frame jigging, etc.) 
  • Your current rules - found at https://www.fsaeonline.com/cdsweb/gen/DocumentResources.aspx
