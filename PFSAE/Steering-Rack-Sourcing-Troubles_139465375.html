Purdue Formula SAE : Steering Rack Sourcing Troubles

Created by Unknown User (reyno105), last modified by Unknown User (xia107) on Jan 16, 2022

If the rack being used is the Narrco, it would probably make sense to make an upright with holes further out for the Kaz rack. We hav had trouble getting hold of a narrco rack, but it is likely KAZ is reliable. Because Kaz uses ~1 inch longer steer arm, additional length and hole could be placed in tierod tab on upright with no effect on structural capability with narrco. This nearly negligible mass could save needing to make new uprights in case of switching.

NOTE: Worth reaching out to steering rack manufacturer for clearer failure criteria as brought up in 2021 Cockpit/Controls/Safety/Brakes Design.
