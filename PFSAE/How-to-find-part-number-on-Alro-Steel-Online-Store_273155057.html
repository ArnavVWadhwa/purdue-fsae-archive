Purdue Formula SAE : How to find part number on Alro Steel Online Store

Created by Dien Quang Nguyen on Sep 25, 2023

RE: Dien Quang Nguyen 

This is how to find the exact part number for your desired stock material on Alro Steel Online Store.

If you don't know whether Alro has a certain material, go to this website → https://www.alro.com (look around to see if they have your desired material)

Once you already have a good idea of the stock spec and dimension, go to this website -> https://www.myalro.com/


It should look like this. If it doesn't, you got the wrong website.


Choose your product line (Metal example below)


Click on the blue circle that matches your material category


You should see all the options from Alro. Again, click on the blue circle to select your material grade


Select all the relevant dimensions and click Next


Boom, you now have the part number and description for your stock material


Any stock material desired from Alro should be reported as: Quantity - part number - description (in full).

Example:  3 PC of 00301500 1 SQ CF C1018
