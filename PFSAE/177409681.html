DO NOT USE THE BRAILLE BATTERY ON THE DYNO NO MATTER WHAT

  • it will kill the braille battery and make it unusable on the car
    • discharging li-on cells below a certain threshold will severely increase the internal resistance of the batter making them into a paperweight
      • if this happens the batteries can blow up too
  • (the braille battery is "dumb" and no BMS or any sort of protection built in)


We are in the negative on current draw on the dyno because we are sometimes not at high rpms for better stator outputs. 

The stepper motor also draws an average of 10 amps at all times.

 Unknown User (jkrawitz) or Williams, Carson Kenneth pls confirm its 10amps ^


Solution:

12V 22ah SLA battery on dyno, while also charging the battery externally at the same time. 

Part: UB12220 Battery 12V 22Ah Upgrade for UB12180 12V 18Ah

link: https://www.impactbattery.com/ub12220-battery-12v-22ah-upgrade-for-ub12180-12v-18ah.html

you can charge it at 6.6 amps max, not sure how fast you can charge it while running the dyno since amp draw is kind of varies

  • hopefully we stay in the positive with 6amps plus stator
