Brake Dyno DCR Feedback:

  • Rotary torque should only be used if it is sponsored otherwise load cell is valid
  • What is the rotary encoder for? If rotors are similar to car just use wheel speed sensor
  • Why would the rotor go to zero? Confused as to why that would happen based on explanation of force on master cylinder
  • Purple pads are for Aluminum! The provided plot shows they are not ideal for what we want
  • Spend time going through caliper specs: I am most interested in Piston sizes
  • I have a contact at essex from design comp
  • What is the AP Racing CP4226 Compound we currently run?
  • Doing tradeoff analysis it would be good to have spreadsheet showing all options together to really see size and weight differences
  • Its already a bitch to package current caliper, packaging constraints will need to be taken into account. If smaller radius rotor is needed to package, pads need to have high performance or pedal box will need to compensate
  • Worth testing multiple materials; need to compare thermal conductivity and specific heats
  • What does fantastic thermal properties mean? When doing tradeoffs it is necessary to show values and rankings based on facts/data
  • Why was Aluminum not considered? Better thermal properties than steel, just less strength especially at temp so would need to have more volumeÂ 
  • I was drilled on heat transfer of brakes in Design. Cross drilled holes really arent doing much because there isnt much air flow through the wheel. Most is through conduction. Worth studying decrease in friction with more and more cross drilled holes.
  • Compare same weight rotor but one with cross drilled and one with slots, good idea for mixture of both
  • How would you quantify brake dust effects
  • Define what is the ideal/perfect brake pad and brake rotor (what would that look like and be)
  • Testing plan should be finalized earlier than 9/17. Need time to manufacture the various rotors for testing.
  • How do we ensure the brake temperature will get to a large enough range
