Purdue Formula SAE : Adding Teams Files (Sharepoint) to Your Personal OneDrive

Created by Unknown User (jpeerbol), last modified by Paradise, Hunter on Jun 01, 2023

OneDrive is super cool and integrates with your Purdue Office 365 stuff (you'll probably use this stuff at a real job and it makes life easier so practice using it now)

  1. Navigate to the files tab on the teams channel
  2. Select Open in SharePoint
  3. It will open a link in your web browser
  4. Select add folder to OneDrive
  5. You will now have a folder locally on your OneDrive that will sync
