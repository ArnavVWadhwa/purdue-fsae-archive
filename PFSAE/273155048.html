BOM: Setup Pad BOM


Reasons why it failed:

  • Heavy af (>15 pounds for left design, >30 pounds for right design)
  • Grissom and Grand Prix track are fairly flat
    • We can just take a giant level and find a flat piece of ground to test on (there are fat pieces of concrete so shouldn't move much)
  • Expensive relative to its worth (both in time and money)
    • Refer to bom linked above
  • Inefficient and timely to set up (>10 minute which was our design requirement)
  • Only time the slope has a huge effect is at comp, where we don't need it


Overall, the setup pad is not worth it given the reasons above + more. Maybe in the future, someone will revive this project, or maybe not...
