Initial Design Criteria (IDR): July 27th, 2022

  • understanding of design criteria & appropriate rules + rule tracker
  • development of any mathematical model/simulations that will be used to design
  • research and selection of a design direction

Component Design (PDR): September 24th, 2022

  • deadline for complete (but not optimized) design in CAD
  • list exact values being used for design criteria (load cases, ect)
  • list of exact analysis to be performed on each part
  • manufacturing process for each part


Validation & Structures Analysis 

  • all validation and structures analysis is completed and checked before the CDR

CDR: October 12th, 2022

  • serves as written documentation of the design
  • evaluate places where design criteria were or weren't met
  • manufacturing plan
