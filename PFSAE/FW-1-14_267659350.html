Purdue Formula SAE : FW-1-14

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Jul 29, 2023

Simulation ML-033

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Added third strake

The third strake aims to spool up a vortex that is released earlier to attempt to prevent vortex bursting.

The two long strakes are very good for Y0 performance, so I want to maintain the same outer orientation while altering the inner portion. 

Definitely worried about FW DF loss due to the additional strake.

Altered strake upper surface

Should help in providing additional outwash by spooling up another vortex on the top of the strake. Really just testing out this geometry because I saw it in F1.

CFD Analysis

CFD PictureDescription

No vortex bursting, complexity definitely increased though. DF increased since the additional vortex is acting on the surface and it allows other vortices to stay alive longer resulting in the other vortices acting for longer.

Note that the RW is wack this iteration, gonna try to find a way to improve that, I feel like it has to do with the down washing flap having flow sep on the down wind side. 

Wake certainly improved here considerably, looks extremely similar to the wake produced in the iteration that got -3.9 Cl. 

Strake turns the flow too much here, so just need to reduce the turning to eliminate that inboard flow sep.
