DUE DATE - October 21, 2023

The Business Subteam onboarding was designed to show prospective Subteam members the ropes of contacting sponsors and keeping the records to maintain professional relationships and secure income for the Team's design and manufacturing interests.

READ THE ENTIRE PAGE BEFORE YOU START ON ONBOARDING

New members interested in the Business Subteam must send me:

A sponsor proposal/email template for the sponsors below:

    • Bosch 
      • Parts and equipment sponsor
      • Asking for Gold Partnership
      • Determine what Bosch would want most from our team
      • What makes our team stand out against other FSAE teams?
    • FedEx
      • Monetary Sponsor
      • Need to determine level of sponsorship, what is the value of the discount?
        • A discount is worth a different amount based on distance of transport, how do we present this to FedEx?

A document with the information below:

  •  Research and Targeting
      • Identify at least 3 companies which would be strong sponsorship candidates for the team.
      • Write a paragraph detailing how these companies benefit from working with us, and we what hope to receive from them. 
      • Draft an email reaching out to the potential sponsors

 


  • Write a small paragraph on what you are hoping to learn from this experience and if there is anything else you would like me to know.
    • This is an informal message. I want to know more about how I can help you learn and achieve your goals on this team. 


As a Business Sub-team member you'd be directly responsible for hunting down future income and bettering the condition of our team by contacting and maintaining sponsors. I plan to develop the team's position within the University as well with events and other initiatives which you would be helping plan and facilitate. You may also be involved in creating the Business Presentation for the knowledge event in the Spring as well as the marketing side of the team. As such, this onboarding is designed to identify your interests and how you can best help the team. I really appreciate your suggestions and feedback and am committed to making the Business Sub-team an experience which develops your marketing and networking skills. Reach out at any time with any questions. 


– Athreya Ramanan
