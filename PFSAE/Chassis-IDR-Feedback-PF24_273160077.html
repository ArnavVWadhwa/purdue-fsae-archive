Rack Mounting and Everything Else:

Aluminum on aluminum translational motion-

  • aluminum on aluminum not good

explore other materials

  • galling force

    • to find best combo of material
  • Seals
    • seal not fully circular
      • 3d print?
        • concerns: material, tolerance
        • make a couple dumby ones to test tolerances for best seal and allow movement of rack
        • TPU?
          • shrinks when printed

Rails:

Throttle:

  • Bend test on forged carbon pedal
    • Manufacturability?
    • 60ksi max in FEA
    • Seems like a lot of force? (Only for max load case)
    • Ask Baja about any testing they may have done
  • Rubber cap on pedal stop
    • Break over time b/c constant wear and tear
  • Double check all carbon fiber holes
    • Weird property- holes shed stress off to each other when near and most stress shed onto last hole
    • Not super concerning?
  • Springs- stainless steel on carbon sliding 
    • Bolt sheet metal onto back of pedal
    • 071 steel??
      • No. .04 aluminmum or smtg like that
  • Some aluminum bends better, look at steel
    • or machining

Pneumatics

  • Clutch Paddle (second regulator and proportional control valve electronics)
    • make a test bench/ on dyno early to physically test
      • to make sure if 25 psi is enough to control
      • biggest uncertainty is clutch force
    • maybe adding position sensor to clutch
      • to know clutch pos at all time
    • many other teams use hand clutch
    • use derivative of paddle for calc
  • clutch paddle logic
  • additional considerations or components of clutch paddle
  • will additional line /reservoir of air be worth it
    • test on dyno to see improvement
      • most likely going to see tenths of seconds so potentially not much improvement?
  • air compressor vs tank for filling
    • make sure think about the thermals
    • tank could go ka-boom...
    • PF23 Carbon fiber
      • worry about about corrosion inside tank in general
        • Arpit says we're chillin'
        • as long as not drenching it in water 24/7
          • even then not too big of an issue
    • model the tank and supports 
      • assume given mass, fill volume and max acceleration
      • model in ansys worse case in each axis
  • additional analysis ideas or suggestions
    • tank clearance on PF24 worse than PF23
      • talk to Aarnav 
        • if something in back were to fail what would fail first 
          • avoid putting tank there
      • probs safest for driver to be behind engine
    • make sure all valves are mounted securely
      • small components have issue of weird failure 
        • since so low mass, small bump can cause weird frequency causing it to shoot off
