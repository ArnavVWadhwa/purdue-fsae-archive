Purdue Formula SAE : 2020.09.26 - Ross Ade - Manual Shifting Times

Created by Unknown User (xia107) on Oct 03, 2020

Notable:

  • First time running vehicle with manual shifter as a replacement for electronic, paddle shifting
  • Manual shifting was used primarily form 2nd to 3rd gear in straight line acceleration. A course representative of AutoX and Endurance was set up. There was really only one opportunity on that course to get into 3rd gear.


Conclusion:

  • Shift times during "AutoX" run averaged 0.40 seconds, this is likely to decrease as drivers are more comfortable with controls
  • Shift times during acceleration run averaged 0.23 seconds.
