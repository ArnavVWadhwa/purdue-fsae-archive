Our excitation is 5V

So volts from it is really input*5

Need to figure out m value to send to izze ( f= m* voltage)

100 =  m*2.04544 * 5

M = 100/(2.04544 * 5 * 10^-3) = 9,777.84731  lbs/v

Send to izze = lbs/uv = m = 9777.84731/(10^-6) = 9.77785E9
