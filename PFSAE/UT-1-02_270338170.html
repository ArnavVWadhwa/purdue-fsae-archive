Purdue Formula SAE : UT-1-02

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Aug 30, 2023

Simulation ML-058

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Fused the inlet with the strake, and added gap between the skirt and the inlet strake.

This should be a better version of the previous two iterations, the additional high-energy flow injection from the inlet should aid in vortex x velocity. 

Ideally this flow injection generates a secondary vortex that splits the two vortices that develop at the outer skirt. This would depower the singular vortex and would allow for better inwash management and better DF production since the side tunnel would actually be working on this side.

CFD Analysis

CFD PictureDescription

This very substantial low energy flow generated due to the tire wake and the substantial curvature of the outer inlet skirt.

This low energy flow goes into the channel and causes the vortex to burst, so to combat this, I think the channel needs to have a more gradual increase in width.

This geometry seems to have improved the inner most channel, but the outer channel is still terrible.
