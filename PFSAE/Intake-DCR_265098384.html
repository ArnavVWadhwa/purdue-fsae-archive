Purdue Formula SAE : Intake DCR

Created by Horton, Tyler Jason, last modified on Jul 12, 2023

Project Scope

  • Problem Statement: Design and manufacture an intake that will produce the desired power, while making the car drivable for the driver (throttle response)
  • System Boundaries:
    • Aero: Intake interfering with rear-wing performance
    • Chassis: Packaging, throttle
    • General: Car feel for driver

Design Requirements

  • Plenum volume is designed for ideal throttle response
  • Runner length is designed for peak power at the desired rpm
  • Meet all rules requirements

Design Overview

  • 3D printed intake made from Nylon 12
    • Custom fuel rail mounting
    • Manual throttle body

Cost

  • Less than $1500 goal

Schedule

  • Follow team schedule and have everything ready one week before 1st run

Analysis

  • Plenum volume and runner length from GT
  • Diffuser angle to accommodate new restrictor for E85
