  • Please provide your Organization's Mission Statement or overarching purpose of your Organization at Purdue University
    • Members of the Purdue Formula SAE team create a new well-designed car each year by combining the theory learned in class with hands-on skills. Students utilize engineering skills and state of the art tools to manufacture and validate the new car. Our team is comprised primarily of mechanical engineering students, but we also welcome students from other majors. Purdue FSAE is first and foremost a team. Our team spends an enormous amount of time together throughout the school year which helps us work together effectively. We collaborate to find new ways to combine our wide array of engineering talents and experiences to build and design a unique race car. At the end of the school year in May, our members compete with our vehicle at the Michigan International Speedway against 100 international universities. Our goal is to excel in vehicle design and maneuverability on track with a top 10 finish in our competition.

  • Is this a new expenditure/project/event: If so, please explain what your specific goals are for this activity and how you intend to achieve them. If not, please describe how successful this expenditure/project/event has been in the past and how many years you have participated in this specific activity
    • This is a new expenditure. The team would like to purchase new dampers for our race car to serve as replacements for our current dampers. Unlike most of our vehicle, the current dampers are reused from year to year, but they have begun to leak, indicating that they should be replaced. The leak degrades the performance of our car in competition, as the dampers become less effective with the leak. The old dampers would be put on our second vehicle which is for test purposes only and not for competition.
  • How does this expenditure/event/project contribute to your mission statement or overarching purpose of your Organization?
    • 1: New dampers will allow for better handling of our car and will improve our performance in the competition events. With the improved performance, we will place higher in the events and in the overall standings to meet our goals.
    • 2: New dampers will allow the suspension geometry and the CAD models to actually correlate with the on-track performance of the Ohlin dampers. If we have to continue usage of our degrading set our mathematical models will not have the ability to account for the losses in performance and as a result, our vehicle's design and running performance will suffer. For us to accomplish our goal of designing a car with the best possible performance, we would need new dampeners to be modeled and running at their best.
  • Please specifically describe how the funding will be used for your expenditure/event/project: describe in detail the plan, date and timeline, and logistics.
    • The funds will be used directly to purchase Ohlins TTX25 MKII dampers during the Spring 2021 semester so that the dampers can be incorporated into the vehicle for the SAE Michigan competition in May 2021.
