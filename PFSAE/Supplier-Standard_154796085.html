This list is not complete and still being worked on

This list exists to ensure that quality products are used on our vehicles. It also can be used as a recommended list of places to purchase products from.

Approved Suppliers

Summit

Earl's Indy

Milspec wiring



Non-approved Suppliers

Amazon

  • can be used if in a bind, but counterfeit products are very common and should have a replacement on the way and be replaced as soon as possible, especially with things that get used on our car i.e. master switches, loctite, etc.
  • "But it's way cheaper on Amazon" only if you can prove with 100% certainty that it's a quality product, you would need to directly compare to a known good quality product
    • saving a few dollars is not worth the risk of ruining a year's work at competition, because a master switch was $10 cheaper than purchasing it from a place like summit
  • Amazon can be used to purchase things that would never be used on the car, i.e. tool boxes, etc.
    • still be careful about counterfeit and lower quality products
