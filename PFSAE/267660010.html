Purdue Formula SAE : Throttle Position | Sensors

Created by Paradise, Hunter, last modified on Jul 31, 2023

Notes:

  • Tells the ECU where the throttle blade is so that it can be determined whether the driver is attempting to idle, cruise at steady state, accelerate, or decelerate
  • Rotary potentiometer
  • 0-5V
    • 0.5-1.0V = Closed throttle
    • 4.5V+ = Wide open throttle (WOT)
  • Many systems consider WOT to be anything greater than 70% because the effective flow rate changes so little at higher percentages
