Changes/Justifications

Description of the Change

Picture

Justification

Reverted strake to FW-13-07

It better
Reverted outwashing winglet to FW-13-07 but with the FW-13-08 twist

FW-13-07 winglet worked better but FW-13-08 is less costly in terms of downforce, best of both worlds.
Simplified endplate geometry

Removed mass flow injector since I don't think it does too much. Removal of mass flow injector gave 1 in more to half span of the front wing, which should equate to more downforce and a wake that's placed more to the outside.

Simplified endplate to a straight endplate since there is too much inwash for the outwash to be useful, as we have demostrated by the strake. A solution that involve gurney flap does not involve flow being able to attach to the endplate, therefore should be more effective. This does leave the diveplane with higher adverse pressure gradient though, but I don't think the diveplane is too lossy at the moment and diveplane can always be moved forward.
