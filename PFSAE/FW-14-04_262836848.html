Purdue Formula SAE : FW-14-04

Created by Zhang, Chenkai, last modified on Jun 19, 2023

Simulation: CZ-029

Changes/Justifications

Description of the Change

Picture

Justification

Reprofiled outer strake and added additional inner strake

Reduced outwash angle of the outer strake for more outwash (due to less separation).

Added additional inner strake to further isolate wake and increase downforce as well as improve undertray performance. The inner strake has less outwash angle due to local flow field.

CFD Results Analysis

CFD Picture

Description

Inner strake flow alignment is not great

Giga junction losses

Due to the inboard nature of the strake, there is more wake going into the undertray. I think the key here is better control flow alignment across the whole strake, although that is inherently more inwashing.
