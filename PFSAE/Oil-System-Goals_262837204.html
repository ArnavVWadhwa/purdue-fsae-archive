Purdue Formula SAE : Oil System Goals

Created by Horton, Tyler Jason, last modified on Jun 20, 2023
  • Provide sufficient oil pressure to protect engine internals
  • Keep oil at a safe temperature to promote good oil viscosity and pressure
    • Use the stock oil cooler on the front of the engine
  • Reduce the amount of air introduced into the oil from the dry sump pump
    • Improve de-aeration in the dry sump tank
  • Reduce system mass. Reduce size if possible
  • Optimize oil changing procedure
  • Optimize system removal/servicing
