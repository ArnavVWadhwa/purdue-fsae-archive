Purdue Formula SAE : FW-1-02 Y15

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Jul 14, 2023

Simulation ML-021

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Changed the sweep into two sections.

Changing the sweep into two sections should result in superior downforce generation, since there is more area for the flow to act on. This also removes the problem caused by the reflexed outer section.

Potentially a gurney could be used on the outer section. Similarly, the gurney may be extended in the inner section.

CFD Analysis

CFD PictureDescription

Vortex blows up still just as much as in previous iterations. 

Due to the mid endplate there is an upwashing-inwashing vortex that I cannot tell if it is helping or hurting. Potentially if this is placed correctly it could be useful but with the inwashing component I sort of question it. Potentially placing this vortex nearer the squirt suppressor vortex we could see some counter-rotating vortex mixing which could result in superior UT performance due to less dirty flow. 

Ultimately, really doubt this is doing any good, likely is hurting UT. If placed higher the upwash may be a beneficial factor in reducing the amount of dirty flow near the UT, but also hurting the RW due to the additional upwash. 

Very likely this vortex is simply bad.
