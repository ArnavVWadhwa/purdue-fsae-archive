Purdue Formula SAE : Sticker Machine Maintenance

Created by Unknown User (jpeerbol) on Jun 27, 2022

When cutting more intricate shapes, small pieces of vinyl get inside of the blade and prevent it from cutting well.


Remove the blade, twist off the protective cap. Remove the small vinyl bits so the blade can spin freely.

remove the adhesive on the protective cap, iso alcohol works pretty well for this.
