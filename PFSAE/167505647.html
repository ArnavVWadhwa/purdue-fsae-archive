Wiring Harness Manufacturing Unknown User (mstarobr)

List out methods of harness manufacturing and pros/cons 

Ideally have final choice but if not say what you need to make that decision

Paper Layout/ 2D Method: 

  1. Pros:
    1. Can be measured and manufactured off site 

    2. Ease of storage

    3. Past experience with process

    4. Would help find major wire length issues

  2. Cons: 
    1. Funneling the main harness through the heat shield
    2. High potential of wires tangling during on-frame continuity testing 
    3. Difficult to find any minor wire length issues
    4. Time consuming, mainly transferring on and off the car
