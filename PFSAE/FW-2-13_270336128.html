Purdue Formula SAE : FW-2-13

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Aug 23, 2023

Simulation ML-055

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Elongated 2nd and 3rd strakes.

This should allow the vortex to stay alive and merge properly with the inner most vortex. This should improve UT performance considerably, but we will see.

The other thing that could be problematic is the fact that this concept relies on a vortex off the outer most strake which may not be present with this strake orientation.
