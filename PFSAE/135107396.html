Waterjetting

If BIDC and Waterjet Cutting of Indiana are not available and a small number of tabs are needed the ME machine shop also has a waterjet. THIS SHOULD BE USED SPARINGLY! 

What to Bring: 

  • DXF's of all desired parts generated in OMAX
  • Drawings of desired parts showing max dimensions and any critical dimensions (list material and quantity on the drawing)
  • Material (under 0.25" only for steel)

How to ask: 

  • Bring drawings to shop and ask if it would be possible to use to ME waterjet
  • Emphasize that other methods were tried first and fell through or are unavailible

If yes: 

  • Email the parts to Adam at: krichbau@purdue.edu
  • Adam will take the drawings and path and cut the parts
  • STAY IN THE SHOP WHILE THIS IS HAPPENING 
  • Be prepared to help clean up / mop while cutting
