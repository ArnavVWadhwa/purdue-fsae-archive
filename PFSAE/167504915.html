Purdue Formula SAE : 1/15/2021 Dyno Testing

Created by Williams, Carson Kenneth, last modified on Jan 16, 2021

Its cold... 37F

Adjusted idle screw. Set 0 on M1 at fully closed throttle. Idle is at 25% throttle. I don't think that's right.

The battery started dying of death. Problem 1-Stepper motor drawing 10A even not in use. Problem 2-Fans turn on at 65C and are way too big and kill the battery. Solution: Mega Box fan and unplug the car fans.

Fixed Idle! Put throttle to true zero. Set offset to 0. Move throttle to 10% true. Set Offset to 0. It will idle

Completed the entire VE table. Woot.

Looked at fuel film. It was confusing.

Some ignition testing. Not much changed even at really low timing.
