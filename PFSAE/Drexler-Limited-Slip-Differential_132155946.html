Purdue Formula SAE : Drexler Limited-Slip Differential

Created by Unknown User (dewey2), last modified by Unknown User (green338) on Apr 03, 2022

Notes for Disassembly:

Procedure:

  1. Undo two filler screws (M8 Bolts on main housing) and drain differential oil
  2. Remove tripod housings and inspect crush washers/M8 bolts
  3. Remove M6 X 14 bolts on main housing that connect side covers (you can use an impact for this)
  4. Lay out 4 towels, one for the sprocket side of the diff, opposite side of the diff, cover hardware, and tripod housing hardware/tripods
  5. Disassemble both sides of the diff keeping everything organized
  6. Inspect wear on differential parts and note down condition
