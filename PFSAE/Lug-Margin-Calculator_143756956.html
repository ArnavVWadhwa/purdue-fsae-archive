This is the tool to use for all lugs welded to the chassis, as well as camber pieces, tierod connection on the uprights, etc. Currently calculates all axial and oblique failures from Airframe Stress Analysis and Sizing.

Welds are analyzed in reference to Steel Structures, Design and Behavior 5th Edition by Charles G. Salmon, John E. Johnson, Faris A. Malhas. The analysis focuses on weld strength and the yield and rupture of the base materials which are welded together.

For the calculations from Airframe Stress Analysis and Sizing,  the user must manually read some charts. But values needed are calculated directly above chart to make process simple.

Values from prior sections may be used in subsequent sections, but not the reverse. Loads are entered at the very end and safety factors are calculated.


Airframe Stress Analysis and Sizing is available from Purdue Library online: Airframe Stress Analysis and Sizing (3rd Edition) - Knovel (purdue.edu). This calculator is 95% based off of section 9.8 of the book (lug analysis).
