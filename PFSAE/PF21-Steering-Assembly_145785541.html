Requirements: 

  • Steering Column
  • Steering Rack Mounts
  • Steering Wheel
  • Steering Column Mounts
  • Minimal Play (<0.5 deg)

Inspirations:

Very insightful article on the considerations IIT's Formula Student team made when designing their steering assembly.

Notes

  • Two design possibities:
    • U-joint
    • 90-deg transmission
  • Double u-joint phasing:
    • Get phasing right for linear output
    • Align symmetrically for symmetric response 
    • http://pami.ca/pdfs/reports_research_updates/(12h)%20Miscellaneous%20Topics/743.PDF details orientation stuff
  • U-joint angles:
    • U-joints have max angles
    • Want to have relatively similar angles for each U-joint
  • Safety:
    • Thorough, standard analysis for all connection points w/ chassis.
    • Create template for documenting structure analysis
  • Rotational play:
    • 1.4 from narrco rack
    • 2020 had additional 0.7 from column
  • Translational play:
    • Comes from bearings + bearing spacer play
    • 2020 used fairly trash needle roller bearings from mcmaster. possibly could reduce play with better bearings. Look at tube OD tolerances
