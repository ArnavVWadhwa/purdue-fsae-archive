2020

In 2020 the only changes to the fuels system were slight updates to fuel tank geometry, to ensure that the entire tank was filled during fueling, the hydramat was more properly utilized. Note that Sus TL does not think that lateral fuel slosh is significant enough to pursue damping beyond a couple baffles.
