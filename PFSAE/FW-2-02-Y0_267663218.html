Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Added additional vortex generator.

Should improve outwash further, which results in superior UT performance, and the downwash generated will aid in RW performance.

CFD PictureDescription

The additional vortex hurts FW by roughly 2 points but gains in UT and RW considerably. With a better upwashing section, this is likely going to be the best way to get more performance in Y0.

Note: drag is high due to an increase in RW performance which of course is quite draggy.
