Purdue Formula SAE : Harness Swap | Dyno

Created by Unknown User (jkrawitz), last modified by Paradise, Hunter on Jun 01, 2023

Start with Harness on Dyno:

  • Make sure diagram is correct and that if there are any changes to build/tune they are accounted for and written down
  • recognize the pins that stay on between harness' - ECU CAN wires and connection to pdm
  • Disconnect each power/ground terminal one by one - take out wires that go to ECU and wrap together with tape to label which they go to.
  • Disconnect CAN wires
  • Disconnect ethernet wires
  • Disconnect any big power wires if needed
  • Disconnect ignition module conenction(s)Â [refers to bosch ignition module]
  • Disconnect all sensors from dyno
  • tape wires together to mark branch out points if needed and to keep together in bundles
  • pull all sensor wires to area next to dyno box(tm) so they wont get caught on anything
  • Disconnect any other wires connecting ECU to dyno
  • Pull wires out of dyno box
  • pull harness off


Take other harness out of Tan Box

Put harness just taken off dyno into Tan Box


Putting Harness on Dyno:

  • connect power and ground wires to terminal blocks
  • connect CAN
  • Connect pins left on car to ECU connectors - ECU CAN wires and connection to pdm
  • pull sensor wires to your left side when looking head on at dyno if single
  • route sensors over fire wall if 4 cylinder
  • plug connectors into each sensor
  • connect starter to PDM
  • connect power wires
  • Check power continuity
  • Check CAN continuity
  • Plug in battery and try it out
