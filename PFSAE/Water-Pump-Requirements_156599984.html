Purdue Formula SAE : Water Pump Requirements

Created by Unknown User (dewey2), last modified on Aug 31, 2020

The general recommendations given by Land and Sea (manufacturers of our water brake) is 8 GPM at 15+ PSI for up to 160 HP. Given our engine will be producing around 85-95 HP, this is the most applicable criteria to meet.


Below are screenshots of the applicable section of the manual for sizing a pump. The 8 GPM at 15+ PSI is a general recommendation, but since 160 HP is much higher than what our engine will be producing, these pump specifications should suffice.
