Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Reduce upper endplate area

Increased upper endplate size considerably, continued to use the sloping strategy to, ideally, reduce endplate area without affecting the aero much. The additional endplate area should eliminate the low energy flow exhibited in the previous iteration on the upper surface.
