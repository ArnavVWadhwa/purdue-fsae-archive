Purdue Formula SAE : Two-Force Member Margin Calculator

Created by Unknown User (reyno105), last modified by Unknown User (agarw162) on Oct 12, 2022
  • Fix thread failure calculation - currently uses a total shear area that does not account for uneven thread loading

The two-force member calculator calculates margins for all possible failures of a steel tube with welded inserts or of a carbon fiber tube with bonded inserts. Johnson (plastic) buckling is included. It can be used for multiple separate tubes.

Select below to download


NOTE: carbon fiber tubes with bonded inserts should not be used in safety critical locations with tension until more testing can be performed on these. We proofed the tierods that went on PF20 to a 1.4 factor, and one of them failed later in the season.
