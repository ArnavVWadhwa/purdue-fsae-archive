Purdue Formula SAE : FW-1-20

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Aug 05, 2023

Simulation ML-039

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification

Increased span of upper flaps.

Increased AoA to be more inline with the main element.

To increase DF production from the flaps I increased the span. Additionally, the AoA of the first element was increased substantially. This will certainly separate on the upper surface, but I think the additional DF production will be worth it.
Changed inner sweep to have more tangency near the nose.

Increasing tangency reduces the overall sweep resulting in more DF production.
