Pressure gauge

This pressure gauge is used to monitor the pressure the air currently being compressed. It is rated up to 6000 psi, but we have only ever pressurized the tank up to 4200 psi max.


Temperature sensor

Supposedly, the display circled below measures the air compressor temperature. However, from my experience, this thing works only 50% of the time, so it is completely normal for it to not display anything.
