Purdue Formula SAE : Part 2 Basic CAD

Created by Crooks, Troy A, last modified on Oct 12, 2023


Section 1

In this section, you will design a part that you will later machine at the Bechtel Innovation and Design Center (BIDC)

You can design anything you would like, it can be simple or complex. (Although I would suggest making something that challenges your current skills and shows me that you want to be a dedicated member of the team)

The only requirements are..

That you use a CNC machine 

That your project is out of some kind of metal (I would recommend Aluminum) 

Show me your design to approve before you begin Part 3 NX drawings 


The BIDC has lots of free materials in the basement stock room all you have to do is as a TA. 


Side note: I understand that not all of you will be super interested in manufacturing and that is okay, but I think that understanding manufacturing (and the problems that can happen) will make you a better engineer and more valuable to a future employer. Also, I am letting you have free reign to choose whatever you want to make, so make something cool that fascinates you. 


Possible Projects

Also don't forget you can make anything you want as long as you ask me first 




The reason we have onboarding is to decided who is determined to learn, and who we should invest our time and knowledge in. Who will be the future of the team, and who will raise the bar of our team.






Section 2

Assemble the Front Left Outboard Assembly in NX 

The goal of this is for you to become more familiar with both NX and suspension components 

I would recommend starting by placing a fixed constraint on the upright and then constraining all your other parts from there
