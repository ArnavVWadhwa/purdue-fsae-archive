Purdue Formula SAE : RW-1-04

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Jun 12, 2023

Simulation ML-005

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Reduce endplate airfoil leading edge curvature.

Reducing the curvature makes it more susceptible to flow separation, but this will also increase the low pressure, potentially solving the low energy flow problem on the upper surface of the RW.

Add slot at trailing edge of endplate airfoil.

If the first change doesn't work, this should nullify the issue. Injecting high energy flow onto the upper surface of the airfoil should mix with the lower energy flow. One potential downside is vortices forming, we will see.
