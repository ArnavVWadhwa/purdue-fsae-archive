Purdue Formula SAE : Updating Adams Model Mass & CG

Created by Unknown User (agarw162), last modified on Apr 29, 2022

Main two things to check is the total mass of the vehicle and where the CG ends up. It's easiest to just add mass to the chassis but make sure to run a static vehicle characteristics (SVC) simulation with 30% front/rear unsprung mass to see how the reaction loads are on the front/rear to give the vehicle CG
