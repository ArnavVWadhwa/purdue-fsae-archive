F.5.8 Main Hoop Bracing

5.8.5 The included angle formed by the Main hoop and Main Braces must be 30 degrees or more.

F.6.3.4 Front Hoop Braces must be attached as near as possible to the top of the front hoop but not more than 50mm below the top-most surface of the Front Hoop.

Top half of FRH is vertical.

Bottom half of FRH is whack.

25.73 degrees from vertical as viewed from the side.

F.6.4 Side Impact Structure

Upper Side Impact Member lies directly in the center of the passing zone (parallel to the ground between 240 mm and 320 mm above the lowest point of the top surface of the lower side impact member)
