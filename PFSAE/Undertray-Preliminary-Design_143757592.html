Large tunnels2020 style undertray. Large tunnels on side of car with flaps. Small undertray.

Wing onlyFlaps across entire side span (chassis to outer wheel face), wing main element. No undertray.

Evaluation Process:

  1. Run bulkhead height sweeps for each design. 0" (flat) to 3" lift in 1" increments to find optimum height. Each design should have 1.5" ground clearance.
  2. Obtain Cl, Cd, and Cg values and plug into lap sim to determine design which results in max points. If there is a "tie" plan on running height sensitivity study to determine winner

All of this is being performed before FW/RW design, since any major decisions (lower RW flap, balance, mass flow) will likely depend on these decisions

As of 5/4 wing only is the leading UT type due to highest downforce, best aero balance, highest overall car ceiling (due to it reducing rw downforce which can easily be recovered), and most feasible manufacturing/assembly.
