Schedule

  • 10/24 - 10/28 VR3 order placed
  • 10/21 - 10/28 Sheet metal stock ordered (tabs, tab jig plates)
  • 10/26 - 11/4 Waterjet run (tube jig plates, tab jig plates, tabs, other jigging plates)
  • 10/24 - 11/18 Jig manufacturing (custom towers, custom arms)
  • 11/? welding begins (dependent on when tubes arrive)

Manufacturing Notes/ Reminders

  • Harness tabs need welded on both side of the tab
  • Tubes 401 & 402 should be done in stage 3
  • For tab jig plates, the rounded corner is the upper right corner when on the driver's left side of the car & upper left corner when on the driver's right side of the car
  • Drill out FUCA arm from 0.19 to 0.194 after suspension lugs for steering cover mounts
  • FLCA arm is likely going to need a little trim for rearmost floorpan tabs
