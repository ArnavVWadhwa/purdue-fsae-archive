Purdue Formula SAE : Assembly Manual

Created by Unknown User (mgal) on Jun 12, 2022

This page can be used to make an assembly plan for the 8020, and talk about hardware and positioning. Some things that can be discussed here are how different components need to slide on in order.


Fasteners


8020 has many different fastener options. The one used a lot in the ergo stand is the anchor nut. Anchor nut locations are notated by an extruded counterbore which is a manufacturing step for the anchor nut in 8020.




Currently, the assembly has way too many fasteners. For example, having three anchor nuts in the frame crossbeams is overkill. To cut down on fasteners I recommend looking at the load paths and using the fastener application test below to remove needed fasteners or change them to other types of fasteners.
