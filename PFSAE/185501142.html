MoTeC Guide on Coil Selection and Operation



Ignition coil dwell calibration for Inductive Ignition (We use inductive ignition on R6)






R35 coils are being used in Mitsubishi Evo Wasted spark systems coil on plug style so perfect for our single cylinder application. 


TLDR: Because of wasted spark and the need to inject half the fuel twice deadtime of the fuel injector can become a concern. In order to reduce deadtime, higher fuel pressures or larger injectors are selected. This however can hurt idle quality due to an injector that is oversized for low rpms. Like most things in formula and powertrain, its a fine balance.
