EVERY SINGLE THING NEEDS A DEADLINE AND AN OWNER. IF THERE IS A BLOCKER, CLEARLY STATE WHAT & WHO. 

Pro Tip: If people are blocking you and you have nothing else you can get done, go help the person who is blocking you. Not meeting deadlines no matter what the cause is unacceptable


Sub-Team should list out

  • Potential Changes from IDR
  • CAD Items 
  • Analysis Items
  • Purchase-Order
  • Manufacturing Season Prep
  • Blockers 


For any blockers, assign a number a number and clearly state with tasks are blocked by which specific blocker

CAD

  • Design X part (Blocker: 1)

Blockers

  1. Test X part on Dyno
