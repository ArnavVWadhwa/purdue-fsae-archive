2018Cooling system overheating on warm days

Cooling system was never properly design

A few compounding issues lead to this point

  • small radiator was chosen to save weight but was not actually verified to work and had no calculations to go with it
  • powertrain never really was tested in a competition ready state, new shrouds and fans kept getting slapped together week to week
  • water pump was failing (used multiple years) and did not flow water to spec
  • testing began much earlier in the spring, colder testing sessions so system was never tested at competition expected ambient temperatures

For comp

  • second larger radiator added to the opposite side the Monday of comp week (this was hell) added roughly 20 lbs? to the car, car did not overheat at comp, but competition wasn't really that hot, car still threw down


Cooling simulation tool designed and used for future seasons

2017Drivetrain explosionHalf shaft was too short, this allowed the tripod to come partially out of the hub. This would occur due to inertial forces from cornering. Initially, this would just pop off the snap ring. Rather than addressing route cause, we just made the snap ring groove deeper. Then when this happened it put the force through the diff and and hub. It broke the diff uprights and the hub.If something is taking more force than makes sense, don't make the part stronger. Instead, figure out why the overload is occurring.

2015/2014Hub broke at thread (center locks)

Either static margins too low or fatigue failure. This location is the perfect storm for a fatigue failure:

  • High cycle count because of rotation
  • Stress concentration from cut threads
  • Made from thick aluminum plate (low fracture toughness) + crack orientation possibly in bad direction (short transverse)

2018+ hubs made this area very solid. The weight to do this is pretty much negligible, so makes sense to design this to a static safety factor >3 and thus never really need to worry about fatigue. Also, take care to put a smooth fillet here. (Use a large radius cutter on lather)Suspension
