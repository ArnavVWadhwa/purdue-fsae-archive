CFD Analysis

CFD PictureDescription

This is a truly bizarre sim, I think it has to do with reducing relaxation factors which found a different FW solution in both Y0 and Y15.

The good thing is that at least the RW separation is practically eliminated and I am happy with this.

Now to talk about the new FW solution...
Keep in mind this is without a fan radiator, so literally the only thing that changed was the RW, and relaxation factors.

The strake separation on the DOWNWIND size got much worse compared to the previous solution, this is due to the curvature at the leading edge of the FW strake.

All things considered, this is a relatively easy fix, just need to curve the leading edges of the strakes, and reduce the inwash flow alignment at the LE of the strake.
