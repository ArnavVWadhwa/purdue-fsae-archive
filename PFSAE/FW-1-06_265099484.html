Purdue Formula SAE : FW-1-06

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Jul 18, 2023

Simulation ML-025

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Increased inner sweep, reduced overlap in inner sweep on the outer portion, swept the down washing flaps to align with inner section.

Testing to see if introducing too much sweep is bad. Eventually, this design will transition into a no-mid endplate concept. But to test the sweep for the no mid endplate concept, I am introducing a considerably harsher sweep in the inner section. This should also result in the vortex not blowing up on the outer portion of the inner section, reducing drag. Additionally, since I increased the sweep, the area also increased for DF generation. Similarly, with a swept down washing flap, there should be more down wash downstream resulting in superior RW and UT performance.
