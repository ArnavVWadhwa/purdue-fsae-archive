Material Properties

  • tu = tension ultimate
  • ty = tension yielding
  • cy = compression yielding
  • cu = compression ultimate
  • μ = Poisson's ratio
  • To find your yield strength and ultimate strength, find it from the 1st table, and then calculate your knockdown based on the number of cycles
    • Knockdown = Maximum stress / TUS
      • To find maximum stress, use the SN curve.
        • Use a stress ratio of 0, because we completely unload between cycles (There is more to it but I can explain in person)
        • For cases with under 10,000 cycles we will assume there is no knockdown 
      • TUS is written underneath the curve.
    • Final strength = Table strength * knockdown

AL 6061

40/35 = 1.14 = Table strength factor (adjusts for material difference)


AL 7050

65.5/60 = 1.09 = Table strength factor (adjusts for material difference)
