Purdue Formula SAE : Fuel Tank Parts

C73-755

This fill cap needs to have the interior threads greater than 35mm a smaller cap would fit better with the filler neck, but would not pass rules.

Can be found at CARSHOPINC.COM.

OR 

2 in. BLACK FILL CAP WITH ALUMINUM WELD BUNG - C73-755 at The Chassis Shop - in michigan so fast via ground shipping


You will need a tip over valve installed on the cap to let air inside the tank as fuel is used up. This opening to let air inside the tank needs to have a way to close if the car tips over so fuel doesn't spill out.

If you search 'tip over valve' on summitracing.com you can find a valve that will work.


 

We have used Jaz tip over valves before because that is what Earl's Indy had in stock. Go with a tip over valve that has a -6AN top and then screw a -6AN female fitting with a hose barb (like the one below) for the vent tube. Get some regular fuel line (colored transparent rubbery stuff 3/8" should be good, something like Tygon fuel line. I like this brand of fuel tubing https://fuel-line.com/ It's hi temp rated and very flexible, makes a good sight tube as well.

When ordering fast flow fuel line (https://fuel-line.com) try code PWC10 for 10% off.

Toss a regular hose clamp on the Tygon line at the barb, run the Tygon line outside the chassis and aero to the bottom of the car (read the rules), we have drilled a hole in the under tray in previous years. 


We have used a tip over valve in the past with a spring to help it close, this type of valve sealed closed with a little pressure and continued to build pressure in the tank.

If you don't use a fuel tank breather tube fuel will slosh out onto the car under high cornering forces. USE THE TUBE
