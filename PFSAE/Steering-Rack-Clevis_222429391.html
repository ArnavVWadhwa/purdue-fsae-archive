Purdue Formula SAE : Steering Rack Clevis

Created by Cole Henry Kniesly, last modified on May 23, 2022

Manufacturing steps:

  1. Mill Blocks down to size
  2. CNC end profiles, make sure to do as many as possible every time
  3. Drill through hole
  4. Drill out top counter bore with end mill
  5. Drill out bottom counter bore with end mill
  6. Drill side hole out
  7. End mill out slot using two methods:
    1. Use same size end mill end with consistent layer drops
    2. Start with 1/2in end mill and run full depth and go with center pass, two rough passes on either side of .025" and two finishing passes at .0062" to reach 9/16" slot width. Finish with 9/16 at full depth to clear out slot
  8. Sand down top profile
