Most Recent Status

  • Look into the design and continue work on some parts, the steering sleeve holder can be improved and be more ergonomic
  • Also, the cross tube can get in the way of someone's legs so you can either make the sleeve holder go down more or look at more options for mounting
  • The tube length needs to be finalized by using rule F.5.7.5

Dimensional Status

  • All the dimensions here are just placed by the driver you will need to finalize them, look at the rule constraints and create checks for all of them
  • Refer to (Dimensions (SD)) steering section


Possible Improvement


Place a torsion spring or other method (maybe friction bearings if that exists) on the steering column to create a drag torque. This can be beneficial for fatigue testing. If you have a drag torque you can have a driver sit and cyclicly steer until they fatigue.
