Initial Frame Material Discussion


Material option = Wood, Steel, Aluminum; Type Depends

Approximately need 312 inch of material

Option 1: 1 x 2 wood 

  • 10 Cents per inch = 31.2 dollars
  • 0.1 lbs per inch

Pros 

Quick to fasten stuff too but can be a disadvantage with fastener strength

very cheap


Cons

heavier than the aluminum

Durability


Option 2: 8020, or other aluminum stock

  • 0.37 - 0.64 cents per inch = 115.4 - 199.2 dollars
  • 0.07 lbs per inch (1x2)


Pros

Way better than other two for ease of design, motion, assembly, can be easier to mount to.


potential combimnation



https://8020.net/1030.html

https://8020.net/fasteningmethods/externalfasteners/bracketsgussetscorners/tallgussetedbrackets/4holetallgussetedinsidecornerbracket/10-series.html

  • 0.3 cents per inch
  • welding

Option 4: Steel Stock (1x2)

  • 0.65 cents per inch
  • 0.1 cents
  • welding
