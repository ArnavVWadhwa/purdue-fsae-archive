Purdue Formula SAE : 3. Suspoint Compliance Design Decisions

Created by Unknown User (hammerly), last modified on Nov 29, 2021

We've likely been interpreting rules regarding external components too conservatively since PF20. Shown below are the regulations regarding "External Items" and the definition of the "Component Envelope" as of the 2022 V2 rules.

Historically, we've interpreted outboard wheel assemblies as External Items, but this may not be the case due to the second point of F.5.11.1. Therefore, we may not need to triangulate suspension mounting points as shown by the team below.

This is where the wireframe model comes in. We can apply cornering loads at the suspension nodes with different tube configurations, evaluate the deflection of the node, then decide whether or not the mass savings is worth the deflection.
