Purdue Formula SAE : Control Arm FEA Forces and Coordinate Point Calculator

Created by Liang, David C on Oct 23, 2023

This calculator is used in suspension to calculate the forces and coordinates used in FEA involving control arms and tire rods. 

It is used in mainly uprights FEA (and tab FEA, but shouldn't be doing that unless some weird geometry which prevents the use of Lug Margin Calculator).

It just utilizes trig and basic math. 


Inputs into the springs tool in connections and remote forces in static structural
