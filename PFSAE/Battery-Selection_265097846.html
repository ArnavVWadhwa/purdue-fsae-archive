Purdue Formula SAE : Battery Selection

Created by Paradise, Hunter, last modified on Jul 16, 2023

Recommended Sourcing Location:

https://antigravitybatteries.com/productline/starter-batteries/restart-oem/


Notes:

  • Size the battery based on the capacity required to complete an endurance without charging (stator failure)
  • Look at the previous year's endurance current averages in i2 and estimate the draw of any new components
  • 1 Ah  = 1 amp for 1 hour, 2 amps for 0.5 hours, and so on...
  • The time required to complete an endurance is about 30 mins
  • See Engine Electronics | Documentation for stator outputs


Calculations

  • Battery Capacity (Ah) = Total Average Current Draw (A) x Time to Complete Endurance (hrs)

  • Power (W) = Voltage (V) x Current (A)
    • Battery Power (W) = Battery Voltage (V) x Battery Capacity (Ah) / Time to Complete Endurance (hrs)
