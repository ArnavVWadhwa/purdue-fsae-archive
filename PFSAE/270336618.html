Prerequisites 

  • Computer:
    • ME 2028 or 2038 lab computer
    • one of the remote desktop computers linked here
    • Personal computer

AppsAnywhere

Teamcenter and NX 1946 are not installed on the machines themselves. They are run through the AppsAnywhere Application.

Launching AppsAnywhere

Go to https://appsanywhere.ecn.purdue.edu/

Personal Computer

If this is your first time running the application, click install now. If not, you have the application already

Lab Computer

The above mentioned computers already have AppsAnywhere. Click "I have it already"

Click "open" on the next dialogue 


You should now see this screen

Launching Teamcenter and NX

click launch

click launch on NX


NX 1946 should open after following the dialogues. 


Once the green tickmark shows up

Select "continue" 


Follow the dialogues, and Teamcenter should open


Now...

Close NX and NX only

The instance of NX that is open is NOT linked to Teamcenter. 


Once NX is closed and ONLY Teamcenter is open, you can now search your parts to your hearts content and open your files through Teamcenter. This will relaunch the instance of NX that is linked to Teamcenter. 


Now, follow this confluence page on how to use Teamcenter → Teamcenter 101 - Purdue Formula SAE - Confluence
