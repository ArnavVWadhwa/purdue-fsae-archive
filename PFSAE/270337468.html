Engine Assembly

  • Dry Assembly 636 block and connecting rods separate

  • Measurements of ID of all connecting rods
  • Bearing selection
  • Bottom end assembly (crank through pistons)

  • Disassemble and check clearance
  • PD test with furthest degree of camshafts and thickest head gasket
  • Calculate comfortable clearance for thinnest head gasket + most cam timing available
  • Water dropper dome volume test
  • Final top end assembly
  • Fit degree wheel and measure cam profile anchors with dial guage
  • Finish the block (oil pump, covers, stator, etc.)
