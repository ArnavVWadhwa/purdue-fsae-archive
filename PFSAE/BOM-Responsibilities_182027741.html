BOM Guidelines:

  • Save your BOM here: Z:\2022\3_Suspension\2_Manufacturing\Component_System_BOMs
    • In that directory, you'll find an example from PF20 (PF22_SUS_BOM_TEMPLATE_20210531.xlsx), Make a Copy, Rename for your responsible components, and use that as starting point
  • Make 3 Tabs (copies of each other), One for each: Purchased Parts, Manufactured Parts, Fasteners (Bolts, nuts, washers, rodends, sphericals)
  • Parts that are different between Left and Right should be listed separately
  • As a start, don't worry about getting the cost/website/source for every single part (especially not fasteners)
    • Right now, just want to get all the parts documented and accounted for. Main goal is to just have everything in a list.
  • If a part is Purchased, but we do additional machining, list it as Purchased.
  • No need to include "Qty Designed" info for now, just "Qty / Car"


General Rule of Thumb for Parts Ownership:

  • The fastener stack set (bolt, washer, nut) should belong to the component that is on the "outside" of the joint "sandwich"
    • Examples:
      • Fastener's belong to camber piece for BOM (which goes with upright) and NOT control arms
      • Rocker BOM takes ownership of the bolts attaching the ARB Droplink, Pushrod, etc.
  • Rod Ends and Spericals should stay with the part they "naturally" want to upon disassembly
    • Spherical will stay in the CA cups, should go with those in BOM
