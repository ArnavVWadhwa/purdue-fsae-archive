Quick page on how to use Teamcenter on the lap computers

  • Prerequisites
  • AppsAnywhere
    • Launching AppsAnywhere
    • Launching Teamcenter and NX

Prerequisites 

  • Need to be on an ME 2028 or 2038 lab computer or the remote desktop computers linked here

AppsAnywhere

Teamcenter and NX 1946 are not installed on the machines themselves. They are run through the AppsAnywhere Application. It is a little bit of a backwards process in order to get started with it.

Launching AppsAnywhere

Go to https://appsanywhere.ecn.purdue.edu/

Select "I have it already"

Click "open" on the next dialogue 


You should now see this screen

Launching Teamcenter and NX

click launch

click launch on NX


NX 1946 should open after following the dialogues. 

Teamcenter will also open. Teamcenter is not required for this assignment. You may ignore or close teamcenter.
