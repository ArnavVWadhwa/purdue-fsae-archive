Purdue Formula SAE : FW-1-04

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Jul 14, 2023

Simulation ML-023

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Increased chord of endplate diveplane, and defined the separation point.

Increasing the chord gives the dive plane more area to generate the vortex for strength purposes. 

Defining a separation point allows for the use of a gurney effect upon dive plane exit.

Reduced AoA of 1st element of squirt suppressors by 5 deg.
On the outboard side, the yaw is causing separation on the upper surface resulting in dirty flow entering the side pods. Reducing AoA should help with this.
