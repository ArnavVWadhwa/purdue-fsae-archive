Side view - Full chassis

Overall goal: Simplify spaceframe design and pursue a rules-minimum frame, i.e. a frame consisting of only members strictly required by rules, with tubes sized to the tightest possible margins, arranged in the most weight-saving configuration possible.

Overall goal: Accommodate pull-rod suspension architecture and flexibility of engine positioning, while staying as "rules-minimum" as possible.
