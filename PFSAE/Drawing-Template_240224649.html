Purdue Formula SAE : Drawing Template

Created by Unknown User (agarw162), last modified by MacNab, Zachary Robert on Oct 25, 2023


  1. Open "PurdueFormulaDraftingTemplate"
    1. Make sure to not edit anything! It should be checked out permanently by MacNab, Zachary Robert 
  2. Open the part that you want to make a drawing for. 
    2. go to drafting
    1. right click the highlighted "Sheet 'A' (Work-Active)" section and copy it
    1. Paste Sheet A into your part. 
  5. You can edit the title block with the Populate Title Block button, highlighted above.
