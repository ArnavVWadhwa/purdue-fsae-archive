Simulation ML-019

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Altered vortex tunnel to a more rectangular shape to find a middle ground between the flat plate and circular tunnels. 

Should reduce inwash while still providing improved DF as seen in Chenkai's 14-14.
Outwashing endplate.

Outwashing endplate should benefit UT by outwashing the blown vortex. Section near vortex tunnel is blocked to provide additional outwash, similar to a gurney. 

This also provides slightly more surface area for pressure recovery as the final two elements benefit the most from this.
