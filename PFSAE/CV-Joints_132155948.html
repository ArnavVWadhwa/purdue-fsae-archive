The CV joints used on the car are RCV lightened tripods. They are more or less what every FSAE team under the sun uses, as there's nothing more compact or easier to use, considering they also sell the halfshafts and CV housings. These allow for 12 degrees of misalignment per the RCV brochure. This angle is monitored in our cad model via the halfshafts, so look at that page on more detail regarding the angle. The CV joints are lubricated with greased, applying grease around the housings as well as directly on the tripods. Be sure to get grease into the needle bearings in the tripods.
