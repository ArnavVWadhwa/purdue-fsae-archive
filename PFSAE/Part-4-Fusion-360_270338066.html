Getting Started for the first time 

  1. Create a Fusion 360 Educational Account and Download Fusion 360 
    1. This video walks you through what CAD and CAM at a very basic level (I recommend watching at 2X speed)

Fusion 360 Download: https://www.autodesk.com/education/edu-software/overview?sorting=featured&filters=individual 

Make sure you register for an edicational license and download to your personal PC

  1. Join the Bechtel Discord 
    1. https://discord.com/invite/TSaw2wY
    2. The discord will have important anouncments 
  2. Join the Bechtel Center Fusion team 
    1. https://forms.office.com/r/1s9aJENpxT Take this quiz to get added to the Bectal Fusion team
    2. MAKE SURE THAT YOUR PROJECT IS SECRET  
      1. After you take this quiz your name will be added to an Excel sheet, and then if you answered all of the questions correctly you will be added to the fusion team in a day or so. 
      2. You will get a link to join the Fusion team through your email ( check your junk mail!) 
        1. If you don't see an email after 48 hours of completing the quiz reach out to me on teams and I will add you Crooks, Troy A 
  3. Create a personal project on the Fusion team

 

NOTE: the Bechtel Fusion team has changed since the creation of this video it is now Bechtel Center NOT Bechtel Innovation Design Center


Steps to complete a project from start to finish 

  1. Export NX part file 
    2. Make new video from NX export to Fusion 360 showing our file folders
  2. Upload a part file to your Fusion 360 project
  3. Evaluate your part and create a plan 
  4. Model your stock
  5. Add your table model
  6. join your stock and model to the fixture
  7. Create a new manufacturing setup
  8. roughing toolpaths
  9. finishing tool paths
  10. Create a project on the BIDC Project Managment System 
    1. https://www.purdue.edu/bidc/os1/accounts/login/?next=/bidc/os1/project/ 
    2. Sign in using career account 
    3. Click on New Project 
      2. take a look at this screen shot for what to put in the discription 
      3. Name your project using this standard
        1. PF24 | Part name | Your name
  11. schedule a consultation with a manufacturing TA and get CAM approved
  12. schedule a time to machine your part
