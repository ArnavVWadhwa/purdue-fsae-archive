Groups

Ideally each group has a 'group manager' who coordinates people and checks the quality of the work. 

  • Harness CAD (Ming, Matt) (NX12 and wiring diagrams/power calculations)

    • Most work during December/January
  • MOTEC Hit Squad (code/software)
    • work on lap timing
    • GPS tracking using ECU
    • Work is pretty spread out
