Structure:

Below is the standard for the Confluence page tree. Bolded pages and their structure should not be changed unless a major architecture update is necessary.

  • Subteam
    • Education | SUB → Fundamental and theory based information relevant to the subteam (very high level)
      • Books/Papers | SUB
        • +++
      • Fundamentals | SUB
        • +++
      • Car Knowledge | SUB → Information that pertains specifically to automobiles
        • +++
    • Design | SUB → All information pertaining to the design of the subteam

      • Design Resources | SUB → How, where, and when to do things regarding the design of the car

        • Tools | SUB
        • +++
        • Subsystem A | Design
        • Subsystem B | Design
        • ...
      • Design PFXX | SUB
        • Meetings PFXX | SUB
        • +++
        • Subsystem A PFXX | Design
        • Subsystem B PFXX | Design
        • ...
      • Design Archive | SUB
        • Design PF(XX-1) | SUB
        • Design PF(XX-2) | SUB
        • ...
    • Manufacturing | SUB → All information pertaining to the manufacturing of the subteam
      • Manufacturing Resources | SUB
        • Troubleshooting | SUB
        • +++
        • Subsystem A | Manufacturing
        • Subsystem B | Manufacturing
        • ...
      • Manufacturing PFXX | SUB
        • Subsystem A PFXX | Manufacturing
        • Subsystem B PFXX | Manufacturing
        • ...
      • Manufacturing Archive | SUB
        • Manufacturing PF(XX-1) | SUB
        • Manufacturing PF(XX-2) | SUB
        • ...
    • Lessons Learned | SUB
      • Lessons Learned PF(XX-1) | SUB
      • Lessons Learned PF(XX-2) | SUB
      • ...

Important Information:

  • No two confluence pages can have the same name. Therefore it is important to be mindful when naming new pages that they have a year or subteam modifier if they are specific to a year or subteam. This will help avoid future headaches and allow for a structure that is consistent across the years.
  • For example, meeting notes that are named using a date (08/08/23) need to be accompanied by an subteam modifier and perhaps even a "title" → (08/08/23 - Title). This ensures that different subteams/subsystems that have meetings on the same day can create unique meeting notes pages.


Notes:

  • SUB: Subteam Modifier (CHA/SUS/AER/ELE/POW)
  • No higher than 4th child page
  • PFXX goes to archive at end of season and PF(XX+1) gets created
  • +++ → Add more here
  • ... → Continuation of pattern
  • Only child pages with a year specific parent page should contain any information specific to that year
