Purdue Formula SAE : Scales

Created by Liang, David C, last modified on Aug 31, 2023

POC: Liang, David C 


----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Decision for 2023-2024:

The decision for PF24 was to recalibrate the scales. If that didn't work, the next step was to just buy cheap bathroom scales. We deemed that the cost of buying new race scales wasn't worth it and we are fine with a pound or two of inaccuracy from the actual weight. The most important part is that the scales are accurate to each other. 

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Options:

  • Buy new scales
  • Find replacement for current one
  • Try to recalibrate scales (PF 19 → Suspension → Suspension Resources → Scales Calibration Manual.pdf)


Wants/Needs: 

  • Warranty
  • 0.1% accuracy
  • High ratings/recommended by FSAE teams
  • Not too expensive (must be <$2000, want to be <$1400-subject to change)
