Purdue Formula SAE : Steering Knowledge Resources

Created by Clayton Cobb, last modified on Aug 24, 2022

This page is a collection of information to help familiarize members on the steering system.

the goal is to know the parts that make up the system, how it works and design considerations.

I also recommend looking at past years steering design and manufacturing in Chassis Archives.

It is still a work in progress, but should help expand your knowledge, nonetheless. 

Steering Fundamentals:

Read through this to get an understanding of the parts that make up a steering system.

Look through the different systems to know what they are but mainly focus on rack and pinion 

and the steering column (U-joints, Gearbox, Etc.).

Steering Systems


Covers design of many parts of FSAE car, all steering products are located between 83-108. Covers Ackerman geometry, Gearbox steering column,

carbon steering wheel and upright design/ manufacturing for variable steering.
