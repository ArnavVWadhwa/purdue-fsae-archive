This is the home for documentation of all design reviews that were performed simultaneously with all sub teams. This includes internal design reviews, alumni design reviews, sponsor design reviews, and competition design reviews.

Inter-Team Design Review 

These design reviews are meant to be intermediate deadlines and held as inter-team reviews before we present to external advisors. This table is intended to be a guide to generally follow in terms of content to present but changes per the system being presented on.
