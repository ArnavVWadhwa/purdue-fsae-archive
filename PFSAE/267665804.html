Lessons learned for PF24 Chassis 

Frame

Part/TopicMistake/LessonReason/CauseCostSolutionPicture (if applicable)

(EXAMPLE) Frame

Engine Mounting

Tool access and overall serviceability for cooling + water pump + exhaust manifold area is shit

Mounting engine to tabs is shit

Engine location + frame dimensions + system packaging

Tabs get bent and warped over time, also hard to line them up 

More time taken for assembly and disassembly of affected systems (cooling, exhaust manifold, water pump)

Consider shifting engine location backwards to clear space for affected systems


Drawings / Jigs

Jigs bottom holes slightly out of spec (not centered)

Didn't dimension jig bottom holes off of center point




Jigging

Welded stage 1 tubes out of position 

Didn't measure enough, removed stage 2 tubes too early

Big gap needed to be filled



Welding

Porosity in welds 

Shielding gas affected by air flow due to welding table being positioned directly underneath vent in BIDC




Jigging

Tubes that are coped to table (stage 1) are misaligned

Too few constraints/jigs for tube




Jigging/Welding

Drilled wrong holes in tubes

Didn't jig all tubes up initially to determine hole location




Jigging/Welding

Didn't weight tubes mass alone

Dien forgot

Don't have reliable data on tube weight pre-welding



Drawing

Didn't indicate critical and non-critical dimensions

Forgot




Engine Jigging

Made new engine jig plate

Didn't know that engine jig plate is bolted to the "jig engine" 




Waterjet

Couldnt cut tabs in time

Didn't have backup plan for tab cutting (waterjet down right before cutting tabs)




Jigging/Welding

Couldn't characterize tolerance and error in frame jigging and welding





SES

SES approval got delayed

Started working on SES too late (2 days after release)




Dimensioning and Tolerancing

Holes in table jig plates too oversized (0.26 for 1/4 hole)



Should change this to 0.255 instead


Dimensioning and Tolerancing

Holes new standard 4in arms too oversized (0.397) for 0.375 holes



Should change to Letter V (0.377) or W (0.386) instead


Jigging

Concentricity of jig holes and table jig plate holes could not be kept

Didn't implement any locating feature/methods


Consider using countersunk bolts to enforce concentricity
