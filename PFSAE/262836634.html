Purdue Formula SAE : ECU vs CAN Expander | PF24

Created by Paradise, Hunter, last modified on Jul 13, 2023

We decided to purchase neither the M150 nor E888 in favor of manufacturing our own CAN expander


Background:

The team has been struggling to accommodate all requested sensors for a while. In addition, ETC for PF25 would require expanded capability, and removing the C125 for a dash in wheel this year (PF24) would require additional slots elsewhere. This is why we have chosen to acquire a MoTeC E888 CAN expander or M150 ECU. The difficulty with this is determining which is a better purchase as they have drastically different price tags. For a while, MoTeC would not respond to emails and so we believed that the M150 was too expensive to buy without a discount given the future of FSAE IC. Following this MoTeC did respond showing us that a 55% discount was available as well as the ability to sell our M130 ECUs for fair market value.


Notes:

E88 comparison factors the cost of the expander with the capabilities and function it + M130. MoTeC will not buy back our M130s, they must be sold to another team.
