Data Sources:

These values come from either a clamp-on ammeter or from the PDM data, both of which act a bit like low-pass filters due to their nature. Thus, there may be some near-instantaneous transient peaks that we cannot detect, but that will mostly only affect very sensitive EM components (as of the time of writing, we do not have such components).

These values will change as parts differ from the 2018/2019 components.
