Purdue Formula SAE : Load Calculation Documentation

Created by Aaltonen, Emil Johan, last modified by Cole Henry Kniesly on Jan 04, 2023

Brake Pedal: 

Load Case: 450lbf (2000N) applied at the ball of the foot

For max load on tabs, the hydraulic bias was maxed out towards the front with a bias of 65%

Calculations:

ForceXY
Pedal Force (lbf)=-Force*cos(angle)=-Force*sin(angle)
MC Force (lbf)
=MA*Force
Reaction Pedal (lbf)=-Pedal Force=-PFy-MCF
Brake Tabs=-^=-^
MC Tab Front=0=bias*MC Force
MC Tab Rear=0=(1-bias)*MC Force

Throttle Pedal: 

Load Case: 150lbf applied at the top of the pedal. 

There is no regulation regarding the force the throttle pedal needs to withstand, but we imagine 150 lbf to be above any force the throttle pedal is expected to see.
