Everyone to start working on drawings


Hubs

  • Length of stock should be exact
    • If stock is short can tell John and it will make ghost passes on the end
  • Stock prep is turning square into circle
  • 0.02 inch fillet might be okay depending on radius of John's tools
  • Will need to make jig plate for CV insert operation
  • Fillets need to be adjusted to be larger than nominal to avoid corner plunging
    • 25-50 thousandths greater ideal >10 would help
  • Hub nut threads manual
  • 3 of each at least → more spares = new member project
  • Need to learn mastercam for ME shop → Ask troy about questions


Actuation

  • TR hex stock is 0.5in hex 4140 and CA stock is 3/4in 4130
  • Tie rod shim bolts should not be press fit
  • Roll shims to 10 thousandths not grind
  • Don't forget to rough the surfaces of the carbon and insert before epoxy
  • Need to make a jig plate for CAs
    • Use same plates as last year and waterjet holes in them
  • Adjust rocker sizing (make certain areas/fillets larger)
  • Rockers will need 3 ops
  • Rocker stock needs to be longer in one direction so it can be held on to by the 5 axis
  • Spring retaining plate is not the same as the Ohlins → Does not have the keying
  • M8x1.25 - 12.7mm deep is damper shaft threading


Uprights

  • Add fillets to front camber piece mounting area (currently a 90)
  • Bring in rear tie rod area so it does not risk hitting wheel shell
  • 2 ops on 3 axis instead of 3 ops
    • Soft jaws
    • Machine the hole all the way though and top complex geometry then flip and put in soft jaws probe off the center hole and keep going
  • No real stock prep on camber pieces
    • Everything on 5 axis
  • 1 spare of each (8 uprights)


Wheel Centers

  • Waterjet donuts with hole in the center and on the outside
  • Need a sacrificial square block with large center hole → Then drill 4 wheel stud holes → Remove center lock and do center profiles and other profiles with 3 axis mill
  • Must learn Master cam
  • MODEL BOLTS FOR SACRIFICIAL BLOCK
