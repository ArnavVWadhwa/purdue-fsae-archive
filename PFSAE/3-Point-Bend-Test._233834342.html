Purdue Formula SAE : 3 Point Bend Test.

Created by Fava, Nicola Bernardo on Aug 26, 2022

Objective: Manufacture and perform structural test on carbon panels to validate layup schedules for given components.

Test 1: Floor Panel

From Test Derive: Stiffness, yield strength, ultimate strength, absorbed energy properties

Plan (in accordance with F.4.3.2. Primary Structure Laminate Testing)Â 

  • Prepare test panel 275mm X 500mm
    • 2x Laminates: Prepreg [0, 90]
    • 1/4" Nomex Honeycomb
    • Have bare edges without skin material
    • Cure @ 250oF
  • Place test panel in 3 point bend machine
    • Support by a span distance of 400mm
    • Load applicator: metallic, radius 50 mm (must overhang test piece to prevent edge loading)
    • Comparison Test
