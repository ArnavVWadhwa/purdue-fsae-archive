Purdue Formula SAE : FW-2-00 Y15

Created by Matthew James Lamear on Aug 08, 2023

Simulation ML-042

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification

Added another element to LE.

Altered sweep.


Adding another element adds an additional suction peak, resulting in higher downforce generation. 

This also allows the sides to generate the vortices closer to each element's lower surface due to sweep alterations.


Bulkhead decrease by 1 inch is now in CFD. The nosecone is adjusted accordingly.

FW-1-18:

(ignore Q criterion in top image)

The most outer vortex streak shown is a depiction of how the new sweep affected vortex generation due to inwash.

The outer vortex doesn't begin spooling until after the first strake. This results in the first strake having an overpowered vortex that ultimately bursts, resulting in poor UT performance. 

This can be avoided by starting the outer most vortex earlier as depicted in FW-1-18.
