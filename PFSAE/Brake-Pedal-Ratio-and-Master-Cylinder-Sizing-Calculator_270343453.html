Purdue Formula SAE : Brake Pedal Ratio and Master Cylinder Sizing Calculator

Created by Maningas, Isaiah on Sep 07, 2023
  • Made a calculator that finds pedal ratio, driver input forces, and reaction forces of brake pedal.
  • Also has what-if graphs that help visualize the effects of MC sizing on brake bias and driver force required to lock wheels.
    • MC sizing should be based on balancing tractive forces
    • The brake system ideally uses the maximum tire traction possible during braking
  • Calculator requires tractive force under specific braking scenarios and brake system specs from SUS.
    • Used tractive force numbers from MATLAB vehicle sim done by Kurisko, Keith Gregory 
  • Pedal specs to input are pedal angle, MC angle, MC length, MC bore sizes, and pedal length to center of brake face.
