Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Jul 25, 2023

Simulation ML-029

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Reverted design back to 1-8, and adjusted the strake length and general shape.

The strake length was increased to eliminate the bellowing issue. Additionally the CAD was improved to eliminate some unfavorable boundary layer formation occurring in the previous design.Â 

The inner strake is not extended the full distance since I believe having the vortex separate at that point should be favorable in depressurizing the tire.

CFD Analysis

CFD PictureDescription

Traded RW performance for UT while increasing DF from FW.

Immediately after the inner strake ends, there is massive flow sep. Need to just extend the strake to the end of the third element instead of ending it. Worried that this will result in slightly more inwash since the flow is turning back up on the second strake instead of the first strake resulting in more dirty flow inboard. But trading UT performance for FW is ok right now for balance's sake.
