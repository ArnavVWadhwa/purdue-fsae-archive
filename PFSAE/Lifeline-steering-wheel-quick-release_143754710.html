Purdue Formula SAE : Lifeline steering wheel quick-release

Created by Unknown User (jpeerbol), last modified on Feb 04, 2022

we have been running this for a while


Note aftermarket connectors we have purchased and connector that comes with the quickrelease are color coded differently


Common issues with the quick release is the steering wheel side connector coming loose

there is a ring that holds the connector on the steering wheel quick-release in place, this ring is liable to loosening after some testing, if this becomes loose the quick-release won't seat all the way and you lose electrical connection between the steering wheel and harness (you will lose shifting).

Do not use red loctite on this ring, you will never be able to take it out without destroying the aluminum quick-release, blue loctite should do you well.
