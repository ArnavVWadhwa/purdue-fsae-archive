Post - PDR

  • Reflecting on PDR
    • Determining Float.
    • Drill size validation.
    • Angled vs Straight.
    • Readjusting Maximum Temperature and Checking bolts + Validating Aluminum.
  • Perform mesh convergence study
  • Consider environmental corrosion
  • Ensure parts are sliding are resistant to galling/high friction
  • Ensure interfacing parts to not collide under intended range of motion
  • Consider tool access
  • Sanity check you use principal stress for brittle materials, not Von Mises 
  • account for worst case tolerances and fit-up loads
  • assess creep for sustatined loads at high tremperatures
  • ensure fatigue calculations are corrected for temperature and include vibration
  • verify human rated factors of safety are used if safety critical
  • Assess margin of safety on yield, ultimate, elongation, stability, fatigue, and joint gapping
  • Double check units at all steps
  • verify results with hand calcs
  • Verify model with test data
  • obtain peer review
