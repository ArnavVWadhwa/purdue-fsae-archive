Purdue Formula SAE : Aero Terminologies

Created by Zhang, Chenkai on Aug 15, 2023

Collection of terminologies, abbreviations, etc. people use. The point is not to define what pressure coefficient is, you should already know that, but the point is to define more niche terms you don't necessarily see unless you've worked on race car aerodynamics for a while or specific to FSAE.

This is a running list. Add things you have found to be confusing and are not on here

Keep it in alphabetical order.
