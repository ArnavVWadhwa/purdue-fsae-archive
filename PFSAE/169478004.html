READ ME: Subsystem Design Review (PDR + CDR) Expectations

  • Disclaimer: The example is for suspension. Below are things Dom expects for peripheral CDR.

Things to include in CDR:

  1. Title Slide with Image of Peripheral (Completed CAD).
  2. Main Goals you had for your peripheral, quantitative / qualitative.
    1. and how you came up with these goals.
  3. Process in which you designed your peripheral.
    1. Talk about design targets you wanted to hit
    2. Talk about mathematical models and how you used them to help design for targets specified
    3. Analysis done? (FEA etc.)
    4. Take us through your final designed product
  4. How did you integrate with other peripheral teams?
  5. Testing that needs to occur, either dyno and/or vehicle testing to prove mathematical models are working as expected.
  6. Plans for manufacturing, how long it will take, skills required, time required, manhours etc. 
  7. How does your design meet your Main Goals talked about in (2.)
  8. How does your design help meet Powertrain Goals (Powertrain 2021), and overall Team Goals (2021 Goals).

This is a formal presentation, do not take us through every single design decision you had to make (that is what the notebooks are for). (I still want detailed explanations of your final design.)

Extras:

  1. Completed CAD including tabs and appropriate material properties
  2. Excel BOM with Part names, quantities, links, prices. (Put in Z:\2021\10_CAD-22\3_Powertrain\PF22_BOM)
  3. Put Final CDR Presentation in Z:\2021\10_CAD-22\3_Powertrain\PF22_CDR_Presentations
    1. so I can view them and organize them on confluence for new members to see
