Purdue Formula SAE : Electrical Component IP rating requirements

Created by Unknown User (jpeerbol), last modified by Unknown User (reyno105) on Sep 18, 2020

Any electrical component required for competition use (anything required for starting and running the engine, controls on steering wheel and dash, related motec things) must have some sort of waterproof rating.

Motec components, M130, PDM15, C125 all use waterproof connectors, while a specific IP rating on the connectors would be better, the added cost for IP67 rated marine grade ecu's is not necessary for our purposes, especially with Motec's known quality and track record.

All Yamaha oem sensors are good. When it is possible and makes sense use a team standard high quality connect (deustch connectors).

For switches that are directly open to the elements, dash switches, steering wheel switches, starter solenoid, etc. should all be at least 'waterproof' from a known high quality supplier (not amazon) or IP67 rated (or higher).
