Justification

Optimized version of Onboarding project_v4

In terms of airfoils I made the leading edge of the main airfoil less pointy, as this will help with flow attachment.

No changes to endplate without the upper elements.

Removed Upper elements (Ianfoils)


old

new

Removed Upper elements as they have proven to only work on cfd and not in the wind tunnel, this is most likely due to the upper elements vortex inducing upwash on the main element. (This is why, when you add upper elements to your rear wing and check your stagnation point for main, its completely off)
