Requirements: (from Evan)

  • Shift time ~100ms (just a goal)
  • 100% reliability (the reason for shifting away from electronic shifting) (CRITICAL)
  • 100% driver confidence (needs to have good feel so the driver knows when the car has shifted) (CRITICAL)

Shifter and Clutch Force Estimations: (From Evan)

CLUTCH

  • Full clutch spline movement is about 30 degrees give or take, should be fully disengaged at about 15 degrees
  • Design adjustable rearward clutch handle travel stop so that driver doesn't pull with everything they've got and break the cable or lever.
  • Clutch spline torque should be around 60 in*lbs, definitely measure this yourself, DO NOT TRUST THIS VALUE

SHIFTER

  • 7.8 deg to shift 15 deg of total movement
  • 22.5 in-lbs shift torque
