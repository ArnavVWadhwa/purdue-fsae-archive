Notes:

  • CAN is the only way to sync M130 and Arduino data
  • Sparkfun amplifiers much harder to work with than standard analogue voltage ones
  • Adhesive must be purchased soon to arrive in time
  • All corners would require an ungodly number of amplifiers at approximately $15 each
  • Probably should use HX711 for Arduino → Will add additional development time
  • 500Hz ish
  • 7 column output to csv (1 for time 6 for force)

  • 120 ohm


Requirements:

  • 6 strain gauges per corner
