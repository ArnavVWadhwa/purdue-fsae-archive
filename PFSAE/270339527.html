Purdue Formula SAE : Is the Setup Pad Worth it?

Created by Liang, David C on Aug 30, 2023

POC: Liang, David C 


Background:

The setup pad will house the scales and allow for a level surface when weighing the car as well as measuring camber.

As we can see that the normal force of the tires change by about 6% of the applied weight when the car it pitched at 10 degrees and even more in roll!

Considering the scales will be only be off by a max of 1 lb, we can confidently say that if the car isn't level, it will severely impact the measurements and in turn will mess up the tuning and setup of the car. Thus the setup pad is worth it to ensure that we have reliable readings from our scales.
