Drivetrain Project â€“ Sprocket Design

  • Tools
    • In-house lap-sim
    • NX 12 (CAD)
    • ANSYS (FEA)
  • Goals
    • Gain familiarity with how to use the lap-sim for analyzing trade-offs for vehicle decisions
    • Learn more advanced tools within NX 12 (expressions, parametric CAD)
    • Learn and understand the sprocket force model and calculate the loads per tooth
    • Gain a beginner to intermediate understanding in how to set up and analyze a component in ANSYS
  • Deliverables
    • Determine final drive (via lap-sim) decision with evidence/reasoning
    • Selected chain size based on applied loads (comparison to different size chains)
    • Model sprocket in NX 12 (Completed preliminary CAD model)
    • Analyze stress under peak loading conditions in ANSYS (completed analysis with mesh convergence)
    • Written report displaying the engineering process and considerations made to design the sprocket (final drive decisions, packaging considerations, material considerations, force model, ANSYS methodology, mesh convergence, manufacturability, etc).
