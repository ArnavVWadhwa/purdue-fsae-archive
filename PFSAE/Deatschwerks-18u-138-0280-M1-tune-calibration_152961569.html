18u-138-0280 data sheet


Need from Deatschwerks:

Peak and Hold or Saturated Injector (Saturated?)

Fuel Injector Primary Peak Current (something like 2A? .1A-12A)

Fuel Injector Primary Hold Current (something like .3A? 0.1A-4A)


Notes of concern:

The original Bosch characterization has a minimum fuel injector volume of 0.001cc, the 18u-138-0280 has a minimum value of 0.0031. This is 3x greater, hopefully this isn't that big of a difference, the problem this could cause is too much fuel during idle / low engine speed. If the original 0.0010cc was a guess it could have also caused idle issues because the injector would have been at the bottom of the non-linear range.
