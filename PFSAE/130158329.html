Design requirement pages

This table has all the confluence pages that have design requirements / standards that MUST be met in all team designs. The rest of the pages in the section of confluence are tools / resources that help with design.

Vehicle Loads and Environments Master ListList of loads and environments the car should withstand. This is all the loads directly applied to the car as well as loads between subsystem boundaries that must be maintained / updated by a subteam for otherr subteams to use.
