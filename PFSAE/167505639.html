Which Set up are we going with in regards to pivot points?

  • The final position of the pivot points allows for the creation of an aerodynamic tip over point (ATOP). This means that from a closed position the flaps will continue to stay closed until the aerodynamic tip over point is reached. When the ATOP is reached the flaps will not begin to automatically open. The process is the same while closing the flaps, once the ATOP is reached in the open position the system will start to close automatically.
