Purdue Formula SAE : 2022.04.02 - Rear Inside Tire Lifting, Issue Tracking

Created by Unknown User (xia107), last modified on Apr 11, 2022

Using this page to track progress and data on attempts to solve inner rear wheel lift issue.


Problem

Scenario: During mid - high speed corners, 60-70 kph

Inner rear wheel seems to lift. More obvious on left hand turns, but our auto cross courses thus far have had more left hand than right hand turns.


Testing DayChanges/MitigationsObservations
4/3Rear ARB changed 2 positions looser (from 30 to 10 setting, then to 20lb/in)Seemed to help slightly, but not much. Driver then requested change to middle setting.

4/9All 4 corners at 400 lb/in with ARB at stiffest setting.

Car is very oversteering, seems to help with wheel lift issue slightly.

4/9Rear stiffness increased to 450 lb/in on rear. ARB still in stiffest setting.Car is very oversteer tendent, nearly undrivable
4/9Front very stiff (450), rear very loose (300). Similar to previous setup that was tested in the rain and inconclusive.Wheel lift improvement is inconclusive, but Dom felt the car was faster. skidpad time was best with this setup, 4.58 as opposed to 4.8 with other settings. Running out of options so continuing with this setting for remaining tests for the day.
