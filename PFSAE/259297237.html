Created by Paradise, Hunter, last modified on Jul 01, 2023

Manufacturing

  • Heat Shrink
    • Place heat shrink over harness before crimping pins even if not actually "shrunk"
    • Heat shrink closer to end of connectors such that boot overlaps with heat shrink
    • Heat shrink boots while harness is off of the car
    • Ensure that all parts of the harness are heat shrunk all the way up to the component
  • Overall
    • Minimize connector usage
    • Deal with shielded wire in a clean manner

Design

  • Design Event
    • Create more appendix slides and be more prepared for design overall
    • Have a very in-depth knowledge of the workings of each sensor/electronic component on the car enough to explain it to a judge
  • Harness
    • Create full pinout in rapid harness and keep it up to date with changes
    • Ensure that pinout and harness layout go all the way up to each component including the steering wheel

Testing

  • Be careful with placement and installation of wires such that none are pinned between two parts of the car
