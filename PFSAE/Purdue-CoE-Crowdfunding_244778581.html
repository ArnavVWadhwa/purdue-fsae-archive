Purdue Formula SAE : Purdue CoE Crowdfunding

Created by Panchal, Aarnav Nilesh, last modified by Rumsey, Helen Nicole on Dec 04, 2023

Guide

  1. Reach out to Tyson McFall-Wankat (tjmcfall@purdue.edu) and Stephen Stewart (stewarsr@purdue.edu) around two months before running the campaign. You'll likely need to meet with them and they'll explain the process and give you the template that needs filled out.
  2. Fill out the template. Try to think of a story. In 2023 we were raising money for engine modifications (since it was new and easier to see how it would have a direct impact on the car and students). Email the template to Tyson or Stephen around a month beforehand. They were a little slow in responding to me after I sent the template. I would email them and remind them about the campaign if nothing has happened and your campaign date is 2 weeks out.
  3. Someone at Purdue for Life will make the website and should send you a sample of the page beforehand. Suggest edits and then your campaign should go live on the date specified. Even when the campaign is live, you can still request for changes to made to the website. I asked them to switch around the prizes and swap out a video without any issues.
  4. Promote the campaign! Friends and family are the best way to get donations. Each week we set aside 5 minutes for everyone to email/ text their contacts during the general meeting. We also had business cards that we gave to people at events (Homecoming, COMVEC) with a QR code to the campaign.
  5. The money will take some time to reach the account, so don't plan on having the money until a couple months after the campaign ends.

Recommendations

  • Run the campaign in the fall so it doesn't distract from PDOG - there will also be a lot of new members who can reach out to their family and hopefully help you get funding
  • Seed the campaign during the first couple of days. It looks a lot better when there's a couple hundred in the pot so have team leads reach out to their family.
  • Best way to raise money is the leverage members in the club and alumni
  • Set a goal of $10,000 next year? We were close to hitting this number

Crowdfunding Fall 2023

  • Formula and Baja will run their crowdfunding campaigns at the same time (Baja didn't end up running their campaign)
  • Campaign dates are September 18th - October 31st. We chose these to hit Homecoming and our trip to COMVEC.
  • Goal set at $7600, but we made close to $9,700
