Parts and Explanation

PartExplanation
Balance BarAllows for the adjustment of breaking forces from front to rear. Changes brake bias.
Master CylindersTransfers pedal force to hydraulic force for the brakes
ReservoirsStore Hydraulic Fluid
Break LinesTransfer Fluid across the system
Heel Tray/ BarSupports heel
Throttle CableTransfer rotation of pedal into linear motion
Throttle Travel StopProvides hard stop for Throttle
Over Travel StopShuts down engine in Case of total pressure loss
Return SpringAllows for return of throttle pedal to starting position
