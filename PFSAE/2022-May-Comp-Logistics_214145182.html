Saturday - Day 4 (Endurance)

  • Pre-run:

    • Once we fuel up, we have to get staged
  • Run order:
    • Dom → JP
  • Driving notes:
    • If engine is overheating
      • it is hard to bring temps down but more doable to keep them from rising
      • mitigation begins with using lower rpms via upshift during maneuvers were power not needed (slaloms, corners with subsequent corners)
      • mitigation continues with short shifts
      • mitigation continues with using higher gears at expense of corner exit acceleration
    • If clutch or shifter feels off, get to 3rd and stay.
