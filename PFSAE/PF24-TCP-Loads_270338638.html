Summarized suspension control arm loading for various load cases

  • Normal (loads without factors of safety)
  • Make sure your calculator adds Factors of Safety to these loads


NOTES:

  • Negative loads are tensile forces
  • Positive loads are compressive forces
  • All forces are in LBF
  • Combined load cases are for loads under both braking + cornering or acceleration + cornering
  • Max lateral inside No WT assumes no weight transfer increasing the load the inside tire should see
  • Reverse braking load case is when the vehicle spins and the driver is to slam on the brakes
  • Fatigue Failure Mode is derived from typical vehicle operation
    • 35mph Cornering
    • 50mph Braking
