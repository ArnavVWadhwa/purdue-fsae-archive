Purdue Formula SAE : Welding

Created by Unknown User (jpeerbol), last modified by Crooks, Troy A on Sep 18, 2023

This is a guide for typical welding that is done on the team.


Steel 

4130

Filler ER70-2


Stainless Steel

304

Typically used for exhaust tubing

Filler rod selection: 308 or 308L 

Aluminum

5052 

Trial for bent items, 6061 works for bent items but 5052 is supposed to work better.

ER5356 - know for strength and ductility

6061

Typically used for fuel tanks, oil tanks, cooling tubes, etc.

Filler rod selection:

4043 - "4043 has no place in a professional environment", "4043 is a joke", "043 will turn a crappy black when anodized and you will be disappointed with the appearance"

ER5356 - should anodize fine, not perfectly though

7000 series

Don't ask if this is possible, this is outside our capabilities on the team.

Titanium

used for welding some powertrain parts, but it requires extremely careful cleaning and lots of gas to avoid oxidation
