Purdue Formula SAE : FW-1-17 Y0

Created by Matthew James Lamear on Aug 01, 2023

Simulation ML-036

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Backed off the strake separation point.

Backing off the exit point should result in no premature vortex bursting since there is less low pressure upstream to influence the vortex strength. 

The reason I'm not just going back to 1-14 separation point is because I want to ensure Y0 works as well as possible in this region.

CFD Analysis

CFD PictureDescription


Exit wake from front wing is good, I have no real issues with it. Seems like the major problem is the third strake unpowering the middle vortex resulting in lower downforce levels. This is interesting, but additionally the downwashing flap is producing considerably more lift. 

The loss in UT performance is honestly perplexing when looking at the iteration that did well. The only thing I can think of is that the inner sweep in combination with the downwashing flap closer to the inner portion produced better inner wake management.
