Tube Sizing Hand Calculations:

Used PF24 TCP Loads along with data provided by RockWestComposites on their 45550-UHM Carbon Tubes courtesy of Vasilev, Dimitar Kirilov for emailing about the specs. 

It appears that the 45550-UHM Carbon Tubes come only in the size of 0.375" x 0.503". This will work for all the tubes suspension needs except for the LCA due to the higher loads. Calcs for this size tube hold near negative or negative margins only for the bonded insert. As of today 10 Sep 2023 I have no clue where our "strength" information for bonded inserts comes from and needs more research. Ideally, our own testing will give us a better idea of the loads to expect. To be safe sizing up the LCA will be a good idea. Another note is that we have yet to validate our TCP loads calculations and need strain gauge testing to do so. Comparing loads to PF23 show less loads because a more conservative tire model was used in PF23 designing leading to potentially overbuilding actuation. A more representative tire model LMUY and LMUX was used (0.625) in determining performance and loads.
