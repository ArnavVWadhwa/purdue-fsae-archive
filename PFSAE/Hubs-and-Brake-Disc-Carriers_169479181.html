Notes:

  • Front carrier to be made from 7050-t7451 1.5" stock (we need all the strength in this part)
    • current front carrier bolt calcs doesnt take credit for this if conservatism needs to be reduced later. Also, the load is in a direction of larger E/D
  • Carrier provides significant strength to hub for cornering loads. Contact between them must be turned to pick up this effect. This also creates bolt loads that need checking. (Carrier bolt loads during pure cornering!)
