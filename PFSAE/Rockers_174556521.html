Purdue Formula SAE : Rockers

Created by Unknown User (reyno105), last modified by MacNab, Zachary Robert on Dec 03, 2022

Machining

Only machined part is the bearing carrier. And the only remarkable detail is to drill to ~0.008-0.012 under the reamer hole, and then ream.


Jigging

A full blown jig plate is not necessary, just 2 bolts (to ensure concentricity for the rocker plates) on the waterjet pieces and spacers across the rocker. Make any custom traffic cones before meeting with a welder and jigging the rockers.  

Welding

  • Make a few spare bearing carriers a) in case you lose them, and b) so you can use them to control plate spacing as you weld.
  • Very slightly press a bearing (just 0.125-0.250" into the carrier) in to the carrier so ensure alignment during welding
  • Use vise grips and C-clamps to hold together.
