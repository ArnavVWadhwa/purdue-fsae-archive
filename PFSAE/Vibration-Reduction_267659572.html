Purdue Formula SAE : Vibration Reduction

Created by Paradise, Hunter, last modified on Jul 30, 2023

POC: Paradise, Hunter Skrzyszowski, MateuszÂ 


Vibration interferes with sensor readings and reduces the lifespan and reliability of electronic components. This page will discuss methods to reduce vibration in electrical components.


  • Mount away from sources of vibration like the engine
  • Stiffen the mounting (to an extent)
  • Use grommets or soft hook-and-loop tape
  • Maximize strain relief for wires
  • Seal with potting compound (ie. epoxy)
