  • Drivetrain
    • White drivetrain tote with: (all of this should already be in there)

      • Spare diff clutch packs (yellow package)
      • Spare CVs
      • Spare Boots
      • Drivetrain tools (snap ring pliers, allen wrenches/rods, whatever was already in there don't take anything out)
      • White box with misc bolts
      • Black box with chain breaker tools
      • Extra sprocket (34 tooth)
      • Extra sprocket spacer(s)
      • Flathead screwdriver
      • Chain breaking tools (black box)
    • Yellow drivetrain box with misc bolts and extra snap rings
    • Diff oil
    • Extra chain
    • Extra halfshafts (see prev pic with white tote)

Diff specific tools are in the white box. I figure we'd be bringing the impact, socket adapter for impact, socket adapters, and sockets so that'd be covered
