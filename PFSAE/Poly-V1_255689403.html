Purdue Formula SAE : Poly V1

Created by Zhang, Chenkai on Mar 23, 2023

Major changes:

  • Polyhedral cells
  • Changed the shape of car refinement block
    • More car shaped to save cell count
    • Cutout for back of the car to save cell count
  • Unrefined the nosecone
    • Removed curvature refinement
  • Refined the nosecone
    • Added more prism layer to get better volume change between prism layer and outer mesh
    • Probably not good for y+
  • Refined the ground
    • Now at 5 layers of prism layer
    • Added surface proximity refinement
      • Properly refine regions between ground and the car
    • Reduced minimum cell size to get better resolution under the car
    • Reduced surface growth rate to slow size growth behind the diffuser and in front of the front wing
  • New car wake block

Results

  • RF tire diverge after 200 iterations

Issues

  • Ground prism layer to outer mesh volume change too big
  • Still want more refinement in side tunnels
  • Parts near the engine or with high curvature, prism to outer mesh volume change too big
  • Wake block possibly too small
