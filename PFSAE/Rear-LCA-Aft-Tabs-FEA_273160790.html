    • Used Rear LCA Aft Tabs v2 as of16 Oct 2023 (i think v3 is bugged atm)

Excel sheet with calcs for forces and springs: Drive → 2024 → 3_Suspension → 3_Structures → Actuation → Actuation Tabs Calcs 


 


Steps

1) Export as STEP → data to export → selected parts → select tabs and near chassis tubes 

2) Import into NX and cut away extra tube length (idk how to do it in spaceclaim)

3) Unite all objects and fillet to stimulate welds (do not do between tabs cuz they don't want to weld that)

4) Export back into STEP

5) i messed up, so have to import into ANSYS 2023 through appsanywhere

6) added loads from PF24 TCP Loads

7) Fix ends of each chassis tube

8) force and spring 1 are the tire rod (rear) and force and spring 2 are the LCA
