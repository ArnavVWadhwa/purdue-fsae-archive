Purdue Formula SAE : Pedal Box PF23 Commentary

Created by Dien Quang Nguyen on Jul 14, 2023

Breakdown of components and design decisions


Pedal box rail

  • Rail with equally spaced holes
    • Designed for accommodation of 5th percentile female to 95th percentile male drivers
  • Pins for connecting pedal box body to rail
    • Increase serviceability - Pin can be released and pedal box body can be moved quickly and easily

Pedal box body

  • 4130 steel chosen for strength and rigidity, to meet stress and loads requirement
  • Several structural tabs were added to increase rigidity


Heel tray 

  • Added for driver comfort 
  • Allows driver to rest their heels on the heel tray and get more control / force output onto pedals

Toe tray


Brake pedal

  • Top hole for brake bias bar (rotational adjustment about the z-axis) → bar moves back and forth


Throttle pedal
