Purdue Formula SAE : RW-1-08

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Jun 24, 2023

Simulation ML-009

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Changed the fourth element to a normal airfoil.

To reduce drag I utilized a normal airfoil. I'm worried about structural integrity of the gurney, but this can be fixed later if needed. 
Altered Chord lengths for each element.

Made chord incrementally decrease. This is to optimize the surface area of the airfoils that are closer to 0 deg AoA.
