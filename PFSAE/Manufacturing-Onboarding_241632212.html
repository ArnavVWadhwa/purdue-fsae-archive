Bechtel Onboarding

We will be doing most of our manufacturing at the Bechtel Innovation Design Center

Below I have included the steps needed to be able use their space.

Step 1) Watch the following videos provided by BIDC to get Fusion 360 downloaded and to join the Bechtel team

1. https://autode.sk/3aP8xBr - Getting Fusion360
2. https://autode.sk/3aQ5WHz - Joining the Team
3. https://autode.sk/2ZNG6NX - Making Correct Project Types
4. https://autode.sk/2NAzQ9V - Transferring Projects
5. https://autode.sk/3kiYRSO - Copying Workholding Files

Step 2) Once you have been added to Bechtel's Fusion team message me on Teams (nbfava) so I can invite you to our project

Step 3) Before you go into use the machines, the respective safety quiz will need to be completed on Bechtel's Passport website, the link is included below.

https://www.openpassport.org/Account/Login?ReturnUrl=%2f%3f_ga%3d2.210395473.590182154.1653148181-764514366.1653148181&_ga=2.210395473.590182154.1653148181-764514366.1653148181

I will let you know what quiz you have to do before you go in for your scheduled time.
