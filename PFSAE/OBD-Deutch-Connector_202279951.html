OBD → On-Board Diagnostic Program


DT connectors were selected over DTM connectors because of backwards compatibility with PF20 and availability. Other rough justification was that it is a larger connector so it is easier to manipulate.


Some of these items can be acquired from TE.com as evaluation samples or purchased from Mouser.com.
