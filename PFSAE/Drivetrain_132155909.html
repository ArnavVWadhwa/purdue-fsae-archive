Minimum Viable Product Drivetrain

  • Overall
    • Able to stand the loads applied from the engine during operation
    • Able to adjust chain tension throughout the season
    • Transmit engine power to the driven rear wheels
  • Halfshafts & CV's
    • Halfshafts sized such that they cannot fall out during vehicle operation
    • CV joints sufficiently greased at all times
  • CV Boots
    • Properly secured to CV housings to prevent foreign objects from entering
  • Chain Guard
    • Satisfies rules requirements
    • Mounting robust enough to prevent movement during vehicle operation
  • Chain
    • Chain properly tensioned at all times
    • No half links
      • Half links used in the past are weaker than the force on the chain, leading to failures including:
        • Binding of half link, preventing chain movement
        • Yielding on pin hole, causing pin to bend or fall out

        • These half links have had pin failures
      • Another vendor here

  • Sprocket & Uprights
    • At minimum, able to sustain loads under peak acceleration without failure
  • Differential
    • Serviced per the Drexler manual
