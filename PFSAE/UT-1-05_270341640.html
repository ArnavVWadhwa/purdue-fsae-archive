Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification

Increased Rad total area to 113 in^2.

This increased sidepod height and width.

Additionally, a fan was added to the radiator, and one of the sidepods is empty.

Implementing the rad/fan combo.

Side tunnel height decreased from 8in->6in

Idea is to test if this is a valid strategy for keeping the vortex alive.

Fully expect UT performance loss, mostly a test.
