Email Invite

The FSAE team will be hosting a design event practice on Sunday, March 26th starting at 1pm EST and I would like to extend an invite out to you. This is to help prepare our presenters for the breakout style design event alongside reviewing our design briefing and supplemental design material. 

The practice will be held through Microsoft Teams with a breakout room for you all based on your experience with the team. We will have 5 total breakout rooms with Suspension, Powertrain, Chassis + Cockpit/Controls/Brakes/Safety, Aerodynamics, and Systems Integration. As of now I have you in [] breakout but if you feel like you’re a better fit in another section just let me know. 

 

The schedule will be:

1pm - 1:10pm: Intro

1:10pm - 2:00pm: Breakout Event

2:00pm - 2:15pm: Closing Remarks

 

All times are in Eastern time and maybe be slightly shorter or longer depending on how the duration of the presentation/feedback. 

If you are interested and available, please reply to me with the breakout you would like to be a part of. 

 

Thank You!
