Purdue Formula SAE : FW-14-19

Created by Zhang, Chenkai, last modified on Jul 12, 2023

Simulation: CZ-048

Changes/Justifications

Description of the Change

Picture

Justification

Reverted to wide wing tip

I have absolute no idea why I decided to ditch this design a few iteration ago, since it's just superior. Larger wing tip depressurizes the downwind side tire better, resulting in increased downforce from downwind side flaps. The trade off of course is flap area, but apparently the trade off it worth it.

CFD Results Analysis

CFD Picture

Description

The depressurization is real. But the performance gain on the downwind side did not offset the performance loss on the upwind side. 

Upwind vortex is slightly tighter, since it's more inboard, leading to a little bit UT performance gain.
