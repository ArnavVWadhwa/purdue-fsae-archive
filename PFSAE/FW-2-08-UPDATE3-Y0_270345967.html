Purdue Formula SAE : FW-2-08-UPDATE3 Y0

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Sep 14, 2023

Simulation ML-066

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Changed base of both turning vanes to symmetric airfoils.

After running a Y0 that went undocumented of the full car with the fan, I realized that the wake from the turning vanes is the ultimate root of the problem when it comes to strake separation.

To combat this I changed the base of the turning vanes to a symmetric airfoil to reduce this separation.

If this still doesn't solve the issue, I am going to remove the inner turning vane, if that still doesn't prevent the issue, I am going to make the turning vanes float, and there will need to be supports holding them up.
