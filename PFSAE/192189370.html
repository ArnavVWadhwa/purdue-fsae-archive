Calibrating the dyno using values from Motec that the load cell is reporting

F = (V + O) * M → F = (V - 723) * -0.01361

V = reported load cell voltage from motec

O = zero offset

M = Load cell slope

F = Force at load cell

Load cell: voltage is in mV/ V of excitation

Our excitation is 5V

So volts from it is really input*5

Need to figure out m value to send to izze ( f= m* voltage)

100 =  m*2.04544 * 5

M = 100/(2.04544 * 5 * 10^-3) = 9,777.84731  lbs/v

Send to izze = lbs/uv = m = 9777.84731/(10^-6) = 9.77785E9

 m = 9778 *10^6
