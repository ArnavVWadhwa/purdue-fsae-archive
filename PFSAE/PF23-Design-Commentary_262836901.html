PF23 Steering Commentary

-Play originates in the steering rack. Cause is still unknown since disassembly is required to determine the exact reason why

-Tube bending mitigation seems to be very successful but more design and though should be applied to help mitigate it even more to increase driver responsiveness.

-Need to look at phasing of U joints and come to a conclusion about that




  • Make designs with manufacturability in mind.Â 
  • Understand what tolerance is and that machining won't be perfect
  • Welds and Bending will introduce error in parts
  • Check clearances to other parts
  • Figure out how assembly will work while designing
  • Reduce part count if possible
  • Use fixtures during mfg and assembly to insure stuff lines up (especially tab welds). DO NOT REMOVE UNTIL WELD IS DONE
  • Warping caused by welding may become an issue (steering col supports PF22)
  • Can we simulate how much the steering column will bend?
