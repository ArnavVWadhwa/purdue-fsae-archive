Changes for 2021:

  • increase size of top cockpit braces (were bending under load of the driver getting in and out)
  • reduced OD of non structural needs to be more closely examined (would larger OD be better due to increased moment of area)
  • pay more attention to small to large thickness weld joints (hard to get a good weld at these connections so avoid)
  • rearmost engine mounts 0.95 was dumb see if small non-structural tubes are good

Requirements: 

  • pass SES
  • meet LLTD requirements
  • meet 1.4 SF on peak expected loads 
  • ...
