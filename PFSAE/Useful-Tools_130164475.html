Hexcel Cured Ply Thickness Calculator

https://www.hexcel.com:82/calculators/src/CPTCalculator.aspx

Use this website to determine the thickness of cured components. Keep in mind that this calculator assumes things are done properly - i.e. leak free samples bagged and cured using VARTM or prepreg.

Values that we use for each entry:

Reinforcement areal weight (gsm): Weight of the carbon. Most of our carbon is either 210gsm prepreg, 335gsm twill fabric, or 88gsm spread tow.

Resin content: Enter the percentage of resin by weight here. Note that it should be the actual percentage (for example if the content is 45% enter 45,Â not 0.45). Prepreg you will know the resin content, for VARTM we typically see resin content between 45% and 40%.

Resin/fiber density: Enter the density. Some of our densities are below.

T300/TR50: 1.77g/cm^3

PTM&W 2712: 0.97g/cm^3

Void volume: This is a really tricky one to know unless you are making hundreds of parts and can manually count the voids to establish a statistical basis for this value. For us leave it as zero and assume that your laminate will be slightly thicker.
