Purdue Formula SAE : Poly V4

Created by Zhang, Chenkai on Mar 30, 2023

Major Changes

  • Optimizing prism layers now
  • Halved front wing prism layer total thickness because front wing boundary layers tend to be very thin
  • Increased front wing prism layer count to 20 to reduce wall y+
  • Reduced rear wing prism layer total thickness because it's too thick (only 3/4'ed)
  • Increased rear wing prism layer count to 19 to reduce wall y+
  • Reduce wheel prism layer thickness and increased prism layer count to better capture the boundary layer and get better wall Y+
  • Captured the rest of the front wing wake with tire wake refinement block, which should also help with yaw cases
