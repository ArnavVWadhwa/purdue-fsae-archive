Purdue Formula SAE : FW-1-12 Y15

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Jul 28, 2023

Simulation ML-031

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Cut area of strake on 2nd element and 3rd element.

Since the separation is so severe in between the strakes higher up, the strakes have been cut to eliminate this separation. There is a second suction peak on the 3rd element that may be formed due to the strake acting as an endplate, so this may be a loss of DF generation. Alternatively, the lack of flow sep could produce as much DF or more than the double suction peak.
Reduced inner strake length.

The increasing section on the inner strake from the previous iteration saw no gain from having the vortex at a higher position. Thus, that section is cut.

CFD Analysis

CFD PictureDescription

The outer vortex is still bursting. My current theory is that the vortex isn't being given enough in wash to not break down. The first strake region is potentially too large. 

Poor RW performance is likely just that the RW is at critical AoA; however, the flow going towards the RW can be improved here. Reducing 3rd element AoA may reduce the wake to the RW and to the down washing flap.
