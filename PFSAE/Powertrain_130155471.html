Purdue Formula SAE : Powertrain

Created by Unknown User (jpeerbol), last modified by Horton, Tyler Jason on Sep 04, 2023

Succ Squeeze Bang Blow Amirite


Minimum Viable Product:

  • The Powertrain's responsibility is to provide reliable power to the wheels, driver needs to be able to trust system
  • Powertrain specific parts should be manufactured to comp spec by specified deadlines
  • 3 endurance runs back to back will ensure the reliability of our system
  • The minimum viable product will need to pass rules as stated in section T5 of the FSAE rule book.
  • Basic rules include:
    • ENGINE: (IC.1)
      • 4-stroke piston engine with a max displacement of 710cc per cycle
    • PERIPHERALS:  
      • INTAKE: (IC.2)
        • intake is restricted to 20mm for gasoline and 19mm for E85
        • throttle (IC.3) can be cable actuated or electronically actuated (IC.4)
        • many additional rules for safety
      • FUEL: (IC.5)
        • fuel selection can be either gasoline or E85 only
        • many additional rules for safety
      • EXHAUST: (IC.7)
        • exhaust must be separated from touching anything (several additional safety rules)
        • exhaust fumes must be directed away from driver
        • vehicle must remain below the permitted sound level
      • COOLING: (T.5.4)
        • must use distilled water
        • no leaks
        • catch can required
      • OIL:
        • no leaks
        • catch can required


System Design Specs:

Cooling system designed to keep engine coolant temp under 120C at worst case vegas, california, mi, nebraska competitions, what ever is worst. 2x endurances without more than driver change break between for hottest Indiana May weather or August? whatever is worst?

Fuel Tank sized to 1.2 safety factor of hard endurance on e85? Want goal of being able to run least 'efficient' fuel at full tilt
