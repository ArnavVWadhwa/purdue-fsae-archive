Purdue Formula SAE : Lowered Bulkhead - Y0

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Jul 15, 2023

Simulation BH-001

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Lowered bulkhead by 1 inch. This makes the floor of the bodywork flat.

Testing a bulkhead height decrease to ensure there are no problems.
Nosecone slightly reshaped.

To account for the decrease in bulkhead, the nosecone shape needs to be altered. This resulted in less curvature at the DF-producing section. Likely to result in less DF but potentially more pressure recovery, helping UT.
