Test day summary:

Met at shop at 8am

Ben Reynolds was driver

Left Grand Prix track at 11:30am


Initial Issues:

  1. Rear left LCA to Upright bolt loose
  2. Front left LCA to Upright bolt loose
  3. Oil leak
  4. Starter did not work

Car Runtime:

  1. Engine ran for ~5 minutes for warm up
  2. Car drove for 5 lap warmup
  3. Attempted to do a 23 lap, 1/2 endurance
    1. Observed slight smoke, ended after 9 laps


Issues developed:

  1. Throttle wouldn't fully return to 4%, instead would stick at 11%
  2. Sticking throttle caused driver to excessively use brakes
    1. Ate through the majority of brake pads (my best guess is 75%) at this testing session
    2. It seems as though calipers are also sticking shut after each brake use, we needed to pull the pedal rearward to "release it" and have the wheel spin freely
