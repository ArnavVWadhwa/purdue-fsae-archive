Goals:

  • Can servos move wings?
  • Can they do so quickly and reliably?
  • Does the mounting method using carbon fiber on wings work?
  • Can servos move wings while car is moving?
  • Can a backup method be added?
  • Can mechanical linkage move all wings


Will we do an initial set up (maybe using old car wings) and test servos on that before on car? Or cardboard or other material cut outs?

Would data gathered using cardboard or other material wings be relevant?

Is there a material similar to carbon fiber(in weight? and durability)  that we could use?


Design circuit - will need ECU and probably DASH and PDM. 

What will testing apparatus be connected to? Will it just be clamped onto a table?

Parameters to Test:

  • speed
  • reliability

Test these by:

  • timing difference between when button is pressed and when wings go into final position both open to close and close to open
  • Open and then close wings 15 times in a row or more?
