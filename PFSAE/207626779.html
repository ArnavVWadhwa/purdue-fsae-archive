General Address Information

  • AVOID: 0s & 100s (unless vital)
  • Use 200s through 500s at your discretion. If something really needs to prioritize everything else on the bus but isn't more important than the vital stuff.
  • Only use 600s for ECU Transmission
  • Only use 500s for PDM Transmission
  • Only use 700s for Dash Transmission (WARNING: Max identifier length on CAN is 11 bits, which is 7FF in Hex. DO NOT use anything greater than this)
