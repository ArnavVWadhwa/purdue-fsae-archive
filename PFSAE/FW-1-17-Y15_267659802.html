Purdue Formula SAE : FW-1-17 Y15

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Aug 01, 2023

Simulation ML-036

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Backed off the strake separation point.

Backing off the exit point should result in no premature vortex bursting since there is less low pressure upstream to influence the vortex strength. 

The reason I'm not just going back to 1-14 separation point is because I want to ensure Y0 works as well as possible in this region.
