Purdue Formula SAE : Medium UT Justification

Created by Zhang, Chenkai on Oct 05, 2022

Sensitivity to Tire Squirt

  • In the last week of design, a CFD error occurred so that we had a big tire squirt volume.
  • This made UT Cl go from 1.39 to 1.14.
  • This is caused by UT inwashing the now increased tire wake.
  • The inwashed tire wake can't generate any vorticity off of the strakes → higher diffuser pressure → lower UT performance everywhere
  • Since we are unsure the exact tire squirt geometry, tire squirt sensitivity is bad


Towbar Considerations

  • In order for the towbar to take be able to take side loading, there needs to be cross bracing
  • The cross braces are nowhere near aligned to the flow → pressurization of diffuser → even worse UT
