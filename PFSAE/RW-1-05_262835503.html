Purdue Formula SAE : RW-1-05

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Jun 13, 2023

Simulation ML-006

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Added flow conditioning secondary flap.

Outer sweep downwashes to reduce the low energy flow on the top surface of the main element. Inner portion up washes for additional downforce.

Airfoil arrangement same as RW-1-04.

Same strategy as before, hoping that removing the slot gap fixes the separation issue. Separation only occurred at the slot gap as discussed, so this should be good.

CFD Analysis

CFD PictureDescription


The endplate is still separating, so I'm going to increase the max thickness more. 

The secondary flap removed the low energy flow on the upper surface, but the downforce-producing section largely failed to produce any downforce due to the low energy flow from the roll bar. Thus, in the next iteration, I am going to try to just use a down-washing outer section.
