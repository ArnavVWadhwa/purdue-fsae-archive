Purdue Formula SAE : GSL 414 Fuel Pump

Created by Grorud, Jesse Tait, last modified by Unknown User (jpeerbol) on Jan 24, 2022

https://www.highflowfuel.com/genuine-walbro-gsl414-external-inline-fuel-pump-cannondale-440-cc-atv-5000168/

https://www.autoperformanceengineering.com/html/ext_pump.html

Auto Performance Engineering has 1 in stock and can get more.

According to them, this line of Walbro pumps can be damaged with PWM. The GSL 414 in particular is very vulnerable.

Also, this pump has a tendency to fail due to overheating, like many exterior pumps. It is best to find a way to keep the pump in a cool/well ventilated area and keep fuel temps lower.

It would be worth it to purchase and test one, and if we are happy with it than backups will be needed. 


Very interesting forum thread about the gsl414 pump with test data and some pictures of a disassembled walbro GSL pump on page 3: https://vansairforce.net/community/showthread.php?t=168793
