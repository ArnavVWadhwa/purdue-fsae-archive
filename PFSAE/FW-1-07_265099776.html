Purdue Formula SAE : FW-1-07

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Jul 18, 2023

Simulation ML-026

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification

Removed mid-endplate in favor of sweep.

Decreased amount of area of the outer portion. 

Reduced outer sweep AoA of down washing flaps.

Sweeping should reduce drag considerably. The flow sep from the previous iteration due to the endplate should no longer exist, improving drag and DF due to cleaner flow. 

To allow for the sweep to have room to sweep properly, the outer portion was moved 2 inches outboard. From CFD results I believe this should be ok, but if it isn't ok it will be very obvious. 

To eliminate some flow sep on the down washing flap, I have reduced AoA of the outer portion. Seems the AoA is close to optimized.
