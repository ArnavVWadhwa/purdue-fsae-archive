Purdue Formula SAE : Poly V9

Created by Zhang, Chenkai, last modified on May 02, 2023

Major Changes

  • Giga domain 4
    • x: (-30, 150) m, m
    • y: (-10, 10) m, m
    • z: (0.35, 30) in, m
    • Without domain size change we are looking at 3.70 CL with transition refinement
  • Added transition refinement
    • Another level of transition that surrounds all existing refinements and extends very far downstream
    • Goal is to reduce residual which is high at on the transition from "freestream cells" to "near car cells", where a lot of volume changes happen
    • Poly size at 70%
  • Coarsened max tet size to 250%
  • Coarsened target surface size for freestream and ground refinement 250%
  • Also really need to reduce cell counts because it's taking way too long to solve (8 hours)
