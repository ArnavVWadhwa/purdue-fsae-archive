Clarifications

  1. A weld is defined as a connection from 1 tube to another tube. Ex: Nodes with 3 tubes branching off would include 3 welds. Tabs welded on both sides count as 2 welds and vice versa.
  2. Non-standard jig towers: Jig towers that are not part of the standard set
  3. FRH middle peak was decided to be driven by steering wheel height
