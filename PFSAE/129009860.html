Purdue Formula SAE : 9/14/2019 Grissom - Aluminum Component Temperature Measurement

Created by Unknown User (reyno105), last modified on Sep 16, 2019

Testing day:Â 9-14-19 PF19 Grissom

Thermal stickers made by Omega engineering were applied to various aluminum components to find the temperatures that should be used for determining strength knockdowns during structures design. Images were taken after multiple tests of approximately 1/4 endurance length.

Thermal stickers used:

  • https://www.omega.com/en-us/sensors-and-sensing-equipment/temperature/temperature-indicators/temperature-indicating-labels/8m-labels/p/8MA-140-60-30
  • Part number: 8MA-140/60
  • 140 to 210 degrees F
