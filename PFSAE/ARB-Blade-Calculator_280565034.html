Purdue Formula SAE : ARB Blade Calculator

Created by Kurisko, Keith Gregory on Dec 07, 2023

Steps:

  1. Determine Stiffness Requirements from blade by sizing tubes appropriatelyÂ 
  2. Select adjustability desired based on vehicle dynamic requirements
  3. Assume blade section is rectangular beam in bending and calculate based on geometry the stiffness when vertical and when horizontal to get a rough range
  4. Design blade in CAD and import into Ansys model
  5. Apply 1lbf force at end and parameterize model to model the blade in multiple orientations
  6. Export deflection results into Calculator to get stiffness
  7. Confirm range and use linear interpolation for the exact adjustment angles
  8. Update CAD design and iterate until solution is found
  9. Apply max expected load to Blade to confirm stress of material does not reach ultimate failure most likely due to 3G bump case
