Saturday - Endurance

  • Pre-run:
    • we will not preheat car apart from start to confirm function. expected to heat some during idling, so starting temp should be ok. in general starting temp can be now lower than ambient of ~30C
  • Run order:
    • Connor → Ben
  • Driving notes:
    • if car is on run fans (currently charge at 2 amps)
    • begin temperature mitigation at 118 sustained (you will see a warning from a peak over 120 before this)
      • it is hard to bring temps down but more doable to keep them from rising
      • mitigation begins with using lower rpms via upshift during maneuvers were power not needed (slaloms, corners with subsequent corners)
      • mitigation continues with short shifts
      • mitigation continues with using higher gears at expense of corner exit acceleration
    • If clutch or shifter feels off, get to 3rd and stay.
    • If engine feels off, look for 15% fuel trim at all times and report at driver
  • Driver change notes:
    • keep fans on - keep eye on voltage
    • have wire to short starter solenoid
