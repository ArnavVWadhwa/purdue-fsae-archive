Before modeling the sprocket in NX, the chain must be selected. The main parameter to select the chain is the maximum chain tension under peak acceleration. This can be calculated based on a first gear launch at peak engine torque or by knowing the maximum vehicle acceleration from test data and backing out the chain tension. Both methods result in a similar value.
Try calculating out both methods and compare. Consider why the two methods may not result in the same exact chain tension value.
