Purdue Formula SAE : Hertzian Contact Stress Calculator

Created by Unknown User (dewey2), last modified on Nov 29, 2020

This calculator is for determining the stress present in different contact scenarios:

  • Cylinder (external) on Cylinder (internal/external)
  • Cylinder (external) on Plane
  • Sphere (external) on Sphere (internal/external)
  • Sphere (external) on Plane

This calculator is primarily used for modeling the stress at the CV Tripod - CV insert contact. The primary output is the Von-Mises stress for the contact, but depends on the depth below the surface of the contact.


Comments on using the tool:

  • z Value
    • This is varied until a maximum stress is obtained based on the Sigma_vm values
