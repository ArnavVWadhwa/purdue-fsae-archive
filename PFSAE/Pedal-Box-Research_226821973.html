Forces to Find:

Pressure required for front and rear breaks: Used for specing master cylinders. Rear cylinder will be larger than front. 

Desired Pedal force: Use to determine pedal ratio to design to and for finding master cylinders

Desired Brake bias and break forces. Look at PF20 to get a good idea of forces

Desired Throttle cable travel, for deciding throttle ratio. (Full pedal travel = full cable travel)
