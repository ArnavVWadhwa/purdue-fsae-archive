Purdue Formula SAE : FW-2-06 Y0

Created by Matthew James Lamear on Aug 13, 2023

Simulation ML-048

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Altered strake orientation.

Increased width of first channel to improve vortex generation in this region.

Currently, there is no vortex forming here, but with more space, it's possible that a vortex could form. With an additional vortex, inwash should be reduced, and df from UT should improve.Â 

The 2nd strake was lengthened back to its original length because the inwash was simply too high, instead my strategy is to lengthen the 3rd strake even more to alleviate some of the vortex bursting.

The 4th strake should aid in vortex bursting on the 3rd strake; however, if this does fail it will likely fail on the 3rd strake bursting.

Making the 4th strake any longer will burst the vortex due to the slot gap, so there's not much to play with here.

Turning vanes are closer together now. (1.5 inches closer)

This won't affect Y15 much, but in Y0 this is intended to merge the two vortices into one.

CFD Analysis

Still not close enough to merge, I think increasing the AoA of the inboard mechanism should improve this. (or of course making it closer)

Wake is greatly improved due to the additional width of the 1st channel. Additionally, the counter-rotating vortices eliminate a considerable amount of tire wake.

FW performance comes from the additional area the vortices have to act on, and the reduction of wake inwash.
