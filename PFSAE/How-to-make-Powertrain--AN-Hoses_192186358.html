Will be using aluminum tube for hydramat pick up for cylindrical pf22 fuel tank. Should be single flared at 37 degrees and using support sleeve.

Standard AN sizes for WR450F:


-3 AN for oil pressure and fuel pressure sensors

-6 AN for fuel


We have typically used ultra pro with ultra pro hose ends for fuel

Speed-flex is also used for brake lines.


Ultra-pro could be a good option for fuel, needs to be used with ultra pro hose ends, go with stainless sleeve for because abrasion resistance.

Ultra-flex is the fanciest, cannot make without special tool. More expensive, could maybe get lines custom made at Earl's Indy.
