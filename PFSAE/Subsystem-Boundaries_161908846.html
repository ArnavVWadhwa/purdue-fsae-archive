Purdue Formula SAE : Subsystem Boundaries

Created by Unknown User (xia107) on Nov 15, 2020

A few clarifications on who owns what components within 2021 Suspension


CA and Rockers includes:

  • Spherical, bolt, traffic cones, bolts, washer, and nuts between CA (lower and upper) and uprights
  • Springs and Dampers
  • CA rod ends, bolts, traffic cones, nuts, and washers between CA and Chassis


ARB and Tierods includes:

  • Rod end, traffic cones, bolts, nuts, washers, on both inboard and outboard end of tierods
  • Rod end, traffic cones, bolts, nuts, washers for the droplink from ARB to rocker
    • ARB should design to rocker location

Hubs and Wheels includes:

  • Wheel Bearings
  • Wheels shells
  • Tires


Uprights includes:

  • Camper pieces and shims for CPs
  • Hardware to attach CPs to Uprights


Brake Rotor and Carrier includes:

  • Brake rotors
  • brake rotor carriers
  • Mounting hardware for carrier to hub
