Purdue Formula SAE : Front Wing PF22

Created by Unknown User (agarw162), last modified by Loeb, Chase Ja-ok on Nov 28, 2020

Focus for initial design:

  • Investigate ways to clean up the air downstream.
    • Lowering the inboard flaps.
    • Sweeping/connecting the outboard flaps with the inboard flaps.
    • Cleaning up dirty air near the center main element, near flap gurneys, near the endplate.
  • Issues with lowering/reducing flaps.
    • Modifications that did not reduce overall performance did throw the Front/Rear aero balance off significantly.
    • Mass flow decreased, though we don't yet know what our mass flow target is.
  • Issue with vortices generating near center of main element.
