Purdue Formula SAE : FW-14-22

Created by Zhang, Chenkai, last modified on Jul 29, 2023

Simulation: CZ-051

Baseline: CZ-050

Aero Intent: depower FW strake vorticity to reduce front wing wake especially in yaw 15

Geometry:

Description of the Change

Picture

Justification

Changed the shedge to be more sympathetic 

By angling shedge IBD, local delta P across the shedge is reduced, depowering the structures shed from strakes and reduce wake.

Worry are 1. the angled shedge is biplaning front wing. 2. structures are shed further IBD.

Due to uncertainties with this new geometry, strake gurney is removed. Plus strake gurney do the exact opposite of what this is trying to achieve.
