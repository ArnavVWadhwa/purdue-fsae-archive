Purdue Formula SAE : FW-14-12

Created by Zhang, Chenkai on Jul 04, 2023

Simulation:

Changes/Justifications

Description of the Change

Picture

Justification

More outwash

Yeah, more outwash to suppress separation. Of course this is planform loss, so should be a downforce loss due to this change along (the suppression of separation should not be significant enough to offset this).
Loaded up upper flap

To counter the downforce loss, upper flap inner chord is increased.

CFD Results Analysis

CFD Picture

Description

Weirdly the increase in outwash inwashed the wake more. I think what's happening here is that since an increase in outwash was achieved by a decrease in inner chord on the second element, plus the fact that upper flap chord was increased, there is an increase in the "diffuser angle" of the inner section, leading to blowing up of the wake. This blowing up of wake is of course not good for either front wing or undertray.

Downwind main element and flaps got significantly more load due to increased flap load.

Mysterious rear wing separation is back.
