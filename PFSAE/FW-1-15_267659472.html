Purdue Formula SAE : FW-1-15

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Jul 30, 2023

Simulation ML-034

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Altered main element sweep in the outboard section.

The sweep was altered to improve vortex housing. The sweep allows more room for the vortex to exist outboard and decreases in height quicker than the previous sweep.

This should reduce vortex bursting further, and potentially give the vortex more of a surface to act on earlier. 

Also improved slot gap between main and 2nd elements. 

Definitely worried that the flow sep on the pressure side will be excessive, leading to significant losses. 

Decreased exit curvature of most inboard strake.

Strake was separating on the downwind side due to excessive flow turning.
