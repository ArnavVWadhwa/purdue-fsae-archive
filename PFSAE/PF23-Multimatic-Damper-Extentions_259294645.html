Purdue Formula SAE : PF23 Multimatic Damper Extentions

Created by MacNab, Zachary Robert, last modified on Apr 27, 2023

Extended the multimatic dampers so they're the same as the ohlins, only a 10.7 mm difference


Need grooved sphericals for the end, need retaining method.

This retaining method is called staking, and its where you bend the lip of the race out so it interferes with the housing and locks the spherical in place. 

It requires a special bearing with a lip and staking tool that is priced out below.
