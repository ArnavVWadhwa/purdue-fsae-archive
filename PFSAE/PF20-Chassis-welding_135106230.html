Purdue Formula SAE : PF20 Chassis welding

Created by Unknown User (jpeerbol), last modified on Jan 24, 2020

Tubes from VR3 arrived on 12-6-19.

Total tube weight was 55 lbs.

General welding direction starts at the front bulkhead and works it's way back.


Bulkhead welding started during exam week, 12-10-19.

This year's bulkhead jig was very useful.

All corners were first tacked, then did the diagonal welds, let welds fully cool before moving on to the next weld to try avoid warping the tubes like we got when we rushed the impact attenuator test bulkhead.

C-clamps were used to keep the tubes tight against the plate, u-bolts were used just for positioning, no nuts on the back. Bulkhead was rotated for each weld to make welding more comfortable for the welder and to make sure tubes were not warping.

12/14/19 Saturday - Noon to midnight

Evan and Michael put together forward of front roll hoop

12/15/19 Sunday - Noon

Forward of front roll hoop is mostly together, under welds still need to be closed out once out of jigs. Now waiting on engine mount caps to test fit engine and start welding rear of main roll hoop


12/18/19 - 11 PM all chassis tubes closed out


Filler Rod Tally

Tabs

All tab welding done by JP

Tab welding began January 7, Tuesday before second semester start, much of suspension tabs done during that week, standard 12 hour days. Final tab push during long weekend of MLK day. Lots of welding.

Chassis ready for normalizing January 24
