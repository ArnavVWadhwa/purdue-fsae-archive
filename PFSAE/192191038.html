Actual assembly order we used:

  1. weld steering rack tabs and bearing supports
  2. weld bottom u-joint to bottom tube & middle tube, weld top u-joint to middle tube, weld quick release spline to top tube (make sure the hole for the electronics wire is already drilled in the top tube before welding) - refer to Pegasus U-Joints for info on making sure the u-joints are lined up
  3. pin adapter to rack
  4. place rack in mounts on car, place bottom tube (now connected to both u-joints) in adapter
  5. line up rack in the middle & bottom tube so the u-joint is in line (as in on the top or bottom - both should be the same)
  6. weld the bottom tube to the adapter
  7. line up the steering wheel to be in the middle (refer to spline directions also)
  8. weld the top tube to the top u-joint
