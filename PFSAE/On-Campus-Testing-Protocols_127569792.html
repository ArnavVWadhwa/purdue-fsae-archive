Purdue Formula SAE : On Campus Testing Protocols

Created by Unknown User (jpeerbol), last modified by Rumsey, Helen Nicole on Jul 12, 2023

This information can be found in the SAEleaders drive under 30_Test

Purdue Campus (Grand Prix Track, Ross-Ade North Parking Lot, East of Airport)

  1. Activity Planning Form must be approved by SAO (submit at least 2 weeks prior to planned test date.)
  2. Testing location must be properly reserved (This can only be done by upper leadership)
    • Grand Prix – Event Scheduling via Accruent (& will require a work order to open the gates)
    • Ross-Ade North Parking Lot – Athletics (Katie Egloff <egloff@purdue.edu>)
    • East of Airport (Adam Baxmeyer <abaxmeye@purdue.edu>)
  3. Notify Purdue Police dispatch (non-emergency) prior to testing on day of testing.
  4. Group must adhere to regulations of SAO, International SAE Guidelines, and the University at all times.
    • This includes design, build, and clothing requirements.
  5. Organization members are to sign Purdue University “Hold Harmless” waivers each semester or as a new student joins the club.  These are to be kept on file for two years with the organization. https://boilerlink.purdue.edu/organization/saocampuslife/documents/view/511973
  6. If any injury occurs it must be reported in a timely manner to SAO (765-494-1231). 
  7. Group will work with Emergency Preparedness to formalize adverse weather procedures document.
