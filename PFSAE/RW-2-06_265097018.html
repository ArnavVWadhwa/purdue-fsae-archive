CFD Analysis

CFD PictureDescription

Vertical endplate gurneys are worth 11 pts Cl and 6 pts Cd. Pressure recovery substantially improved. (1st image new, 2nd image old)





(1st image new, 2nd image old) This is inwash, orange is inwash blue is outwash. In the vertical gurney version, the outwash is increased due to the introduction of low pressure. This results in additional upwash, ultimately resulting in higher energy flow that improves pressure recovery which improves overall DF.
