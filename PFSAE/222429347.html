Purdue Formula SAE : Transponder | Documentation

Created by Unknown User (jpeerbol), last modified by Paradise, Hunter on Jun 06, 2023


Notes:

  • Transponder number 3337237
  • This is an older-style transponder, which cannot be directly replaced
  • This style is very desirable. New units require a subscription or a very expensive single purchase and have an internal battery that must be charged
  • This wired version is great because you don't have to worry about charging it


Location:

  • The transponder used to be required to be mounted on the driver's right side of the car forward of the front roll hoop and no more than 60cm above the track.
  • The transponder must have a clear line of sight down to the track. The transponder is marked with the side that needs to face the track.
  • It is also recommended to mount the transponder where it will be protected from obstacles.


Mounting:

  • You should probably design a tab to mount this, it's one of the most important components to be comp spec.
  • The transponder can be mounted with screws, blind rivets, or zip ties.
