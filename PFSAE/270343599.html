Purdue Formula SAE : Efficiency Implementation | LAPSIM

Created by MacNab, Zachary Robert, last modified on Oct 13, 2023

POC: MacNab, Zachary Robert 

PF23's efficiency score was a fat 0, but we got 2nd in endurance. If we traded just a little performance and dropped down in endurance, but actually got an efficiency score, we would have gotten 2nd at least. Time to consider it much more in the design process and evaluate where we went wrong with our previous trade studies.
