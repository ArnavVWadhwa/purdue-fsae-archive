Some papers on modelling brake rotor temps


Papers with thermal conductivity and or specific heat of brake pad materials:

  • Some papers give thermal diffusivity and thermal conductivity. Thermal diffusivity is alpha in the following eq
  • This means ro*c_p = k/alpha so it could still be used to calculate gamma (split into pad vs disc) with the eq from the original paper

  • k: 2.2 at 150 C, 1.9 at 400 C
  • cp: not given

  • k: 1.8 W/m-K at 25C
