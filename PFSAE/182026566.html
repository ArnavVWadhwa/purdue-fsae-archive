Cap Opening Pressure  = "31-33lbs" https://www.summitracing.com/parts/mor-63332/overview


Cap Opening Corresponding Temperature 

  • Atmospheric Pressure : 14.7 psi
  • Gauge Pressure in Cooling System: 32psig
  • Absolute Pressure: 32+14.7 = 46.7psi

At 31psig, water will boil at ~135C

135C runaway point is higher than the engine safe limit. Driver should back down when temps get to ~115C  125C

https://www.engineeringtoolbox.com/boiling-point-water-d_926.html?vA=32&units=P#
