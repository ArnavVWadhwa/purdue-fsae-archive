Component Boundaries

Suspension









X

  • Steering: 

    • Rack selection outward towards upright is owned by SUS. 

    • Rack and clevises CHA
    • Rack bushings and placement SUS

    • Rack mounting tabs, mounting hardware, machined mounts, welded column supports, machined bearing carriers in column supports, bearings, column, u joints, steering wheel, owned by CHA. 

    • CHA owns selection of steering wheel diameter 

    • Purchasing goes with CAD ownership

  • Pedal box:

    • CHA owns all of pedal box. suspension provides required pressures from MCs. CAD crossover is fitting into brake calipers. Purchasing goes with CAD ownership

    • SUS owns brake disc design

  • Actuation:

    • SUS defines suspoints with inputs from CHA (Frame)

    • SUS owns design of control arms, rockers, pushrods, tierods

    • SUS owns tabs and bolts (including rocker bolts) and traffic cones. Purchasing goes with CAD ownership.
    • Manufacturing crossover: CHA owns CA drawings and jigging (of SUS tabs) (remember the CA tabs!). SUS owns making the traffic cones.
  • Rear hubs: rear hub is owned by suspension, cv inserts owned by drivetrain
  • Being within half shaft angle. Drivetrain needs to bother suspension if erroneous
  • Shock Pots:
    • Sensors owned by ELE
    • Mounting and CAD owned by SUS
  • Wheel Speeds:
    • Sensors owned by ELE
    • Mounting and CAD owned by SUS
    • Trigger wheel packaging owned by SUS
    • Trigger wheel high-level design requirements determined by ELE
  • (Tentative) Tire Temperatures:
    • Sensors owned by ELE
    • Mounting and CAD of sensor owned by SUS

  • Aerodynamics / Composites:

    • Carbon manufacturing belongs to AER

Chassis







X

  • Engine:
    • CHA owns engine mounting and jigs, POW owns engine wireframe
    • CHA owns engine packaging clearances with checks from powertrain and chief
  • Peripherals:
    • CHA owns firewall and engine cover
  • Pneumatic Shifter & Clutch: 
    • CHA owns control cables
    • CHA owns until attachments on engine (spline and/or lever)
  • Tabs:
    • All powertrain component tabs designed by POW, jigged and manufactured by CHA with input from POW
  • Dash:
    • Dash layout and placement owned by ELE
    • Tabs owned by ELE, jigging owned by CHA
  • Brake Pressure:
    • Sensors owned by ELE (picking & sourcing)
    • Mounting and CAD owned by CHA
  • Steering Wheel:
    • Controls/button selection/sourcing owned by ELE
    • Mounting and CAD owned by CHA
    • Quick release owned by CHA 
  • Pneumatics:
    • Sensors and wiring owned by ELE
    • Solenoids and pistons owned by CHA
    • Control software owned by ELE under guidance from CHA
  • Brake Overtravel Switch:
    • Switch owned by ELE
    • Mounting and CAD owned by CHA
  • Steering Position:
    • Sensor selection owned by ELE
    • Mounting and CAD owned by CHA
  • (Tentative) Electronic Brake Bias:
    • Motor and controls owned by ELE
    • Mounting, CAD, and bias bar connection owned by CHA
  • Tabs:
    • AER is responsible for designing  components mounting tabs (FW, UT, RW). CHA responsible for jigging and assembling tabs on vehicle
  • Peripherals/Cockpit:
    • CHA owns floor closeouts and body panels (CAD and hardware)
    • AER manufactures all carbon parts (firewall, seat, floor pan, etc)
  • Frame:
    • CHA frame sub system owns front bulk head placement. AER has say in height for AER performance
    • CHA owns tow bar placement AER has say in location relative to rear diffuser.
    • CHA owns MRH design, AER has say in MRH height
Powertrain







X

  • Ignition/Injection:
    • Injectors and spark plugs owned by POW
    • Connectors owned by ELE
    • Coil packs owned by ELE
  • Engine ground bolt and hole owned by ELE
  • MAPT, TPS, OILP, OILT, COOLT, FPTS, KNOCK, LMDA
    • Sensors owned by ELE
    • Mounting and CAD owned by POW
  • LTC and IGN4 owned by ELE
  • Rectifier owned by ELE
  • Starter motor wires and on owned by ELE
  • MoTeC
    • Component incorporation owned by ELE
    • Calibration owned by POW
  • FPUMP, WPUMP, FAN
    • Wiring and switching owned by ELE
    • Component, mounting, and CAD owned by POW
  • Traction and Launch Control:
    • Values from POW
    • Work together to implement into MoTeC
    • Controls owned by ELE
  • Intake CFD - Aero
  • cooling sys cfd - aero
  • Radiator placement - aero
  • Radiator ducts analysis+manufacturing - aero
  • mounting of ducts in cad - aero
  • mounting of ducts physically - pow
