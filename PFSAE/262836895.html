Top Level Goals and Objectives: Pass all rules

Steering Wheel Singhal, Ria 

  • Allow the driver to comfortably steer 
  • Contain all required buttons and devices within safe reach

Steering Column Cole Henry Kniesly 

  • Support the steering wheel and position it to be comfortable for the driver to use
  • Have enough support to reduce play and bending in column to allow for better driver control

Steering Rack Jiang, Zhao Singhal, Ria

  • Transmit rotational force from steering column into linear motion through the tie rods
  • Lighter and better than NARRco rack
