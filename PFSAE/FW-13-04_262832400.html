Purdue Formula SAE : FW-13-04

Created by Zhang, Chenkai, last modified on May 30, 2023

Simulation: CZ-009

Changes/Justifications

Description of the Change

Picture

Justification

Added a flap to tire squirt suppressor

Did someone say downwash?

On top of that also shedding two vortices instead of one, so the additional outwash should help.

Additional pressurization underneath should also help in controlling front wing wake.

The amount of lift generated by a flapped setup is concerning though. We will see.

CFD Results Analysis

CFD Picture

Description

Significant lift produced. Front wing lost 7 points of Cl. Also flow alignment issue on the leading edge causing losses and additional suction.

The good old co rotating vortex merging. Losses increases with vortex merging.

A ton of outwash, really good for undertray performance.

The merged vortex pair smacks right into the sidepod leading edge, resulting in a massive separation across the side of the sidepod.

Sidepod top flow attaches much better with increased downwash. But flow is separating inside the sidepod now.
