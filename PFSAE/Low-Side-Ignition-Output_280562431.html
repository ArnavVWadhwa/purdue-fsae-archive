Purdue Formula SAE : Low Side Ignition Output

Created by Paradise, Hunter on Nov 24, 2023
  • The mnemonic within Build and Tune is IGN_LS.
  • 2 Amp open collector outputs (switch to BAT_NEG).
  • 0-40mA programmable current source (8 bit).
  • All IGN_LS outputs are driven from individual current sources. All current sources are enabled together.

  • No internal recirculation circuitry. Cam solenoids require an external recirculation diode.
  • Pin voltage is monitored by a 12-bit A/D converter (per output).
  • Can also be used as a PWM aux output.
  • 24bit timers 0.1usec resolution.
  • Max voltage 40V.
  • Max current 3.5 Amps minimum.
  • RMS current 2 Amps.
  • Either 8 or 12 Low Side Ignition pins per device - hardware dependant.
