Email Invite

The FSAE team will be hosting a design review on Saturday, October 15th starting at 1pm EST and I would like to extend an invite out to you.

The design review will be held through Microsoft Teams and for those of you who would like to attend in-person, our team will be holding the review in the Purdue Mechanical Engineering Building, Room 1185. We will be sectioning out times throughout the day for each sub-team to present so feel free to drop-in for any of the presentations that you would be interested in. 


The schedule will be:

1pm - 1:30pm: Intro+ Vehicle Architecture

1:30pm - 2:30pm: Suspension + Brakes

2:30pm - 3:30pm: Chassis + Cockpit/Controls

3:30pm - 4:30pm: Aerodynamics

4:30pm - 5:30pm: Powertrain

5:30pm - 6pm: Systems Integration

6pm - 6:30: Closing Remarks


All times are in Eastern time and maybe be slightly shorter or longer depending on how the duration of the presentation/feedback. 

If you are interested and available, please reply to me with the presentations you are available for. 


Thank You!
