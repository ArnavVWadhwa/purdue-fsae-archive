Pros

  • Linear/Customizable throttle maps
  • Automatic throttle blips on downshift
  • Slightly improved traction control
  • Gas pedal sensitivity
  • Adjustable Idle


Cons

  • Weight
  • Cost
  • Reliability/Extra Failure modes
  • Notice of Intent and comprehensive FMEA
  • Requires redundant sensors
  • Tech inspection difficulty
