Purdue Formula SAE : FW-1-09 Y0

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Jul 23, 2023

Simulation ML-028

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Straightened outer sweep, and added gurney for the full sweep.

The gurney should produce more downforce which is currently an issue, without affecting other component performances (potentially additional upwash could hurt UT/RW but we'll see)

Straightening the outer sweep should fix the issue with flow bellowing over at the end of the endplate since the flow is more aligned to the geometry. Potentially another way to fix this is to increase the outwashing sweep, since in Y0 the flow is partially inwashing due to the proximity to the tire.
