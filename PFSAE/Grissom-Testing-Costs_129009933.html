A UHaul box truck would be $90 for 3 days plus $0.99 per mile. Grissom for two days is about 250 miles.


Renting a truck through Purdue and using the comp trailer is the best way to travel to Grissom. The car is safer, you have more space for tools and it is easier and cheaper than renting from UHaul.

This does require slightly more planning because you need to reserve the truck rental through SAO when submitting your APF 2 weeks+ before the testing event.
