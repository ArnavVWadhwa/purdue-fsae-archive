Created by Rumsey, Helen Nicole, last modified on Oct 09, 2023

Total number of shifts = 648 (including getting up to the line)

The tank pressure doesn't seem to drop until part way through because the pressure sensor isn't rated to the 4500 psi the tank can be filled to, so the sensor doesn't accurately start reading the tank pressure until the tank pressure is around 3300 psi.


Quick maths using slope gives approximate pressure drop per shift = 3.147 psi/shock
