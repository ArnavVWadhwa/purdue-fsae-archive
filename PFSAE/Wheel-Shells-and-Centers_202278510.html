Purdue Formula SAE : Wheel Shells and Centers

Created by Unknown User (xia107), last modified on Mar 23, 2022

Assembly

Key thing to note here is sealing the area between the two wheel shells to seal the air. For PF22 wheels, we used Permatex Ultra Grey Gasket Maker.

Apply a ring (roughly 0.1-0.25" thick) around the contact area and completely around the bolt holes.

Assembly the inner shell, outer shell, and center. You may have to hand tighten the bolts initially, then torque to spec later, but refer to the directions on Gasket Maker.


Drill a hole on the outer shell for the valve stem. Make sure the stem can sit completely on a flat face so it can seal properly, but also make sure it doesn't interfere with the tire or inner wheel shell.

Use the valve stem puller tool to thread onto and pull the valve stem through the shell and seat it.
