Purdue Formula SAE : FW-14-21

Created by Zhang, Chenkai on Jul 12, 2023

Simulation: CZ-050

Changes/Justifications

Description of the Change

Picture

Justification

Tire squirt suppressor optimization

Reduced angle of attack globally by 5 deg to control separation (it's not enough, I don't think, but the limiting factor is pressure side separation on the other side), and hopefully get rid some of the suction peaks formed.

Increasing first element leading edge radius to control separation. 

Flow conditioner optimization

Moved flow conditioner up to prevent front wing inner vortex form smashing into it and creating limit. Rather this vortex should go under it now and reduce overall lift generated.

Reduced angle of attack to prevent pressure side separation.

Reduced flow conditioner span to reduce overall lift generated while imposing minimal impact on rear wing. Since the tip vortex gets severely outwashed (at least in yaw 0), moving the vortex closer to the rear wing by physically reducing span may offset the loss of circulation.
