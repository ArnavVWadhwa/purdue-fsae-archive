Purdue Formula SAE : RW-2-03

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Jul 02, 2023

Simulation ML-014

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Increased AoA of the main element, increased height of arrangement, altered airfoil arrangement to work with AoA change.

Increasing AoA of the main element to garner increased DF. The final element decreased camber to aid in flow sep issues. The second element was slightly altered to improve pressure recovery.

CFD Analysis

CFD PictureDescription

Pressure is too low on the final element and the 2nd element is at too high of an AoA to produce substantial DF. Stagnation point is suboptimal too.

BL on the main element is getting a bit too large at the TE. This results in a considerable amount of dirty flow entering the lower surface of the next element. 2nd Element is also having the same problem.
