Purdue Formula SAE : 2022.04.03 - JP & Dom endurance driving comparison

Created by Unknown User (jpeerbol), last modified on Apr 04, 2022

JP drove the first stint

Muffler blew two holes, holes were created during lap 4, JP finished the timed lap. Stopped to check what the issue was and continued driving.

Differential was leaking after JP's stint, decided to pause and fix the diff. This took multiple hours so track conditions changed slightly between Dom and JP's stints, but ambient temperate did not change, track temp might have changed slightly due to the sun going down.


Lap analysis

JP's fastest lap was lap 1 so tire temps seemed to not be an issue, looking at the data, it looks like it took about half a lap to get the tires to temperature.

JP stalled the car 3 times in the final hairpin, due to very hard braking and fully locking the rear tires and stalling the engine. Lost about 7 seconds for the first stall, 13 seconds for the second stall, and 23 seconds for the 3rd stall.

Fastest lap was the first lap with a 1:10.695, second fastest was the 9th lap (10 in the data due to pausing for muffler) with a 1:10.806. Eclectic time of 1:08.014, with a rolling minimum of 1:09.910. Average (no stall) lap time of 1:11.848
