Justification

Bargeboard 

Increase height on top to reduce vortex induced load on top of SPOD.

Also reduce losses on top of SPOD going into SW.

Due to the increase in distance between the vortex and the surface, scrub is reduced, further reducing losses.

OBS leading edge realignment to reduce losses.

MS backed off to reduce separation.

Added IBS to support MS, with the IBS even more backed off.

HHD length increase to fit the third strake.
