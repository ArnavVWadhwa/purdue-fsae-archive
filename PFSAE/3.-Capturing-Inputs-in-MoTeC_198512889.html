Purdue Formula SAE : 3. Capturing Inputs in MoTeC

Created by Unknown User (dnocon), last modified on Dec 02, 2021

The cam and crank sensors raw signal is not recorded and saved by MoTeC. Recording these waveforms can be extremely helpful when setting up sensor parameters.

In M1: Online → Capture Inputs

Note when using this feature to get cam and crank sensors, you will need to have the engine cranking before you click go. 


An example of capturing the WR450F Crank position Mag pickup waveform:

We were able to determine not only what the waveform looked like, but it was also cool to see the shape of the waveform squash during different strokes of the engine when the crank rotated slower.
