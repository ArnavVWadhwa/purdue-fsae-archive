Created by Unknown User (agarw162), last modified by Unknown User (green338) on Oct 04, 2019

Basic Introduction:

Composites are any non-homogeneous material. On FSAE composites we are dealing only with FRP composites (fiber reinforced polymers). Most of our fibers are carbon fiber (CFRP - carbon fiber reinforced polymers), although occasionally we will do layups with fiberglass or kevlar. These are typically only for very specific uses - either to cut cost with fiberglass, or improve abrasion resistance with kevlar.
