BOM/Budget Stallings, Simon Tibor 

  • System owners are responsible for keeping BOM up to date, Stallings, Simon Tibor is responsible for ensuring spending requests are included on the BOM

  • Stallings, Simon Tibor is responsible for making sure $5000 remain in the formula account at all times. reimbursement/spending will be frozen when the account reaches $5000
  • Stallings, Simon Tibor is responsible for maintain "budget" sheet
