Notes for Max:

  • Cut width and length to rough size using drop bandsaw
  • Use endmill to bring sides to correct size over sizing a couple thou is better than undersizing
  • Once squared up use a flycutter to bring height of block down to 1" exactly, roughly 30 thou passes until ~1.020" then smaller passes
  • Check with John about fixture plate bolt size to drill respective holes (center drill before drilling holes)

Sacrificial Block

CAM: 

  • Brake and Accelerator Pedals

Throttle Pedal:

  • Create Sacrificial block to bolt to with 2 respective holes
  • Stock: 10.400" x 0.900" x 1.200"
  • Drill 2 holes on the side to fasten to sacrificial block
  • CNC two top pockets and outside contour
  • Flip and CNC other two pockets
  • Drill Spring and Rivet holes
