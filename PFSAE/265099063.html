Notes:

  • Rules minimum of 2 in (50.8 mm) of shock travel
    • Nothing we have found suits the shocks perfectly, we operate on the assumption that we will rarely, if ever, see full compression but will see full extension
  • Choose the minimum size that will comply with desired shock travel for the year
  • Autosport is generally a poor idea given how often these are replaced
  • Active Sensors must be emailed to make a purchase (sales@activesensors.com)
    • They will only supply FSAE with general or high-performance potentiometers
