Description

Outwash on the downwind side works against inwash to prevent the intended outer side tunnel vortex from forming. Reducing downforce since the diffuser angle on this part is very low.

Upwind side inner side tunnel is totally separation (does attach further downstream).

This is the opposite of rear tire wake management. Though this happens on the downwind side so that's a little better.

Not enough angle on the leading edge of the undertray. To make it work across all yaw angles, leading edge would be necessary.
