Purdue Formula SAE : FW-1-05 Y0

Created by Matthew James Lamear on Jul 17, 2023

Simulation ML-024

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Altered sweep to allow for AoA decrease of second element.

Reducing diffuser height by reducing AoA of second element allows for the vortices to not blow up. This results in cleaner flow all around. At the end of the 2nd element, two other elements are seen at very high AoA. This allows for rapid diffusion of the flow to ultimately produce more DF but provide dirty flow to the tires, depressurizing the tires.


The inner section massively outwashes, hopeful that this improves flow quality to the UT.

CFD Analysis

CFD PictureDescription

Mid endplate is super draggy in Y0 too. Need to increase SA for additional DF in yaw 0, currently losing 10 points to FW performance compared to CZ 43. The other points are coming from UT performance, this can likely be improved by eliminating the endplate, which eliminates the in-washing vortex, and the low-pressure region which is promoting inwash.

Flow separates in Y0 and Y15 at the upper surface of the main element. I'm not too concerned about this at the moment, can optimize this later. 

The final element is also separating. Can just reduce AoA here for additional drag reduction.
