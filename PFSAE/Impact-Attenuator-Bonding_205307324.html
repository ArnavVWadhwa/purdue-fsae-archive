Purdue Formula SAE : Impact Attenuator Bonding

Created by Rumsey, Helen Nicole on Jan 31, 2022

To bond the the IA w used an epoxy, specifically the one pictured below.

We mapped out where the center of the plate would be and applied a liberal amount of epoxy. We want enough epoxy so that when the honeycomb is pressed on to the plate, some epoxy squeezes out the edges to show the judges the honeycomb is fully bonded.
