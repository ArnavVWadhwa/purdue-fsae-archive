Purdue Formula SAE : FW-1-21

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Aug 05, 2023

Simulation ML-040

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Endplates altered to a single sweep.

This design should reduce drag, especially the separation from the main element. 

Due to the sweep, the low-pressure region generated by the endplate portion should be lower resulting in more vorticity.
