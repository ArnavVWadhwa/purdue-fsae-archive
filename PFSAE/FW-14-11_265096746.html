Purdue Formula SAE : FW-14-11

Created by Zhang, Chenkai on Jul 04, 2023

Simulation: CZ-040

Changes/Justifications

Description of the Change

Picture

Justification

Added outwashing winglets

These winglets are small and on the last element and in front of tires, which means they will have will small impacts when it comes to front wing downforce loss.

These winglets are designed to shed vortices that is locally outwashing on the bottom to help suppress strake separation.
