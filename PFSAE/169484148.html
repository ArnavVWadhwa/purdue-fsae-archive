Team goals with Cockpit, Controls, Brakes, Safety subgoals

  • Safety:
    • All safety critical components meet team safety factor and analysis requirements (level 2)
      • Stress under ult / 1.4
    • Testing performed where analysis not met before driving
    • Additional safety concerns than addressed in rules considered
  • Rules compliance
  • Reliability + Serviceability:
    • Since these systems are safety critical, reliability = safety
  • Driver Confidence:
    • Good visibility - driver can see road ahead and edges of vehicles:
      • line of sight to front wing end plates
    • Responsive forces - controls inform driver of vehicles closeness to performance limits and are easy to modulate
      • steering force > 10 Nm
    • Non-fatiguing forces - driver can complete full endurance event without being affected by muscle fatigue
      • steering force < 30 Nm
    • Comfort - driver not distracted by pain or needing to support self
    • Driver trusts safety and reliability of car
