Purdue Formula SAE : FW-1-01

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Jul 11, 2023

Simulation ML-020

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Increased chord of squirt suppressor. 
This should result in the vortex forming farther outboard to attempt to outwash the blown-up vortices more. 
Angled outer section and up washing exit.

Angled outer section should result in further flow attachment in yaw resulting in the vortex forming higher in the tunnel reducing inwash longer.

The upwashing exit utilizes a similar idea to the rear wing where it is used as a dive plane. This also improves outwash by producing a low pressure region on exit upon flow sep.

CFD Analysis

CFD PictureDescription

Vortex tunnels failed to capture the vortex any more than the last iteration, and the up washing section ended up being useless because the vortex that is captured blows up in the tunnel resulting in no gains.

Increasing the chord led to no value since the edge of the windward side is in the blown vortex which resulted in very little vorticity being produced.
