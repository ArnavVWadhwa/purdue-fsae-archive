Purdue Formula SAE : Supply Voltage Outputs

Created by Paradise, Hunter on Nov 24, 2023

Care should be taken in wiring harness design to ensure that no current paths are routed to Sensor Zero Volt pins.

  • Supply and zero-volt outputs for 5V and 6V3 Sensor supplies.
  • Zero-volt outputs are FET-driven earths.
    • Not suitable for scope probes or other earths.
    • Do not provide an earth path when the ECU is not powered.
