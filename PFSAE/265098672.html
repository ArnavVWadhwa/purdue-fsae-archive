Purdue Formula SAE : Uprights PF24 | Design

Created by Dunlap, Galen Mathew, last modified by Morgan, Cameron Ronald on Oct 11, 2023

PF24 Improvement Goals:

  • Reduce weight from PF23 by 5% (subject to change)
  • Improve adjustment capabilities
    • look into better camber adjustment systems other than shims
    • create easer tool access for actuation joints
    • change clearance between camber pieces and brakes so that the brakes do not have to be removed to adjust camber
  • Refine ANSYS models 
    • last year slop developed between the upright and the hubs 
      • investigate if this was a result of yielding, a compliance issue, or even if it was a problem with the uprights
      • better models of forces and geometry to help reduce weight 
  • Improve camber pieces 
    • last year, issues with camber pieces led to incorrect camber angles
      • refine the tolerances between the interface of camber pieces and uprights
      • continue to improve on 5-axis machining Troy started last year
