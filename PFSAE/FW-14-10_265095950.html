Changes/Justifications

Description of the Change

Picture

Justification

Tip reprofile

Reprofiled tip to be continuously outwashing. The effect at the very tips should propagate inboard, helping to control junction losses at the strake. The will effect is somewhat dampened by the loss of local outwash angle at the inner strake though. 

This geometry also means less planform area, and we will see how much downforce that losses. But if it solves the issue of junction separation, the loss of planform will be greatly outweighed

Unloaded tip airfoil 

Reduced suction side camber on this to unload the tips, helping control junction losses and tip vortex break down.
