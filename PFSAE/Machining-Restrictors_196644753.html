Purdue Formula SAE : Machining Restrictors

Created by Unknown User (jpeerbol), last modified on Nov 18, 2021

The intake restrictor is a critical rules mandated part that is used to limit the amount of power cars can make at competition. 

Read the rules here to see mandated size.


Here is a step file of the restrictor that is designed for our car this year. It is to be made from 6061 aluminum.

PF22_POW_6969_ restrictor.stp


The restrictors need to be cnc lathed at the BIDC. Start by going through the getting started on the BIDC's website https://www.purdue.edu/bidc/ .

We have 6061 material for when you start machining, after you get the cam approved.
