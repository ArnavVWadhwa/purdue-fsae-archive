Purdue Formula SAE : Ergo Driver Notes 2020

Created by Unknown User (reyno105), last modified on Nov 13, 2020
  • Complaints:
    • Things that hurt: 
      • Shoulder blades pressed into seat - foam/seat inserts
      • Bruises from left knee hitting clutch shifter assembly and right knee hitting - padding in nose
      • Bruises from right knee hitting front roll hoop (i think) -padding in nose
      • Bruises on inside of both legs from hitting steering column (only affect taller drivers that have more knee bend) - pool noodle on the steering column
      • Right elbow (for connor) hitting radiator tab that sticks into cockpit - dont put tabs into the cockpit
    • Lack of constraint on body motion:
      • seat needs inserts for side motion - foam/seat inserts + could also add support at bottom of seat
      • seat needs to be stronger to stop lateral motion - do the coMposItE STrucTurES AnALYSiS 
      • feet slipped out of foot tray - heal cutouts could be deeper
      • top of foot moves - brake + throttle pedal should both get box thing to keep ball of foot centered (current one of these for throttle pedal is too low, doesn't support at ball of your foot)
    • Cockpit controls:
      • could use more room for elbows while steering - wider chassis (main roll hoop)
      • Steering seems to have a little "bind" at ~45 degrees
    • Things breaking:
      • Seat needs properly designed hard points
      • bearing near column works it's way out. - add shaft collar
