Bolt Sizing

Assume all fastening is in single-sided shear, with applied force of 200 lbs.

F.S. of 1.1, Assume shear strength = 60% tensile.

Tensile Yield Stress of 4130≈ 143,000 psi

Shear Y.S. = 143,000 psi * .63 * (1/1.1) = 8190 psi

Force / Shear = Area

200 lbs / 8190 psi = .0244 in ^2

∴ Fitting Diameter = .176 inches or greater = 4.47 mm

Use NAS6203
