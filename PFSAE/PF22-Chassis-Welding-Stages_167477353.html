Purdue Formula SAE : PF22 Chassis Welding Stages

Created by Unknown User (hammerly), last modified on Oct 16, 2021

Welding Operations Recommendations for the 22 car. Based on experience with PF20

DO THIS FIRST: "dry" fit the entire tube and jig assembly prior to even turning on the welder. make sure everything goes together well and there are no issues with clearances between tubes and jigs (if there are just use the angle grinder on the jig)


Weld OperationTubes AddedJig Setup
Weld Op1: MRH, FRH, BH and the tubes connecting these on the bottom of the car should be welded first (only have jigs for these tubes in place)

Weld Op2: next "level" up of tubes can be welded next

Weld Op3: more tubes. the rear should be welded in going towards the back of the car

Weld Op4: keep building tubes upÂ 

Weld Op5: final few tubes, likely all hand placed
