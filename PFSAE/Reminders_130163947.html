Rear LCA AFT tabs decreased spacing by 1/16" for upper and lower. so TC size will change. Has not been modified in CAD

The rear left upright is 7050-t76511 4" rather than 7055-t74511 3". This is amounts to a 10 ksi reduction in both yield and ultimate strength. The part still holds positive Ultimate margins with 1.4 safety factor and 1.15 uncertainty factor. This is with a linear model, so using a point stress is quite conservative. The only load case that will drive yield at the critical location is launch, so if yield occurs there will not be cyclic-reversed yielding. As such the fatigue is still of minimal concern. The loads in the launch case are some of the best understood due to tabulation of acceleration data from launches on a highly grippy surface (asphalt at FCA's testing grounds). Accordingly, the 1.15 uncertainty factor is likely conservative. Finally, if failure were to occur, it would be during a launch when speeds are low, so risk of driver injury is small.
