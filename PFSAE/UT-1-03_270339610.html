Purdue Formula SAE : UT-1-03

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Aug 31, 2023

Simulation ML-059

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Inlet side channel decreased overall width, but more importantly the change in width is much more gradual.

With the improvement to the gradual sweep, the vortex should develop as expected, and hopefully survive the length of the channel.

It's very likely the inwash is still too high but this should be a step in the right direction.

Strake leading edges have been curved.

This should just improve yaw sensitivity marginally, but it will work across all yaw angles.
