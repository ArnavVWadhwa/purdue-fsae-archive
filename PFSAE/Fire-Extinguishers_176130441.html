Purdue Formula SAE : Fire Extinguishers

Created by Unknown User (reyno105) on Apr 02, 2021

The fire extinguisher requirements from the rules (2021)

 

I bought 2x fire extinguishers that meet rules:

  • 2.5 lb capacity (>2)
  • ABC II Dry Chemical (meets requirement for dry chemical / powder)
  • Does have gauge showing charge as required
  • Rating: 1A 10BC (meets min rating)


 


Where to purchase:

  • Purchased from Ace Fire Protection in Lafayette, can recharge there
  • Cost was ~$30 each for used (still carries inspection from Ace Fire)
  • You should be able to recharge them at ace if they get used


Notes:

  • The number is the size of fire that can extinguished
  • The letter is the type of fire:
    • So getting an A extinguisher adds ability to put out fires of typical office flammables (or old ones where just 10BC)
  • Reuse
    • If the background of the dial is white, it's 1x use - cant be recharged
    • If the background of the dial is all red, it can be recharged.
