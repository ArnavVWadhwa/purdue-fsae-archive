Design Scoring Sheet

SYSTEMS MANAGEMENT & INTEGRATION (0-20pts)

Are the vehicle and team well integrated? Do the electronic/electrical systems effectively support performance? Is the team progressive, well-balanced, and capable of repeating their efforts? In addition to electrical systems, this category considers the overall vehicle strategy and approach. This is inclusive of architectural trade-offs, packaging, instrumentation, and team development/management. 

  • Are accessory devices (ECU, Data, Comm. Equip., Control system components) complementary, efficiently packaged, and adequately protected? Do they perform as a system?

  • Do test equipment/data systems effectively support vehicle development? Or just look good?

  • Is data collected and utilized during the competition to improve performance? If so how?  

  • Has wiring been correctly selected, safely routed, color-coded, and marked for function?

  • Can the team produce wiring, plumbing, and sub-system schematics for the vehicle?

  • Is the team’s approach to each system congruent with the overall goals? Is the integrated vehicle better than just the sum of its parts?

  • Has the team proven its fluency with simulation and advanced analysis techniques?

  • Has Project Management been a priority for the team? Can they show detailed schedules?

  • Can the team provide engineering and build documentation?

  • Have the organizational skills of the team been well demonstrated?

  • Has the team really read the rulebook?

    1. Crank
    2. Check Sync
    3. Reconnect Fuel Injector
  4.  Roll Car Outside + Pull E-stop
    1. Check Fuel Pressure
    2. Crank and Start
    3. Monitor Engine Sensors
    4. Have Powertrain Visually Inspect System
    5. Monitor Vehicle Sensors
  5. Pull Data
    1. Confirm Logging from All Sensors in i2

Electronics Minimum Viable Product

General / High Level

  • Pass rules
  • Consistently reliable system throughout vehicle lifespan
  • All design and manufacturing deadlines hit


Electronics / Wiring

  • Full system CAD integrated into the vehicle model
  • Fully documented harness, including schematics, current draws, the voltage drop across each wire, and all measured currents & voltage at each sensor/module.
  • Sealed and abrasion-resistant harness
  • High-quality crimps for every connector


Software / Firmware

  • Fully documented code
  • Complete CAN block architecture documentation
  • Easy-to-read data analysis suite through i2 pro and external scripts
