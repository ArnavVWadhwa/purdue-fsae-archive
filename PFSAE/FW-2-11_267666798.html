Purdue Formula SAE : FW-2-11

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Aug 20, 2023

Simulation ML-053

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Altered inner diveplane.

Should spool the vortex closer to the vortex we want to outwash. This should result in better RW performance.
Tire squirt suppressor moved forward 1 inch.

Potentially can move it closer, just worried about upwash from the FW interfering with attachment/vortex upwashed. 

Currently in Y15 the suppressor isn't really doing anything since the wake takes it over. Thus, moving it forward should give it enough flow to at least produce one vortex which should see better UT performance.

CFD Analysis


Turning vane vortex position didn't end up changing due to this diveplane, likely the low-pressure region increased resulting in less of a vortex forming that co-rotates the turning vane vortex outboard.

Moving the squirt suppressor closer to the FW hurt performance by roughly a point and added about a point and a half to UT performance. I don't really like that change, and I think its much more important to just manage inwash instead because as one can see on the right, too much inwash and the vortex is much too overpowered.
