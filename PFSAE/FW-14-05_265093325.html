Changes/Justifications

Description of the Change

Picture

Justification

Reprofiled strakes and added a new strake

Back to outwashing strakes because outwash make sense for front tire wake geometry. It's profiled to for good flow alignment to limit strength of vortex shed across the strakes, which again limits front wing wake and improves undertray performance. 

Additional strake discretize the vortices more. And with the additional strake near wing tip, the pressurization across it should also help stabilize VC vortex. 

CFD Results Analysis

CFD Picture

Description

Although it's good for high wake outwash, low wake is just too vortex dominated. Any curve generates the vortex aka losses, and the vortex spins in the wrong direction. Need better way for outwash here.
