Purdue Formula SAE : Materials Properties and Factor of Safety

Created by Cole Henry Kniesly, last modified on Jan 04, 2023

Factors of Safety:

  • Brake Pedal

    • In general, a F.S. of 1 was used because the test force is significantly higher than the expected load force. The force used to test was a 450lbf (2000N) at the top of the brake pedal
  • Throttle Pedal
    • In general, a F.S. of 1 was used because the test force is significantly higher than the expected load force. The force used to test was a 150lbf at the top of the throttle pedal
  • Pedal Box
    • A F.S. of 1 was used because the test force is significantly higher than the expected load force. The force used to test was a 450lbf at the top of the brake pedal
    • The throttle pedal used a 150lb load at the top of the throttle pedal. (This load case is not limiting for the pedal box, but we calculated it just in case.)

Materials Used:

  • Aluminum (7055)
  • Steel (4130)
