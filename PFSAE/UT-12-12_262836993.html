Description of the Change

Justification

New ground clearance rule

Undertray skirt edges are now 1.1 in based on new roll gradient target. Actual height of undertray and side tunnel surfaces are unchanged since even at 1.5 in, they are at ride height sensitivity limits. However, reduce ride height for strakes limits inwash better. The vortex shed of the strakes will be more lossy as well - that's not good.Â 
Increased strake length

Increased strake length by 10 in to better isolate inwash.

Description

With reduced strake ride height, the vortex in the outwash region bursts.

Adding strake in this region should improve this.

Better front wing flow conditioning and low wake outwash will also improve this the bursting is caused by ingestion of tire wake.
