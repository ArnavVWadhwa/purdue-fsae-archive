Purdue Formula SAE : Finding Source Definition of Linked Body

Created by Unknown User (xia107) on Aug 29, 2021

With the CAD structure implemented on our team, it is often that we are viewing assemblies that contain nothing by .prt files with only a linked body. Usually none of those .prt files actually contain the geometry defining the component. Those fundamental definitions are usually rooted in larger "Layout" files.


To find original source .prt in which geometry is defined:

  1. Open Part Navigator Tree and find the Linked Body corresponding to the one you are inquiring about. Right click and select Properties.
  2. In the pop-up window, go to the General and click on the circled i.
  3. Here you can find the .prt and the feature in that .prt which defines your linked body
