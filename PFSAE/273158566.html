Purpose

The purpose of IDR is to get as many eyes on our design as possible. Ultimately, IDR is an amazing resource for us to ask questions. Team debates and such can be asked at design review, and a professional opinion can be gained. In general, keep in mind what you exact have questions on, as IDR's purpose is to get these questions answered.
