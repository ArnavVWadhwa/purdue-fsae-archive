Purdue Formula SAE : 1. Powertrain Concerns

Created by Unknown User (hammerly), last modified on Dec 13, 2022

Initial Concerns

  •  Reliability
    • Cycle testing on dyno
  • Clutch Engagement
  • Getting into neutral
    • secondary piston
    • secondary regulator
    • tuning pressure of clutch actuation via solenoid
      • lighter than adding another piston
  • Knowing shift actuation times - determine ignition cut timing
  • Downshifting protection - also applicable for upshifts
    • too quickly - electronic or mechanical control
      • "very okay with that"
    • throttle blipping
      • vary levels of clutch engagement for this
  • Manual option designed and tested slightly on the car – matching last years for minimum effort / maximum reliability
    • build in tabs for manual option for testing
  • Automatic shift under worst case scenarios
    • low pressure in bottle - shift to 2nd
    • if bottle pressure is dropping too quickly, warning light

Williams, Carson Kenneth  needs to think about fuel cut and ignition cut.

DFMEA Follow-Up

A Design Failure Mode and Effect Analysis was conducted to think through any possible failure modes for the system, evaluate how severe each mode may be, and brainstorm any possible countermeasures to be implemented via hardware or software with Skrzyszowski, Mateusz Williams, Carson Kenneth .
