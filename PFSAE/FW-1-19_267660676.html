Purdue Formula SAE : FW-1-19

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Aug 05, 2023

Simulation ML-038

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification

Reduced AoA on main element of upper flap. 

Moved upper flap back.

Swept the downwashing flap more than previously.

The reduction in AoA should aid in separation of the main element flap. 

Moving the upper flap back should aid in LE attachment in yaw. 

Sweeping the downwashing flap should result in a stronger vortex at the tip and should produce less lift.

Increased length of inner strake.

This should improve downforce in Y0 by increasing the strength of the inner vortex; however, I am worried about Y15 vortex overpowering and bursting.
