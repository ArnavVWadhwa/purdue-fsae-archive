Comments:

PDR Comments: 

  • Ya done good
  • do motion study to check side to side motion of master cylinders
  • PF20 line pressure justification (need to also verify rest of braking components) (chassis owns up to the calipers) 
  • Throttle stop tab: more stiffness from gussets than from making the tab thicker (also thick stuff is hard to weld)
  • Closed top of toe tray: purpose: keep foot from bouncing out (probably not needed)
  • Material selection: mild steel tube won't work cause you can't weld it to the 4130 (generally use 4130 for any welded steel structures)
  • Spring holder: use the castle nut set up cause you don't want to squish it (no preload)
  • do motion study check to see if the master cylinder bolts can be removed without removing the pedal box
  • bot switch may be too close (weird thing with master cylinder moving after being held?) (extend the tab to let you bend the tab to move the switch back)
    • or maybe move it back and add something else to adjust when it hits
  • Foot tray design: 
    • may limit the adjustment range
