Preflight:

General:

  • Clean Debris off Track

Suspension:

  • Check Brake Fluid and leaks in circuit
  • Check Tire Pressures
  • Deflate tires to 10 PSI (cold)
  • Thorough Bolt check, with torque stripes
  • Compliance check including lifting vehicle to check for vertical compliance at LCA
  • Brake Pad Check
  • Make sure we have pages in data binder
  • Check hall sensor mounting / cable routing
  •  
  • Real Springs on Car (Post dyno specific)
  • Rear ARB reattached (Post dyno specific)
  • Tires are ok (Post dyno specific)

Powertrain:

Intake

  • Intake / throttle mounted securely

Exhaust

  • Exhaust / muffler mounted securely
  • Verify powertrain and non-powertrain components are safe from exhaust heat 

Fuel

  • Fuel system mounted securely, no leaks 

Cooling

  • Pressure Test Cooling System to 15PSI and check for leaks, mounted securely

Oil

  • Verify Oil System is filled and no visible leaks

Drivetrain

  • Drivetrain mounted correctly, differential filled, CV joints "gooked" up 
  • Drivetrain chain appropriate slack (may require tensioning)

Engine

  • Engine Cam Position Sensor Mounted Correctly
  • Cycle Lock

SIQ:

  • Crank car in shop, no fuel injector
  • Fire car outside
  • Check sensors
  • Check harness mounting, zip tie up loose wire
  • Check throttle position sensor

Aero:

  • Check seat connection
  • Check all bolts on endplates
  • Check swan and tierod bolts 
  • Check all jam nuts
  • Re-tape any sharp edges

Chassis:

Shifter:

  • check all shifter bolts
  • check push pull cable and clutch cable washers
  • shift up & down to check hard stops/ push pull cable
  • check clutch cable tension

Steering:

  • check steering rack pins (make sure it's not walking out)
  • check clevis bolts (make sure safety wire is still loose)
  • check rack mount bolts

Pedal Box:

  • check throttle pedal (minimal deadzone, hits the hard stop at appropriate time, return to <1% throttle on quick and slow release, reaches 100% throttle position)
  • bend in throttle cable near pedal isn’t damaging 
  • check pressing brakes changes brake pressure 

Other:

  • check engine bolts
