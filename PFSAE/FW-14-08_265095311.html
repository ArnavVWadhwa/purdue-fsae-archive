Purdue Formula SAE : FW-14-08

Created by Zhang, Chenkai, last modified on Jun 28, 2023

Simulation: CZ-037

Changes/Justifications

Description of the Change

Picture

Justification

Reduced cascade angle of attack globally

Fix flow separation across these and hopefully fix the junction losses.
New strake

Added the third small strake, which I call inner inner strake so I don't have to change the naming convention. This strake will provide some pressurization on the onboard side to fight against inner strake separation across the slot gap. Yes, you will be separation across this strake now, but this strake is short enough to make the separation into a vortex, hopefully, that's the idea.

CFD Results Analysis

CFD Picture

Description

No more separation (bursting is still there, but that's to be expected), but suction improvement is pretty small. Too small to be worth it in terms of UT performance trade off with having a strake this far inboard.
