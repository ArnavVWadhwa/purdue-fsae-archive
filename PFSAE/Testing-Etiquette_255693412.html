Purdue Formula SAE : Testing Etiquette

Created by Panchal, Aarnav Nilesh, last modified by Paradise, Hunter on May 24, 2023

Testing General Rules:

  1. Safety
    1. Fire Extinguishers
      1. Have a person with an extinguisher at all times around the car
      2. Do not leave extinguishers on track, keep it with you
    2. Radios
      1. Radio Etiquette
      2. Dont be on track/corner working without a radio
    3. Stay off the track especially when the car is running
    4. Be urgent and stay on track for the least time possible to avoid chances of being hit
  2. Downtime
    1. Stay out of people's way during work
  3. Trailer
    1. Remove items from desk area
    2. Keep tools on car side of trailer
    3. Keep out zone next to trailer for drivers to pull in
  4. If you are assigned work, do the work!
    1. Need to give advance notice of missing a session if confirmed to come
  5. Be careful around the car
    1. Lifting, pushing, helping with maintenance
    2. Coordinate setting down the car
  6. DO NOT TOUCH THE CAR WITHOUT TEAM LEAD PERMISSION
  7. Diligence on Pre-Flights
    1. Check the car whenever it comes in
