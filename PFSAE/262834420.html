Project Scope

  • Rules compliant
  • Ensure driver safety (operate well within and beyond (how much?)) vehicle capabilities 
  • Stiff
  • Lightweight
  • Easy to manufacture 
  • Cheap
  • Serviceability
  • Stakeholders & Subsystem boundaries
    • Powertrain: 
      • Engine mounting
      • Component clearance
      • Operating & crash loads
      • Intake component boundary
    • Suspension:
      • Interface of control arm, rocker and damper points
      • Weight distribution, cg and torsional stiffness targets
    • Chassis
      • Steering:
        • Packaging and placement of steering wheel height
      • Pedalbox:
        • Pedal box mounting
      • Pneumatic:
        • Component and tank packaging
    • Aerodynamics:
      • Engine location interfaces with UT design
      • Chassis ground clearance affects UT Design and mounting

Design Requirements

Known:

  • Rules
    • All relevant rules
  • Safety
    • Pass all operating load cases
      • Max Cornering 
      • Max Launch
      • Max Bump - Front, Rear, Left, Right
    • Pass all crash load cases
      • Front Impact
      • Rollover
  • Performance
    • Meets torsional rigidity target of...
