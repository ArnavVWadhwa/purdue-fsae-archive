Purdue Formula SAE : CAD Sanity Checks | CHA

Created by Dien Quang Nguyen, last modified on Jun 20, 2023

Make a comprehensive and extensive list that acts as the standard for CAD and design in general. All Chassis system owners must refer to this checklist during the design and CAD process.

Open for anyone to add additional review items based on their prior experience 


  • Used hole tool?
  • Assigned material properties?
    • No duplicate bodies? - Check usage of wave-link geometry and layout files 
  • No interfering/intersecting bodies?
  • Sufficient clearance?
  • Manufacturing considerations?
  • Assembly considerations?
  • Disassembly considerations?
