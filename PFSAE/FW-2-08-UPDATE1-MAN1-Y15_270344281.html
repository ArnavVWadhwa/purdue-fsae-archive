Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Added sheet that connects each strake together.

A potential route for manufacturing this strake orientation.
Added connector flap between the turning vanes.

Should not impact much, just trying to improve structural integrity.

CFD Analysis

FW loss is due to strake separation causing FW stall. The space between the strake and the lower surface of the sheet is too small, which results in massive BL interaction causing BL growth which leads to a stall.

On the windward side, the flow detaches due to the lack of vorticity in the previous channels due to the lack of height for the strakes.


Note: no noticeable difference in vortex strength downstream with the connector flap, this should be fine.
