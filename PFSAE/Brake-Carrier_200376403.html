At the moment, the plan is to use the ME Laser cutter as this part is a flat profile. Will update later when complete and we know how it went.

Stock Prep

Front and rear carriers are different thicknesses, make sure stock is sorted accordingly

High Level Steps

Laser cutting

Take all that needs to be laser cut to John, preferably all in one go to not annoy him with a million little things

When laser cutting, make sure to undersize holes. First round of cutting did not go well due to small walls at certain points, and holes that did not account for laser cutting tolerances.

Either completely remove hole for the trigger wheel, or double the wall thickness because that was the main point of failure for the first run
