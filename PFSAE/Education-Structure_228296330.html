Purdue Formula SAE : Education Structure

Created by Unknown User (xia107), last modified by Paradise, Hunter on May 07, 2023

Goal: Educate new members about fundamentals to get involved with the team.

Philosophy: I think we need to make these strongly encouraged. These lectures should not be recorded. I think it needs to be emphasized that to be a contributing member of the team, new members should have the attitude that FSAE takes priority in their schedule. Also, none of these topics are extremely novel, therefore nothing they miss cannot be researched independently.
