Design:

  • Consider the 2022 cooling sim but don't trust the sim's completely. Be cautious and think about what is reasonable.
  • Make the system with the COTS global library you should be developing 
  • Use all straight 0.035in wall aluminum tubing from mcmaster or equivalent and use the bead roller 
  • the mounting we used in PF22 was the best i've ever seen, so do something similar / maybe easier to manufacture.
