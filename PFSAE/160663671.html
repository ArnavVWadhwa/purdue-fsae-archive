Purdue Formula SAE : Front Control Arm, Pushrod, and Rockers

Created by Unknown User (xia107), last modified by Klaveano, Alexander Ryan on Nov 23, 2020

Design Criteria and Constraints

  • Control Arms and attachment points must sustain maximum loads plus a safety factor 
  • Must link Suspension to Chassis at the designated suspension points
  • Must be entirely parametric in the CAD models, so that updating for changes to sus points, for example, is easy
  • Reduce weight from PF20 if possible
  • Work with Uprights to develop appropriate mounting geometry

Material Selection

  • Final Material Choice: Steel
  • The ability to be welded makes design and assembly much simpler, less room for failure, without sacrificing much in the way of strength.
