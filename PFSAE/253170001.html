Created by Unknown User (agarw162), last modified on Mar 17, 2023

General Notes:

Meet at shop at 7am EST

Rules Brief:

  1. The Grissom Gate must be locked with no exceptions, this is a condition with which the site was given to us for the weekend
  2. The key to the gate must be with Aarnav, if it is given to you to open and close the gate it needs to make its way back to him ASAP
  3. You are responsible for your own safety, be safe around the car to avoid accidents, and follow instructions from Aarnav/TLs.
  4. Keep the site clean, this is a sponsorship and we must respect the fact that they are giving us this opportunity.
  5. If there is ANYTHING you are confused about don't hesitate to ask Aarnav, there are no stupid questions when it comes to Grissom policies.
  6. Waivers must be signed, will be done on Aarnav's IPad day of.

Car Rules:

  1. Whenever the car is on there needs to be someone equipped with a fire extinguisher, we will often have multiple people
  2. When the car is running no other vehicles may be driving around the test site unless they're on the side roads
  3. There may be people asked to be corner workers, stay out of the path of the car (don't stand in front of the car's possible trajectories in case control is lost)
  4. You may have a radio, but keep off the radio unless you have to say something important. They are a safety tool.


Notes:

Grissom has no restrooms, only a BP gas station that is a 2-minute ride away has them. If you need to use the restroom there will be trips that are taken to the gas station. The gas station also has a Subway we get lunch at. If it is an emergency we will get someone to take you. It's also usually cold, you will be outside for maybe 8 hours at a time so dress warm, also bring water and snacks as needed.

Goals:

  • Unload Car and team-leads pre-flight car again 
  • Setup initial courses - Matt Leading
    • Accel Length

  • Launch Control Testing
    • If all goes well, test out autoshifting and traction control
  • Skidpad and Slalom 
  • Setup AutoX Course and get couple AutoX runs towards end of day for camber adjustment, brake bias adjustment

Preflight:

General:

  • Clean Debris off Track / Check Track

Suspension:

  • Check brake fluid leaks
  • Tire PSI to setup sheet
  • Torque stripe check
  • Compliance check
  • Brake pad wear check
  • Tire wear indicators
  • Check shockpot mounting
  • Verify hall sensor distance 


Powertrain:

Intake

  • Intake / throttle mounted securely
  • Torque stripe check 

Exhaust

  • Exhaust / muffler mounted securely
  • Verify powertrain and non-powertrain components are safe from exhaust heat 

Fuel

  • Fuel system mounted securely, no leaks 
  • Torque stripe check 

Cooling

  • Pressure Test Cooling System to 15PSI and check for leaks, mounted securely
  • Check fan mounting
  • Check cooling lines, make sure they're mounted securely
  • Torque stripe check

Oil

  • Verify Oil System is filled and no visible leaks
  • Check oil tank mounting
  • Torque stripe check 

Drivetrain

  • Drivetrain mounted correctly, differential filled, CV joints greased up 
  • Drivetrain chain appropriate slack and appropriately lubed (may require tensioning)
  • Torque stripe check 

Engine

  • Cycle Lock

SIQ:

  • Crank car in shop, no fuel injector
  • Fire car outside
  • Check sensors
  • Check harness mounting, zip tie up loose wire
  • Check throttle position sensor
  • Check wheel speed sensors 

Aero:

  • Check seat connection
  • Check all body panel rivets
  • Check nosecone mounting
  • Check all bolts on endplates
  • Check swan and tierod bolts 
  • Check all jam nuts
  • Re-tape any sharp edges

Chassis:

Shifter:

  • Pneumatic Shifter
    • Tank is securely mounted 
    • All solenoids, hoses, are mounted and routed securely 
    • check all bolted connections 
      • connection to shifter itself isn't to tight to cause binding 
    • shift up & down and clutch in 
  • Mechanical Shifter
    • check all shifter bolts
    • check push pull cable and clutch cable washers
    • shift up & down to check hard stops/ push pull cable
    • check clutch cable tension

Steering:

  • check U-Joint for wear and binding due to forced constraint 
  • check steering rack pins (make sure it's not walking out)
  • check clevis bolts (make sure safety wire is still loose)
  • check rack mount bolts

Pedal Box:

  • check all bolts
  • check throttle pedal (minimal deadzone, hits the hard stop at appropriate time, return to <1% throttle on quick and slow release, reaches 100% throttle position)
  • bend in throttle cable near pedal isn’t damaging 
  • check pressing brakes changes brake pressure 
  • check fluid reservoir mounting 
  • check rail compliance and signs of crack propagation 

Other:

  • check engine bolts

  • Check diff bolts

  • Check pedal box
