Purdue Formula SAE : UT-12-05

Created by Zhang, Chenkai, last modified on Jun 08, 2023

Simulation: CZ-018

Changes/Justifications

Description of the Change

Picture

Justification

Sidepod leading edge flow alignmemnt

Increased local geometric outwash again to improve flow alignment
Added UT outwashing winglet

Outwashing winglet to power down the bulk flow rotation behind the tires. Although the bulk rotation itself might just be CFD making things up about tire wake, the benefits of the outwash is there regardless of tire wake geometry.

The vortex shed off the top is locally outwash at floor edge, which is again good. However, the vortex itself is going to be increase rotation rate of tire wake, but I don't think that is a concern since the outwash itself should overpower what the vortex does.

Another worry is side wing flap performance. The reduction of tire wake rotation and the local outwash will make flow more prone to separation along the inwashing portion of the sidepods.
