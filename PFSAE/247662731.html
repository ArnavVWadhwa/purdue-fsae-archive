Purdue Formula SAE : Carbon Tube Testing (Suspension)

Created by MacNab, Zachary Robert, last modified on Mar 04, 2023


Why: we want to conduct this testing in order to gain more knowledge on our carbon properties, specifically in tension, torsion, and a little bit of bending at the bond. The end goal for this is to develop a fully carbon suspension package and to convert to pull rods in the front for decoupled suspension. I think it would be a great "introduction" to prep the team for converting to carbon wheel shells as well, which is a huge challenge and undertaking.


Carbon Testing Planning

Things we would like to vary:

  • Loading scenarios (ie.: Tensile ultimate with and without torsion applied, cyclic loading, etc.)
  • Type of epoxy 
  • Type of carbon fiber
  • Temperature
  • Type of aluminum insert
  • Preparation of aluminum insert
