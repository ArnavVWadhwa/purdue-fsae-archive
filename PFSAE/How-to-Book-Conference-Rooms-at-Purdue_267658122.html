Purdue Formula SAE : How to Book Conference Rooms at Purdue

Created by MacNab, Zachary Robert, last modified by Rumsey, Helen Nicole on Sep 07, 2023

POC: MacNab, Zachary Robert 

This confluence page serves as a guide on how to book conference rooms at Purdue. 


Purdue Libraries - https://calendar.lib.purdue.edu

Link should be relatively self explanatory. Best rooms are on here as of writing this are WALC 3rd floors rooms. They have a TV and adequate seating for a small - medium sized meeting. 

Through Outlook Directly 

Windows Tutorial

  • https://engineering.purdue.edu/ECN/Support/KB/Docs/How%20to%20reserve%20room
  • DUDL 1119 & 2119 are some of our go-tos

Mac Tutorial 

Make sure you are logged into outlook. 

  1. go to make a new event 
  2. click scheduling assistant at the top bar 
  3. hit add location on the left side
    2. click browse with room finder
  4. search up your desired building on the right column
  5. When you send you invite, you should get a confirmation email if your conference room has accepted your invite or not.
