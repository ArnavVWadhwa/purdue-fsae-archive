Purdue Formula SAE : 7. Selecting Fuel Injector(s) (Sizing Calculator)

Created by Unknown User (dnocon), last modified on May 02, 2022

This is the last step before you are ready to design and implement the peripherals to the engine!!

Choosing the right fuel injector is extremely important for engine calibration. 

You want to be out of the non-linear range (because the injector calibration is not guaranteed to be good), but you also want to stay below about 70% duty cycle for good atomization. 

A fuel injector calculator was developed by Dom in Fall of 2020 in MATLAB but has since evolved into the most recent version (fall 2021) in excel.

Newest version is linked below. 

Follow the directions in green on the workbook. Sources and Update Log are also included within the workbook.
