Purdue Formula SAE : Low Side Injector Output

Created by Paradise, Hunter on Nov 24, 2023
  • The Mnemonic within Build and Tune is INJ_LS.
  • 2 Amp open collector saturated drive (switch to BAT_NEG) suitable for high impedance injectors (> 12
    Ohms).

  • No internal recirculation circuitry. Cam solenoids require an external recirculation diode.
  • Can also be used as a PWM aux output.
  • 24bit timers 0.1usec resolution.
  • Max voltage 60V.
  • Max current 3.5 Amps minimum.
  • RMS current 2 Amps.
  • 2 Low Side Injector pins on the M130.
  • Suitable for permanently powered loads.
