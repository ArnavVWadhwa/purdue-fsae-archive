Purdue Formula SAE : Oil DCR

Created by Horton, Tyler Jason, last modified on Jul 12, 2023

Project Scope

  • Problem Statement:
    • Provide ample, cool, and air free oil to the engine to protect internal components from wear
  • System Boundaries:
    • Cooling: Extra heat from oil being added to water
    • Chassis: Packaging components
    • Suspension: Packing components
    • SIQ: Pressure and temperature sensorsÂ 

Design Requirements

  • Supply ample oil to keep engine internals safe during operation
  • Keep oil temperatures low enough to meet the engines specifications for oiling
  • Remove as much air as possible from the oil to increase flow and protect against component wear

Design Overview

  • Dry sump oiling system
    • Dailey Engineering SP2 Pump
    • Custom aluminum tank with baffles
    • Custom aluminum oil pan
    • Use the oil cooler that comes with the engine
    • -10AN and -12AN lines
    • Custom Catch Can
    • Filters in AN lines

Cost

  • <$1500 is the goal

Schedule

  • Follow team schedule and have everything ready one week before 1st run

Analysis

  • i2 data to study oil pressure during driving events
  • i2 data to analyze oil temps during driving events
