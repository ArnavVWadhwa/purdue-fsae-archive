Purdue Formula SAE : Dimensions (SD)

Created by Unknown User (mgal), last modified on Jun 12, 2022

Firstly, I wanted to reiterate that the current CAD dimensions arent meaningful, most things were just placed around the driver. However, we have to design to the 5 th through 95 th percentile human and design to a bunch of rules. The next step is to finalize the rule planes and all the components' dimensions and positioning. Luckily the model is fully parametric and updating dimensions is very easy (Just make sure every part is fully loaded).

The purpose of this page is to display all the driving dimensions and planes/rules that drive the ergo stand. Feel free to play with any of these dimensions when the model is fully loaded, and make sure not to save it until you choose the final dimensions.
