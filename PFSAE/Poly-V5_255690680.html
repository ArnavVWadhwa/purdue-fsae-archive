Purdue Formula SAE : Poly V5

Created by Zhang, Chenkai, last modified on Apr 12, 2023

Major Changes:

  • Target first cell height: 0.019 mm
  • Decreased aero prism layer growth rate to 1.2
  • Increased number of front wing prism layer to 24
  • Increased number of rear wing prism layer to 25
  • Increased number of UT/SW prism layer to 28
  • Increased UT/SW prism layer thickness to 14 mm
