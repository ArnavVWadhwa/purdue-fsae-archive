Save this anywhere on your computer, then double click. From there, this template should show up when you open i2 Standard.


Note: this includes strain gauge calculations, but do not assume it applies to all strain gauges. Must know values of resistors used in circuit at hand as well as the strain gauge factor! Once you have the strain, the same goes for loads. They are currently based on .625 OD, .049 wall tube.
