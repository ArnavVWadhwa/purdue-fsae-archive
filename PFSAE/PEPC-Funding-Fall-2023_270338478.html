Purdue Formula SAE : PEPC Funding Fall 2023

Created by Rumsey, Helen Nicole, last modified on Dec 04, 2023

Description of what funding will be going towards:

The funding will be used for engine modifications for our 2024 vehicle. In previous years, we have purchased used engines, but in order to be competitive and efficient at competition we need to modify stock engines. The items on the list include services for boring out the cylinder, replacement oil pump, new pistons and rods, and tools for rebuilding our competition engine. 


What does the organization hope to achieve with this funding?

Not only will the funding will enable us to be more competitive, efficient, and powerful at competition, it's the next step in member development on our team. It will serve as a hands-on learning opportunity for internal engine development not seen anywhere else on campus.  It will lead to a better engine model and additional tuning improvement. In order to compete in 2024, we need to rebuild our current engine, this funding would allow us to improve the engine and teach our members in the process.
