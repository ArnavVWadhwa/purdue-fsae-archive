Purdue Formula SAE : FW-1-13

Created by Matthew James Lamear, last modified on Jul 29, 2023

Simulation ML-032

Changes/Justifications

Description of the ChangePictureJustification
Cut the outer strake, and reduced inlet channel widths.

The cut and the reduction in inlet channel width changes are done to place the vortex in the 2nd channel higher resulting in more inwash for the final channel to prevent vortex bursting.
Smoothed overall sweep from inner to mid-span.

This could result in more dirty air in the inner portion but may produce less drag in the mid-section due to a more gradual sweep. 
3rd element AoA decreased, 4th element AoA increased in outer section.

To improve RW performance marginally, the third element AoA was decreased to reduce wake. 4th element increase in AoA to compensate for 3rd element decrease.

CFD Analysis

CFD PictureDescription

Vortex bursts earlier, but due to the inner strake containing a cut, the middle vortex forms higher, resulting in the vortex surviving longer.

This results in substantial outwash in the inner section, just need to try to contain that outer vortex and this design should come back to life.
