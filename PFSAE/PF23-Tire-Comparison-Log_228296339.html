How we will determine which tire is best (Process):

Ben's Theory:

  1. FY vs SA is most important when considering performance
    1. Each curve is for a given FZ and IA
    2. Slope (Cornering Stiffness) determines transient response on turn in and how much steering input is needed to maintain Ay below maximum
      1. A greater yaw acceleration means the vehicle will turn in faster because of a higher cornering stiffness
    3. Needing higher slip angles to reach peak FY is bad because it increases drag and a large component of the lateral force becomes longitudinal 
    4. Higher slip angles require more energy and will heat tires faster/more causing increased tire wear
    5. Max Fy is good for comparison between tire data but be skeptical of the value as the tire tests occur on ideal surfaces with higher friction
    6. Evaluate transitional region to determine driver feeling characteristics near max acceleration (drop off will cause snap oversteer moments)
