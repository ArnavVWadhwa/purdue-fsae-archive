Just a note, which probably isn't super critical at the moment, but specifically for the KTM 450 and Yamaha 450 there are at least 2 different models of each 450 from both manufacturers. I would expect ballpark estimates to be similar but a Yamaha YFZ 450 (motorcross race bike) engine is going to be built differently and have slightly different features compared to a Yamaha WR450 (a crosscountry or enduro bike). I would guess that a YFZ450 is going to rev higher, make more power, and maybe lighter? while a WR450 might make less power, maybe be a little more reliable, slightly heavier, but have a larger stator and make more electrical power.
